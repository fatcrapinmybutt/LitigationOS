### Verification Tiers
1. **VERIFIED**: Found in `research_authorities` (verified=1), `citation_validation` (is_valid=1), `citation_index` (is_valid=1), or `mcl_authority_library`/`mcr_authority_library`

3. **UNKNOWN**: Not found in any of 14 queried DB tables
4. **FABRICATED**: Matches known fabrication list (McCraney v Ford Motor Co, MCL 600.2145, Jane Berry, Patricia Berry)

### Fuzzy Matching
- Case citations: party name substring matching against verified/known sets
- MCL/MCR: base statute/rule number matching (ignoring subsections)
- USC: normalized form matching with variant formats
