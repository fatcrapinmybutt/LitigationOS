# CORTEX — Legal & Compliance Intelligence Starter Pack

> **Free domain pack** for the [CORTEX Intelligence Platform](https://fatcrapinmybutt.github.io/cortex-site/)

## What Is This?

A JSON domain pack that turns CORTEX into a **legal research and compliance intelligence tool**. Point it at your local files and get interactive D3.js knowledge graphs showing:

- **Contract clauses** — indemnification, force majeure, non-compete, arbitration
- **Legal citations** — U.S. Code, CFR, case law, public law references
- **Regulations** — GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA, SOX, Dodd-Frank, FCPA, PCI-DSS
- **Courts & parties** — courts, plaintiffs, defendants, legal actions
- **Monetary amounts** — dollar figures in contracts, settlements, judgments

## Quick Start

1. Download [CORTEX](https://fatcrapinmybutt.github.io/cortex-site/) (free desktop app)
2. Drop `legal_starter.json` into the `domains/` folder
3. Run: `cortex.exe scan --pack legal_starter --dir /path/to/legal/files`
4. Open the interactive graph in your browser
