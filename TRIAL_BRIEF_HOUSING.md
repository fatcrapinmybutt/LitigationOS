## III. LEGAL FRAMEWORK

### A. MCL 554.139 — Covenant of Habitability
Every landlord covenants that premises are fit for their intended use and will be maintained in reasonable repair. This covenant CANNOT be waived.

### B. MCL 445.901 et seq. — Michigan Consumer Protection Act
Prohibits unfair, unconscionable, or deceptive practices in trade or commerce. Residential leasing is "trade or commerce." Violations subject to **treble damages** (MCL 445.911(2)).

### C. MCL 125.534-540 — Housing Law of Michigan
Establishes minimum standards for housing. Violations enforceable by tenants.

### D. Common Law
- Breach of contract (lease terms)
- Negligence (failure to maintain)
- Fraud/misrepresentation (concealing conditions)
- Constructive eviction (if conditions forced departure)
