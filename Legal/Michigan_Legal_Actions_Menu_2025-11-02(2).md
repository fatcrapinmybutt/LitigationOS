## 2) Personal Protection Orders (PPO)

**Rules & Statutes**
- **Court Rules:** MCR 3.705–3.707 (PPO procedure, modification/termination, hearings). Start here: https://www.courts.michigan.gov/siteassets/rules-instructions-administrative-orders/michigan-court-rules/court-rules-book-ch-3-responsive-html5.zip/index.html
- **Statutes:** MCL 600.2950 / 600.2950a (domestic/non‑domestic PPO). Example statute hub: https://www.legislature.mi.gov/Laws/MCL?objectName=mcl-600-2950

**Key Actions**
- Petition for PPO / Motion to Modify/Terminate PPO (MCR 3.707).
- Motion to Dissolve/Terminate Ex Parte PPO; demand evidentiary hearing with live testimony.
- Motion for Reconsideration (MCR 2.119(F)) if denied relief.
- Appeal or **Application for Leave** to COA (see Appellate section).

**Official Forms**
- SCAO PPO Forms index: https://www.courts.michigan.gov/SCAO-forms/PPO/

## 4) Appellate Pathways (COA & MSC)

**Court of Appeals (COA)**
- **Jurisdiction / Modes:** MCR 7.202–7.206; rule hub: https://www.courts.michigan.gov/siteassets/rules-instructions-administrative-orders/michigan-court-rules/court-rules-book-ch-7-responsive-html5.zip/index.html
- **Appeal of Right / By Leave:** MCR 7.203 & **MCR 7.205** (applications by leave). Time to appeal generally **21 days** (MCR 7.204(A)(1)(a)); beware tolling by timely post‑judgment motions (see practice note PDF: https://www.dickinson-wright.com/-/media/files/news/2022/12/article--effect-of-postjudgment-motions-on-time-to.pdf)
- **Original Actions (Extraordinary Writs):** **MCR 7.206** (superintending control, mandamus, prohibition, habeas). Rule PDF: https://www.courts.michigan.gov/siteassets/rules-instructions-administrative-orders/michigan-court-rules/court-rules-book-ch-7-responsive-html5.zip/index.html#Rule_7.206
- **Stays & Bonds Pending Appeal:** **MCR 7.208–7.209**; note the **May 21, 2025 amendment of MCR 7.209** (appellate courts can **sua sponte** order a stay). Order: https://www.courts.michigan.gov/siteassets/rules-instructions-administrative-orders/proposed-and-recently-adopted-orders-on-admin-matters/adopted-orders/2023-33_2025-05-21_formor_amdmcr7.209.pdf

**Michigan Supreme Court (MSC)**
- **Jurisdiction:** **MCR 7.303**; Applications for Leave: **MCR 7.305**; MSC processing guide: https://www.courts.michigan.gov/4af555/siteassets/forms/msc/other-msc-forms-and-guides/msc-case-processing.pdf

**Superintending Control at Trial Level**
- Circuit courts have superintending control over district courts (**MCR 3.302**). Rule index: https://www.courts.michigan.gov/siteassets/rules-instructions-administrative-orders/michigan-court-rules/court-rules-book-ch-3-responsive-html5.zip/index.html#Rule_3.302

**Stay Practice Quick Hits**
- Trial court usually addresses stay/bond first (**MCR 7.209(A)(2)**); trial court retains authority over stay/bond (**MCR 7.208(G)**). Benchbook snippet: https://www.courts.michigan.gov/4909e0/siteassets/publications/benchbooks/civil/civilresponsivehtml5.zip/Civil/Ch_2_Jurisdiction%2C_Venue%2C_and_Standing/Trial_Courts_Authority_After_Appeal-pz1v.htm

## 5) FOIA (to compel records for litigation)

- **Response deadline:** 5 business days; one 10‑day extension allowed (**MCL 15.235**). Statute: https://www.legislature.mi.gov/Laws/MCL?objectName=MCL-15-235
- **Remedies:** Civil action in circuit court; attorney fees/damages available for prevailing requesters (**MCL 15.240**). Statute: https://www.legislature.mi.gov/Laws/MCL?objectName=mcl-15-240
- **AG FOIA Handbook (Jan 2023):** https://www.michigan.gov/ag/-/media/Project/Websites/AG/FOIA/FOIA-Handbook-January-2023.pdf

## 6) Quick “Best‑Action” Map (Tailored to custody/PPO posture)

1. **Demand prompt evidentiary hearing** on any ex parte restraints affecting parenting time (**MCR 3.207 / 3.210**) and seek interim **TRO** restoring contact (**MCR 3.310**).
2. **File/renew Motion to Modify Parenting Time** (FOC 65 + **MC 416**) citing **MCL 722.27a**; attach AppClose logs and police‑report timeline as exhibits.
3. If local relief is blocked or repeatedly continued **without findings**, prepare **COA Application for Leave** and/or **Original Action for Superintending Control** (**MCR 7.205/7.206**) focused on due‑process violations and failure to hold required hearings.
4. Concurrently, file **Motion to Disqualify** if bias grounds exist (**MCR 2.003**) and preserve **Reconsideration** (**MCR 2.119(F)**) to toll appeal deadlines.
5. For housing: preserve and file under **MCR 4.201** with proper SCAO forms; leverage habitability/EGLE issues as defenses where supported.
6. Enforce record access with **FOIA litigation** if agencies or courts withhold files (**MCL 15.235/.240**).
