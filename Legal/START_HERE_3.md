# START_HERE — CAPSTONE_PORTFOLIO_STACK_v2026-01-24.1

## Goal: desktop icon runs everything
You download the ZIP to Downloads. You keep ONE launcher on your Desktop.
You double-click the Desktop launcher. It copies, extracts, and starts Studio.

### One-time setup
1) Download `CAPSTONE_DESKTOP_ONECLICK.cmd`
2) Move it to `C:\Users\andre\Desktop\`
3) Optional: right-click -> Create shortcut, then rename shortcut to "Capstone Studio"

### Every time
1) Download the pack ZIP. It lands in:
   `C:\Users\andre\Downloads\CAPSTONE_PORTFOLIO_STACK_*.zip`
2) Double-click the Desktop launcher:
   `CAPSTONE_DESKTOP_ONECLICK.cmd`

### Where it installs
`F:\CAPSTONE\Packs\<ZIPBASE>\`
Example:
`F:\CAPSTONE\Packs\CAPSTONE_PORTFOLIO_STACK_v2026-01-24.1\scripts\00_LAUNCH_STUDIO.cmd`

### If anything fails
Open:
`F:\CAPSTONE\Packs\desktop_bootstrap.log`


## Desktop one-click
1) Download the pack ZIP.
2) Open the ZIP and drag `CAPSTONE_DESKTOP_ONECLICK.cmd` to your Desktop.
3) Double-click it. It will install/extract into `F:\CAPSTONE\Packs\` and start Studio.
