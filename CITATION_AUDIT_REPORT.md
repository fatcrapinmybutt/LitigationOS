## METHODOLOGY

1. **Extraction:** Regex matching `Case v Case, Vol Reporter Page (Year)` and variants
2. **Deduplication:** Normalized by case name + reporter + year
3. **Verification:** Cross-referenced against 14 known-good citations
4. **Hallucination screen:** Checked for McCraney v Ford Motor Co (known fabrication)
5. **Misapplication screen:** Checked MCL 600.2145 for right-to-counsel misuse
6. **Variant detection:** Grouped by normalized case name to find reporter inconsistencies

### Limitations
- Regex may miss non-standard citation formats or citations split across lines
- 'UNVERIFIED' = not in our 14-citation verified list, NOT necessarily wrong
- Statute context analysis limited to known misapplications
- Some citation formatting in markdown (italics, bold) may affect extraction
