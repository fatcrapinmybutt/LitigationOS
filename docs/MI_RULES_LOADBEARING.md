## MCR 2.119(C) motion timing defaults
- Motion + notice of hearing + supporting brief/affidavits: serve
  - 9 days before hearing if served by first-class mail
  - 7 days before hearing if served by delivery/electronic service (as defined by rule)
- Responses: serve
  - 5 days before hearing if served by first-class mail
  - 3 days before hearing if served by delivery/electronic service
- Filing: motion filed at least 7 days before hearing; response filed at least 3 days before hearing unless court orders otherwise.

Source: Michigan Courts – MCR Chapter 2 (MCR 2.119).

## MCR 1.109(D) filing standards (format)
- Legible, English language; 8.5×11
- Font size 12–13 point body; ≥10 point footnotes; exception for SCAO-approved forms.
- Caption must include court name, parties/title, case number, and document identification.

Source: Michigan Courts – MCR Chapter 1 (MCR 1.109).

## MiFILE Electronic Document Standards (Policies & Standards No. 2) – e-filing constraints
- PDF preferred text-searchable; direct-to-PDF where possible; OCR recommended for scans.
- 25MB per file limit (split into parts with labels when needed).
- Attachments: Index to Attachments on last page of lead doc; attachments identified/numbered and referenced by file name.

Source: Michigan Courts – MiFILE Electronic Document Standards PDF (rev. 7/17/2020).
