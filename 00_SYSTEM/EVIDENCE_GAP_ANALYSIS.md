## 📋 EVIDENCE ACQUISITION METHODS

| Method | Use Case | Timeline | Cost |
|--------|----------|----------|------|
| **FOIA Request** | Government records, inspection reports, police reports | 5-30 days | Free-$50 |
| **Subpoena** | Third-party records, phone/text records, business records | Post-filing | Filing fees |
| **Deposition** | Witness testimony under oath | Post-filing | $500-2000/day |
| **Declaration/Affidavit** | First-person accounts, expert opinions | 1-7 days | Free-$200 |
| **Court Records** | Orders, docket entries, transcripts | 1-5 days | $10-50/page |
| **Photography/Video** | Physical conditions, property damage | Immediate | Free |
| **Expert Report** | Professional opinions on damages, conditions | 2-8 weeks | $1000-5000 |
| **Public Records** | Corporate filings, property records, vital records | 1-10 days | Free-$25 |
