## Filing Steps

1. Go to https://mifile.courts.michigan.gov
2. Log in (create account if needed)
3. Select court and case
4. Upload documents in order listed above
5. Pay filing fee ($0) or attach fee waiver
6. Submit and save confirmation number
7. Serve all parties per proof of service
