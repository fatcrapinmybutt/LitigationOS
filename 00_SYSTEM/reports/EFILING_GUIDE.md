## PACER / CM-ECF (Federal Court)
**URL:** https://pacer.uscourts.gov
**Used For:** F4
**Cost:** $405 filing fee (IFP waives)

### Registration:
1. Go to pacer.uscourts.gov
2. Click "Register for an Account"
3. Select "Register for a PACER Account" first
4. Complete registration form
5. Then register for CM-ECF at USDC Western District of Michigan
6. Go to ecf.miwd.uscourts.gov
7. Complete attorney/pro se registration (may require in-person verification)
8. NOTE: Federal pro se e-filing may require court permission

### Filing Steps:
1. Log in to CM-ECF for USDC WDMI
2. Click "Civil" → "Complaints/New Cases"
3. Enter party information (all defendants)
4. Upload complaint PDF
5. Upload civil cover sheet (JS 44)
6. Upload IFP application if applicable
7. Pay filing fee ($405) or submit IFP
8. Serve defendants per FRCP 4 (US Marshal for IFP plaintiffs)

### Tips:
- 💡 Federal court is stricter on formatting (see local rules)
- 💡 Margins: 1 inch all sides; font: 12pt Times New Roman
- 💡 Page numbers required on every page
- 💡 Certificate of service must be on separate page
- 💡 File the IFP application WITH the complaint — not after

## Judicial Tenure Commission
**URL:** https://jtc.courts.michigan.gov
**Used For:** F6
**Cost:** FREE

### Registration:
1. NO registration required — anyone can file a complaint

### Filing Steps:
1. Download complaint form from jtc.courts.michigan.gov
2. OR write a letter-format complaint
3. Include: Your name, judge name, case number, specific conduct
4. Attach supporting documentation (keep under 50 pages)
5. Mail to: Judicial Tenure Commission, 3034 W Grand Blvd Ste 8-450, Detroit, MI 48202
6. OR email: jtc@courts.mi.gov (confirm current email)
7. Keep copy of everything you send
8. JTC will acknowledge receipt within 30 days

### Tips:
- 💡 Be specific and factual — cite dates, case numbers, exact conduct
- 💡 Do NOT use emotional language — stick to facts
- 💡 Reference specific Canon violations (see Tool #103)
- 💡 JTC investigations are confidential — they won't tell you the status
- 💡 Filing is FREE — no cost whatsoever

## Court of Appeals (via MiFILE)
**URL:** https://mifile.courts.michigan.gov
**Used For:** F8, F9
**Cost:** $375 application fee (IFP waives)

### Registration:
1. Same MiFILE account as circuit court
2. Add COA case: 366810
3. Select "Court of Appeals" as the court

### Filing Steps:
1. Log in to MiFILE
2. Select Court of Appeals case (366810)
3. For F8 (Leave): Upload application for leave to appeal
4. For F9 (Brief): Upload appellant brief (50 page limit)
5. Include: Table of contents, index of authorities, statement of questions
6. Upload appendix as separate document
7. File proof of service on opposing party
8. Pay fee ($375 for leave, $0 for brief if leave granted) or IFP

### Tips:
- 💡 CALL CLERK FIRST: (517) 373-0786 to confirm deadlines for 366810
- 💡 Brief must cite to lower court record (page numbers)
- 💡 Follow MCR 7.212 formatting requirements exactly
- 💡 Binding: left margin stapled, not spiral bound
- 💡 File 1 original + copies as required by clerk
