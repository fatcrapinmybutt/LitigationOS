# REUNIFICATION PROTOCOL — ANDREW & L.D.W.

*Graduated Reintroduction Based on Attachment Theory + Michigan Guidelines*

**5 phases | 36 steps | Graduated over 6+ months**

## Phase 1: REINTRODUCTION (Week 1-2)
**Goal:** Reestablish father-child bond in safe, structured setting

### Schedule
- **Day 1-3:** 2-hour supervised visits at neutral location (library, park) (Every other day)
- **Day 4-7:** 3-hour visits, child-led play, no pressure (Every other day)
- **Week 2:** 4-hour visits including meal together (3x per week)

### Guidelines
- Let L.D.W. set the pace — no forcing physical contact
- Bring familiar toys/items from before separation
- Keep routine predictable — same time, same location
- No negative talk about Emily — EVER (MCL 722.23(j))
- Document each visit: duration, child's mood, activities

## Phase 2: BUILDING COMFORT (Week 3-4)
**Goal:** Transition to unsupervised visits at Andrew's home

### Schedule
- **Week 3:** Full-day visits (8 hours) at Andrew's home (2x per week)
- **Week 3:** Include one meal preparation together (Each visit)
- **Week 4:** First overnight stay (Friday to Saturday) (1x this week)
- **Week 4:** Continue daytime visits (2x additional)

### Guidelines
- Set up L.D.W.'s room with familiar items
- Establish bedtime routine immediately
- FaceTime/video calls on non-visit days
- Share photos of visits with court/evaluator if required

## Phase 3: STANDARD PARENTING TIME (Week 5-8)
**Goal:** Achieve Michigan standard parenting time schedule

### Schedule
- **Every other weekend:** Friday 6pm to Sunday 6pm (Bi-weekly)
- **Wednesday:** After school/daycare to 8pm (Weekly)
- **Holidays:** Per Michigan FOC holiday schedule (As applicable)

### Guidelines
- This is the MINIMUM standard per Michigan FOC guidelines
- Exchange locations should be neutral (police station, school)
- Maintain communication log for all exchanges
- Begin make-up time calculation — MCL 722.27a(8)

## Phase 4: EXPANDED TIME + MAKE-UP (Month 3-6)
**Goal:** Recover lost parenting time + establish 50/50 schedule

### Schedule
- **Alternating weeks:** Move toward week-on/week-off (Bi-weekly)
- **Make-up time:** Additional overnights per MCL 722.27a(8) (As ordered)
- **Extended holidays:** 1-2 week blocks during school breaks (Per schedule)

### Guidelines
- Make-up time is MANDATORY — court SHALL grant it
- Document all denied or shortened visits for make-up calculation
- Request modification to joint physical custody if going well
- File motion for equal parenting time if Emily resists

## Phase 5: LONG-TERM STABILITY (Month 6+)
**Goal:** Established co-parenting relationship with equal time

### Schedule
- **Equal time:** 50/50 or per agreed schedule (Ongoing)
- **Communication:** Use OurFamilyWizard or similar co-parenting app (Daily as needed)

### Guidelines
- Maintain detailed records of all exchanges and communications
- If alienation continues, file for primary custody
- Continue therapy for L.D.W. if recommended
- Annual review of parenting plan per court order

## Professional Support Team

- **Family therapist:** Facilitate reunification, assess attachment recovery
- **Guardian ad litem (GAL):** Independent assessment of L.D.W.'s best interests — MCL 722.24
- **Parenting coordinator:** Resolve day-to-day co-parenting disputes without court
- **Child psychologist:** Assess developmental impact of separation and recovery trajectory
