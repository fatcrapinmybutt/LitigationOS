## 14th Judicial Circuit Court, Family Division
**Filings:** F1, F2, F3, F7
**System:** MiFILE
**URL:** https://mifile.courts.michigan.gov
**Pro Se Access:** ✅ Yes

### Fees
- **Motion**: $20.00 (MCL 600.2529)
- **Complaint**: $175.00 (civil)
- **Response**: $0 (responses are free)
- **Ifp**: Fee waiver via MC 20 (Affidavit & Order to Waive Fees)

### Format Requirements
- **File Type**: PDF (searchable text, not scanned images)
- **Page Size**: 8.5" x 11" (Letter)
- **Margins**: 1" all sides
- **Font**: 12-point proportional (Times New Roman, Arial)
- **Line Spacing**: Double-spaced (briefs), Single-spaced (block quotes)
- **Page Limit**: 20 pages for briefs (MCR 2.119(A)(2))
- **File Size**: 25 MB per document
- **Naming**: Descriptive (e.g., "Motion_for_Disqualification.pdf")

### Filing Steps
1. Register at mifile.courts.michigan.gov/register (select "Self-Represented Litigant")
2. Verify email and complete profile
3. Select "14th Circuit Court" → "Muskegon County"
4. Select existing case number (2024-001507-DC or 2023-5907-PP)
5. Upload PDF filing as "Lead Document"
6. Upload exhibits as "Attachments" (separate PDFs)
7. Pay filing fee or upload MC 20 fee waiver
8. Submit — system generates timestamp and confirmation
9. Download confirmation receipt for your records
10. System auto-serves all registered parties electronically

### Service of Process
MiFILE handles electronic service to all registered parties. For unregistered parties (Emily Watson, if not on MiFILE), you must serve separately by mail per MCR 2.107.

### ⚠️ Important Notes
- MiFILE is MANDATORY for circuit courts as of 2024
- Pro se filers get full access — same as attorneys
- Emergency motions: File normally, then call clerk at (231) 724-6241
- Ex parte motions: Must include "EX PARTE" in document title
- Fee waiver: File MC 20 first, then file motions at $0

**URL:** https://ecf.miwd.uscourts.gov
**Pro Se Access:** ✅ Yes

### Fees
- **Complaint**: $405.00
- **Ifp**: Fee waiver via 28 U.S.C. § 1915 (in forma pauperis)

### Format Requirements
- **File Type**: PDF (searchable)
- **Page Size**: 8.5" x 11"
- **Margins**: 1" all sides
- **Font**: 12-point, double-spaced
- **Page Limit**: No strict limit for complaints
- **File Size**: 35 MB per document

### Filing Steps
1. Register at pacer.uscourts.gov (create PACER account)
2. Request CM/ECF filing credentials from WDMI clerk
3. Pro se: May need to file initial complaint by MAIL then get e-filing access
4. Mail to: U.S. District Court, 399 Federal Building, 110 Michigan St NW, Grand Rapids, MI 49503
5. Or call clerk: (616) 456-2381 for pro se e-filing procedures
6. Include civil cover sheet (JS-44)
7. Include summons for each defendant
8. Pay $405 fee or file IFP affidavit (28 U.S.C. § 1915)
9. After filing, court issues case number and summons
10. Serve defendants within 90 days (Fed. R. Civ. P. 4(m))

### Service of Process
Must serve each defendant per Fed. R. Civ. P. 4. For Judge McNeill (in official capacity): serve Michigan Attorney General. For Emily Watson: personal service or certified mail.

### ⚠️ Important Notes
- 42 U.S.C. § 1983 complaint — federal question jurisdiction
- No page limit but keep complaint focused (30-40 pages)
- Pro se filers may mail initial complaint if CM/ECF access not yet granted
- IFP: Must complete financial affidavit showing inability to pay
- Service on state officials: serve MI Attorney General per Fed. R. Civ. P. 4(j)(2)
