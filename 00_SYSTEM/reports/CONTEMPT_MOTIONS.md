## CONTEMPT LAW SUMMARY

**Civil Contempt (MCR 3.606):** Designed to compel compliance with court orders.
The contemnor (violator) can purge by complying. No jail unless refusal to comply.

**Three Elements Required:**
1. ✅ Clear and definite court order existed
2. ✅ Contemnor had knowledge of the order
3. ✅ Contemnor willfully failed to comply

**Burden:** Clear and convincing evidence (preponderance in some circuits)
