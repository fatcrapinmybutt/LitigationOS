## Surviving Cross-Examination

1. **Listen to the ENTIRE question before answering**
2. **Answer ONLY the question asked — do not volunteer**
3. **If you don't understand: "Could you rephrase that?"**
4. **If you don't remember: "I don't recall" (never guess)**
5. **If it's a yes/no question: answer yes or no, then explain if needed**
6. **NEVER argue with the examiner — address the judge**
7. **If the question mischaracterizes: "That's not accurate because..."**
8. **If compound question: "I'd like to answer that in parts"**
9. **TAKE YOUR TIME — there is no penalty for pausing to think**
10. **If you feel trapped: "I'd like to explain my answer, Your Honor"**
