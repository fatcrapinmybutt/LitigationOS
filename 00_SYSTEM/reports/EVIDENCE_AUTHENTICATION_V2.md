# EVIDENCE AUTHENTICATION CHECKLIST v2

*Michigan Rules of Evidence — Authentication for Every Evidence Type*

**10 evidence types | 39 authentication steps**

## Text Messages / SMS
*Rule: MRE 901(b)(1), (b)(4)*

1. Screenshot with phone number and contact name
2. Metadata with date/time stamps
3. Testimony identifying sender's number
4. Content referencing details only sender would know
5. Phone carrier records (subpoena if needed)

**Defense:** Self-authentication by distinctive characteristics — MRE 901(b)(4)

## Emails
*Rule: MRE 901(b)(1), (b)(4)*

1. Full email headers (From/To/Date/Subject)
2. Email address identification
3. Chain of custody from receipt to court
4. Reply doctrine: reply chain authenticates both

**Defense:** Reply doctrine + distinctive characteristics

## Social Media Posts
*Rule: MRE 901(b)(1), (b)(4)*

1. Screenshot with URL, profile name, date visible
2. Archive.org/Wayback copy if available
3. Testimony identifying account as party's
4. Content consistent with party's known statements

**Defense:** Screenshots + testimony + distinctive content

## Court Records / Orders
*Rule: MRE 901(b)(7), 902(4)*

1. Certified copy from court clerk (self-authenticating)
2. Case number and judge visible
3. Court seal/stamp present

**Defense:** Self-authenticating — MRE 902(4)

## Photographs
*Rule: MRE 901(b)(1)*

1. Testimony that photo accurately depicts scene
2. EXIF metadata (date/time/location)
3. Chain of custody from capture to court
4. Original unedited file with hash

**Defense:** Fair and accurate depiction testimony

## Audio / Video Recordings
*Rule: MRE 901(b)(1), (b)(5)*

1. Testimony identifying voices/persons
2. Original file with metadata preserved
3. No gaps or undisclosed edits
4. MI is ONE-PARTY CONSENT — MCL 750.539c
5. Transcription provided to court and opposing party

**Defense:** Voice identification + one-party consent

## Medical / Mental Health Records
*Rule: MRE 803(6), 902(11)*

1. Certified copy from provider
2. Business records affidavit — MRE 803(6)
3. HIPAA-compliant release on file
4. Records custodian certification

**Defense:** Business records exception — MRE 803(6)

## Financial Records
*Rule: MRE 803(6), 902(11)*

1. Certified copy from institution
2. Business records affidavit
3. Tax returns: certified from IRS or MI Treasury

**Defense:** Business records exception + certified copies

## Police / CPS Reports
*Rule: MRE 803(8), 902(4)*

1. Certified copy from agency (self-authenticating)
2. Report number and officer identification
3. MRE 803(8) public records exception

**Defense:** Public records exception — MRE 803(8)

## Affidavits / Declarations
*Rule: MCR 2.119(B)*

1. Signed under oath before notary
2. Notary seal, signature, commission expiration
3. Personal knowledge stated (MRE 602)
4. Specific facts not conclusions

**Defense:** Sworn under penalty of perjury — MCL 600.1561a
