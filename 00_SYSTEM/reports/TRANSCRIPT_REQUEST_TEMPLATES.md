## USING TRANSCRIPTS IN FILINGS

- **Citation format:** Tr. [Date] at p. [page], ll. [lines] (e.g., Tr. 03/15/2024 at p. 47, ll. 5-12)
- **Certification:** All transcript excerpts must be from certified copies
- **Context:** Include sufficient surrounding context to avoid misleading excerpts
- **Accuracy:** Quote verbatim — never paraphrase in sworn filings
- **Exhibit marking:** Attach relevant transcript pages as numbered exhibits
- **Hearsay:** Transcript testimony is NOT hearsay — it's prior testimony (MRE 801(d)(1))
- **Impeachment:** Use transcript inconsistencies to impeach at future hearings (MRE 613)
