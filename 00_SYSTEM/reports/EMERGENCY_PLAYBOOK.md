## 🟡 Emily Denies Court-Ordered Parenting Time
**Severity:** HIGH

### ⚡ IMMEDIATE (within minutes):
1. Document the denial: time, date, what she said, any witnesses
1. Send written message via OurFamilyWizard or text (create paper trail)
1. Do NOT force the issue — walk away calmly
1. Contact police for civil standby if needed (non-emergency line)

### 📋 WITHIN 24 HOURS:
1. File motion for make-up time (MCL 722.27a(7)(c))
1. File motion for contempt (MCR 3.606) — use Tool #126 templates
1. Request attorney fees for the contempt motion

**Authorities:** MCL 722.27a(7)(c), MCR 3.606, Tool #126

## 🔴 New False Allegations Filed Against You
**Severity:** CRITICAL

### ⚡ IMMEDIATE (within minutes):
1. DO NOT RESPOND ON SOCIAL MEDIA — say nothing publicly
1. Get a copy of the filing from the clerk ASAP
1. Read it carefully — note every factual claim
1. Start documenting your defense immediately

### 📋 WITHIN 24 HOURS:
1. File response within timeline (usually 21 days, but check)
1. Cross-reference claims against your evidence DB
1. Prepare counter-evidence for each false claim
1. Consider filing counter-complaint for perjury (MCL 750.423)

**Authorities:** MCL 750.423, MCR 2.108, Tool #111 perjury traps

## 🔴 Emergency Ex Parte Order Against You
**Severity:** CRITICAL

### ⚡ IMMEDIATE (within minutes):
1. COMPLY WITH THE ORDER — even if you believe it is wrong
1. Get a certified copy immediately
1. Note: ex parte orders require a hearing within 14 days (MCR 3.310(B))
1. Prepare your response for the hearing

### 📋 WITHIN 24 HOURS:
1. File objection to the ex parte order
1. Request expedited hearing
1. Prepare affidavit countering every claim in the ex parte motion
1. Gather all evidence contradicting the basis for the order

**Authorities:** MCR 3.310(B), MCL 722.27a(3), MCR 2.119(F)

## 🔴 Emily Relocates with L.D.W.
**Severity:** CRITICAL

### ⚡ IMMEDIATE (within minutes):
1. MCL 722.31 requires 100-mile notice — check if she gave notice
1. File IMMEDIATE motion to prevent relocation
1. Request emergency hearing
1. Contact law enforcement if court order is being violated

### 📋 WITHIN 24 HOURS:
1. File emergency motion for return of child
1. File contempt motion if relocation violates existing order
1. Request change of custody based on Factor I violation

**Authorities:** MCL 722.31, MCL 722.23(i) Factor I, MCR 3.214

## 🔴 You Are Arrested or Detained
**Severity:** CRITICAL

### ⚡ IMMEDIATE (within minutes):
1. REMAIN SILENT — invoke 5th Amendment immediately
1. Request an attorney — "I want a lawyer before I answer any questions"
1. Do not consent to searches
1. Do not discuss your custody case with police

### 📋 WITHIN 24 HOURS:
1. Contact legal aid (LSEM: 231-726-3815)
1. Document everything about the arrest
1. If arrest is related to false allegations → this is evidence for F4 (§1983)
1. File complaint with internal affairs if arrest was improper

**Authorities:** 5th Amendment, 4th Amendment, 42 USC §1983

## 🟡 CPS Investigation Initiated
**Severity:** HIGH

### ⚡ IMMEDIATE (within minutes):
1. COOPERATE FULLY with CPS — do not refuse access
1. Be polite and professional at all times
1. You CAN have an attorney present during interview
1. Document the investigator name, badge number, and every question asked

### 📋 WITHIN 24 HOURS:
1. Request a copy of the complaint (you have a right to it)
1. Prepare your home for a visit (clean, safe, child-friendly)
1. Gather character references (employer, neighbors, family)
1. If investigation is baseless → use as evidence of harassment pattern

**Authorities:** MCL 722.627, MCL 722.628, Tool #134 evidence preservation

## 🟡 Judge Refuses to Follow MCR/MCL
**Severity:** HIGH

### ⚡ IMMEDIATE (within minutes):
1. Object ON THE RECORD — "Your Honor, I respectfully object..."
1. Cite the specific rule or statute being violated
1. Request the ruling be put in writing
1. State for the record: "I preserve this issue for appeal"

### 📋 WITHIN 24 HOURS:
1. File motion for reconsideration citing the specific MCR/MCL
1. Add to JTC complaint (F6) if pattern continues
1. Preserve for COA appeal (F8/F9)

**Authorities:** MCR 2.119(F), MCR 7.205, MCR 2.003
