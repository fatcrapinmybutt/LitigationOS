## Case Study: The Origin Story

### "Built by a Pro Se Father Fighting for His Son"

> *"I couldn't afford a $15,000 retainer. I couldn't afford to lose my son either. So I built the tool I wished existed."*

**The Problem:**
Andre faced a family court system designed for lawyers, not fathers. Filing deadlines were confusing. Motion formats were arcane. Legal research was locked behind $400/hour billing rates. Every mistake meant delayed hearings and more time away from his son.

**The Solution:**
What started as personal Python scripts to track deadlines and generate motions became LitigationOS - an AI-powered legal platform that gives self-represented litigants the same tools that $500/hour attorneys use.

**Key Metrics:**
- 200+ hours of legal research automated
- 50+ court documents generated
- $12,000+ in estimated legal fee savings
- Built entirely while navigating an active custody case

**Why It Matters:**
- 76% of civil cases have at least one self-represented party
- Average family law attorney costs $250-$500/hour
- Most pro se litigants lose not because they're wrong, but because they can't navigate the system

**The Vision:**
Every parent, every person, deserves access to competent legal tools regardless of their bank account. LitigationOS exists because the justice system should serve everyone - not just those who can afford it.
