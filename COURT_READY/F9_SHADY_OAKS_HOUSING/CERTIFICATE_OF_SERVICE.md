## SERVICE NOTES AND REMINDERS

### MCR 2.107 Requirements
- Service of pleadings after the initial complaint must be made on every party that has appeared or is required to appear.
- Service may be made by delivery to the party or the party's attorney, or by mailing to the party's or attorney's last known address.
- **Proof of service** must be filed with the court.

### MCR 2.105 — Service of Process (Initial Summons and Complaint)
- **LLC Service (MCR 2.105(D))**: Service upon an LLC is made by serving a summons and complaint upon an officer, director, managing agent, or registered agent.
- **Foreign LLC (MCR 2.105(E))**: A foreign LLC doing business in Michigan may be served through its registered agent or through the Secretary of State if no registered agent is designated.
- **Dissolved LLC**: If Homes of America, LLC has no registered agent due to dissolution, service may be made via the Michigan Secretary of State pursuant to MCL 450.4210.
