## AUTHENTICATION METHODOLOGY

All exhibits will be authenticated through one or more of the following methods pursuant to the Michigan Rules of Evidence:

- **MRE 901(b)(1)** — Testimony of a witness with knowledge (Plaintiff as photographer, author, or recipient);
- **MRE 901(b)(4)** — Distinctive characteristics (metadata, formatting, timestamps);
- **MRE 803(6)** — Business records exception (Defendants' own ledgers, billing records);
- **MRE 801(d)(2)** — Admissions by party opponent (Defendants' own statements, emails, filings);
- **MRE 902(4)** — Self-authenticating public records (EGLE reports, LARA records, court filings, tax records);
- **MRE 1003** — Admissibility of duplicates;
- **MRE 1006** — Summaries of voluminous records.
