## NOTES ON AUTHENTICATION

All exhibits are offered pursuant to the Michigan Rules of Evidence (MRE) and the Michigan Court Rules (MCR). Specific authentication bases are noted for each exhibit above.

- **Court Records (Exhibits A, B, J, K, L):** Self-authenticating under MRE 902(4) as certified copies of public records.
- **Business Records (Exhibits C, G, H, I):** Admissible under MRE 803(6) as records made at or near the time of the event, kept in the course of a regularly conducted business activity, and made as a regular practice of that activity.
- **Recordings and Communications (Exhibits D, E):** Authenticated through Plaintiff's personal knowledge as a party to or recipient of the communications under MRE 901(b)(1).
- **Summary (Exhibit F):** Admissible under MRE 1006 as a summary of voluminous records.

Plaintiff reserves the right to supplement this exhibit list prior to or at the evidentiary hearing.
