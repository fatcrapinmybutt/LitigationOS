#### Pattern Evidence — Perjury

The volume and variety of false statements demonstrates a **pattern of fabrication**, not isolated mistakes:
- Multiple categories of false allegations (poison, drugs, assault, stalking, threats)
- All served the same purpose: obtaining court orders against Complainant
- None corroborated by any independent evidence
- Pattern eliminates "honest mistake" defense — this was deliberate, systematic perjury
