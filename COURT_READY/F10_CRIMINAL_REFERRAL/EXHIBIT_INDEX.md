### EXHIBIT A — PPO PETITION (False Statements Highlighted)

**Description:** The original Personal Protection Order petition filed by Emily A. Watson in Case No. 2023-5907-PP. This document contains Watson's sworn false statements, including the arsenic poisoning allegation, the inflated "straw incident," surveillance/stalking claims, methamphetamine attribution, and fabricated threat allegations.

**How to Obtain:** Request certified copy from the Muskegon County Circuit Court Clerk, 990 Terrace Street, Muskegon, MI 49442.

**Relevance:** Establishes the foundation of Watson's perjury. Each false sworn statement in this document constitutes a separate count of perjury under MCL 750.423. This document is also the "poisonous tree" — the PPO obtained through this perjury was subsequently used to justify the suspension of all parenting time.

**Preparation Note:** Highlight or annotate each false statement with a marginal note identifying the specific claim and the contradicting evidence.

### EXHIBIT D — ALBERT WATSON KITCHEN RECORDING (November 2024)

**Description:** Audio/video recording from November 2024 captured at 2160 Garland Drive, Norton Shores, MI 49441 (Watson residence). The recording captures Albert Watson (Emily's father) and Emily Watson berating Complainant in the kitchen. Albert Watson explicitly states: **"I will make sure you don't see your son."**

**Format:** Audio/video file (digital format — provide on USB drive with transcript)

**Admissibility:** Recorded with Complainant's consent as a party to the conversation. Michigan is a one-party consent state (MCL 750.539c). Recording is admissible under MRE 901(b)(1) through Complainant's testimony as a participant.

**Relevance:** This is the single most important piece of evidence in the criminal referral. It establishes:
- Direct statement of the conspiracy's objective (preventing father from seeing son)
- Albert Watson's active participation in the scheme
- Emily Watson's presence and tacit agreement
- Premeditation — the statement was made months before the August 2025 custody suspension
- The threatening and intimidating environment created by the Watson family

**Preparation Note:** Prepare a written transcript of the recording. Provide both the original digital file and the transcript to the Prosecutor's Office.
