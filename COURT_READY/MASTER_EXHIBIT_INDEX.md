## AUTHENTICATION REQUIREMENTS

### For Court Records
- Obtain **certified copies** from the Clerk of Court
- Each certified copy has a clerk's stamp/seal and date
- Cost: typically $1/page + certification fee

### For Recordings (Albert Watson Audio)
- Create a written transcript
- Include affidavit of authenticity: "I, Andrew James Pigors, personally made this recording on [DATE] at 2160 Garland Drive, Norton Shores, MI. The recording has not been altered."
- Michigan is a ONE-PARTY consent state (MCL 750.539c) — Andrew's presence makes it legal
- Bring the original recording device to court if possible

### For Text Messages / Screenshots
- Print full-page screenshots showing: contact name, phone number, date/time, message content
- Include affidavit: "These screenshots were taken from my personal device on [DATE] and accurately reflect the messages received."
- Preserve original device as backup

### For AppClose Records
- Export official records from the AppClose platform
- Include account holder verification
- Time-stamped records are self-authenticating business records

### For HealthWest Records
- Obtain via FOIA request to HealthWest
- Records obtained via FOIA are self-authenticating under MRE 803(6) (business records exception)
- Keep the FOIA response letter with the records
