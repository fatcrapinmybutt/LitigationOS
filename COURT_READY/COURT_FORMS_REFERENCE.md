This document lists every SCAO form needed for each filing, with download URLs and auto-fill status.

## UNIVERSAL FORMS (Needed for most filings)

### MC 02 — Proof of Service
- **URL:** https://courts.michigan.gov/siteassets/forms/scao/mc02.pdf
- **Required for:** F1, F2, F3, F5, F7, F8, F9
- **Auto-fill fields:** 9 (plaintiff name, defendant name, case number, court, date, method, server info)

### MC 20 — Fee Waiver Request
- **URL:** https://courts.michigan.gov/siteassets/forms/scao/mc20.pdf
- **Use when:** Cannot afford filing fee ($175-$405)
- **Auto-fill fields:** 16 (income, expenses, assets, dependents)
- **Applies to:** F4 ($405), F5 ($375), F7 ($375), F9 (~$175)
