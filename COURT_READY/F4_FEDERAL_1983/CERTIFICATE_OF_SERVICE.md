## I. SERVICE OF PROCESS — FRCP RULE 4

Plaintiff proceeds in forma pauperis pursuant to 28 U.S.C. § 1915. Under **28 U.S.C. § 1915(d)**, "the officers of the court shall issue and serve all process" for IFP litigants. Accordingly, Plaintiff requests that the **Clerk of Court issue summonses** and that the **United States Marshal** effectuate service of process upon all Defendants pursuant to **Fed. R. Civ. P. 4(c)(3)**.

Service must be completed within **90 days** of the filing of this Complaint. Fed. R. Civ. P. 4(m).
