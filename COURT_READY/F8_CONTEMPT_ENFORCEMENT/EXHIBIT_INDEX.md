## EXHIBIT G — MCL 552.644 REMEDY CHART: MAKEUP PARENTING TIME CALCULATION

**Statutory Authority:** MCL 722.27a(7) ("If a parent is denied parenting time... the court

shall order makeup parenting time of the same type and duration as the parenting time

that was denied.")

| Calculation Item | Value |
|-----------------|-------|
| **Date parenting time denied began** | August 8, 2025 |
| **Date of this filing** | March 19, 2026 |
| **Total consecutive days denied** | 223 days |
| **Prior parenting time schedule** | 50/50 (equal time) |
| **Parenting time days owed (50% of 223)** | 111.5 days (round to 112 days) |
| **Statutory deadline to exercise makeup time** | Within one year of each denial (MCL 552.642) |
| **Makeup schedule requested** | Plaintiff selects dates with advance notice to Defendant and FOC |

**Note:** The 223-day count is based on the calendar count from August 8, 2025, to

March 19, 2026. Under the prior 50/50 schedule, Plaintiff would have had L.D.W.

approximately 50% of this time, yielding approximately 112 days of makeup parenting

time owed. The actual count continues to increase with each passing day.

**Additional violations (pre-August 8, 2025):** Defendant's sporadic denials, canceled

visits, and 44 AppClose violations during the 50/50 period represent additional parenting

time occasions denied, for which separate makeup time may be warranted.
