1. **24 ex parte orders without notice** — no judge should enter 24 one-sided orders in a custody case. This is the centerpiece. It survives immunity (pattern evidence), Rooker-Feldman (process claim), and discretion defense (mandatory hearing requirements).

4. **HealthWest biasing letter** — if provable, this is both a due process violation AND the best immunity-piercing argument. A judge secretly writing to a psychological evaluator to influence findings is not a "judicial act."

5. **AppClose cooperative messages** — 124 cooperative co-parenting messages from the court-approved platform directly rebut the "dangerous father" narrative.
