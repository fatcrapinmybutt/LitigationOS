### FT-001: General Motion Template

```
STATE OF MICHIGAN
IN THE 14TH JUDICIAL CIRCUIT COURT FOR MUSKEGON COUNTY

[PLAINTIFF NAME],                    Case No. [YYYY-NNNNNN-XX]
        Plaintiff/Petitioner,
                                     Hon. [JUDGE NAME]
v.

[DEFENDANT NAME],
        Defendant/Respondent.
________________________________________/

                    MOTION FOR [DESCRIPTION]

        NOW COMES [Plaintiff/Petitioner], [pro se / by counsel],
    and respectfully moves this Honorable Court for an order
    [specific relief requested], and in support states:

    1. [Factual basis paragraph 1]

    2. [Factual basis paragraph 2]

    3. [Legal authority paragraph — cite MCR/MCL/case law]

    4. [Relief requested — specific and actionable]

        WHEREFORE, [Plaintiff/Petitioner] respectfully requests
    that this Court enter an order [specific relief].

                                    Respectfully submitted,

                                    ___________________________
                                    [Name]
                                    [Address]
                                    [Phone]
                                    [Email]
                                    [Date]
```

### FT-006: Custody Motion Template (Lane A)

```
STATE OF MICHIGAN
IN THE 14TH JUDICIAL CIRCUIT COURT FOR MUSKEGON COUNTY
FAMILY DIVISION

[PARENT NAME],                       Case No. 2024-001507-DC
        Plaintiff,
                                     Hon. McNeill
v.

[PARENT NAME],
        Defendant.
________________________________________/

        MOTION TO MODIFY CUSTODY / PARENTING TIME

    Pursuant to MCR 3.210 and MCL 722.27, [Movant] moves for
    modification of [custody / parenting time] and states:

    PROPER CAUSE / CHANGE OF CIRCUMSTANCES
    (Vodvarka v Grasmeyer, 259 Mich App 1 (2003))

    1. Since the last custody order dated [DATE], the following
       change(s) of circumstances have occurred:
       [Specific factual changes]

    BEST INTEREST FACTORS (MCL 722.23)

    2. Factor (a) — Love, affection, emotional ties:
       [Facts supporting this factor]

    3. Factor (b) — Capacity to provide:
       [Facts]

    [Continue through all 12 factors as applicable]

    VERIFIED STATEMENT (MCR 3.206(A))

    I declare under penalty of perjury that the facts stated
    in this motion are true to the best of my knowledge.

                                    ___________________________
                                    [Name], [Pro se / Attorney]
                                    [Date]
```

### FT-008: Civil Complaint Template (Lane B)

```
STATE OF MICHIGAN
IN THE 14TH JUDICIAL CIRCUIT COURT FOR MUSKEGON COUNTY

[TENANT NAME],                       Case No. 2025-002760-CZ
        Plaintiff,
                                     Hon. Hoopes
v.

SHADY OAKS HOMES OF AMERICA, LLC,
a limited liability company, and
ALDEN GLOBAL CAPITAL, LLC,
a limited liability company,
        Defendants.
________________________________________/

                    COMPLAINT

    COUNT I — BREACH OF WARRANTY OF HABITABILITY
    (MCL 554.139)

    1. [Factual allegations — lease, conditions, notice]
    2. [Specific habitability defects]
    3. [Damages]

    COUNT II — MICHIGAN CONSUMER PROTECTION ACT
    (MCL 445.903)

    4. [Unfair/deceptive practices allegations]
    5. [Specific violations]

    PRAYER FOR RELIEF

    WHEREFORE, Plaintiff requests:
    a. Compensatory damages in excess of $25,000;
    b. Treble damages under MCPA;
    c. Injunctive relief requiring repairs;
    d. Attorney fees and costs;
    e. Such other relief as the Court deems just.

                                    ___________________________
                                    [Name]
                                    [Date]
```

### Formatting Requirements — 14th Judicial Circuit

| Element | Requirement | Authority |
|---------|-------------|-----------|
| Font | 12-point Times New Roman or equivalent | MCR 2.119(A)(1) |
| Spacing | Double-spaced (except block quotes) | MCR 2.119(A)(1) |
| Margins | 1 inch all sides | MCR 2.119(A)(1) |
| Page limit (briefs) | 20 pages unless leave granted | Local Rule |
| Paper size | 8.5 × 11 inches | MCR 2.119(A)(1) |
| Caption | Full case caption on first page | MCR 2.113(C) |
| Signature | Wet or electronic per MCR 2.114 | MCR 2.114(C) |
