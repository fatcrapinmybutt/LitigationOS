# Service Requirements — litigation-filing-architect

## Michigan Service of Process Rules

This reference covers all service requirements for the 14th Judicial Circuit,
Muskegon County, across all three case lanes in Pigors v Watson litigation.

---

## Service Methods — MCR 2.105 (Initial Service / Summons)

### Personal Service (MCR 2.105(A))
| Element | Requirement |
|---------|-------------|
| Who serves | Any person ≥ 18 years old who is not a party |
| Delivery | Hand-deliver to defendant personally |
| Where | Anywhere defendant is found |
| When | Any time (avoid Sundays if possible by custom) |
| Proof | Affidavit of service by server |

### Substituted Service (MCR 2.105(A)(2))
| Element | Requirement |
|---------|-------------|
| Condition | Personal service unsuccessful after reasonable diligence |
| Method | Leave at defendant's usual place of abode with person of suitable age |
| Additional | Mail copy to last known address |
| Proof | Affidavit detailing attempts + substituted service |

### Service by Mail (MCR 2.105(A)(2))
| Element | Requirement |
|---------|-------------|
| Method | Registered or certified mail, return receipt requested |
| Address | Defendant's last known address |
| Proof | Return receipt (signed) + affidavit |
| Effective | Date of delivery shown on return receipt |

### Service by Publication (MCR 2.106)
| Element | Requirement |
|---------|-------------|
| Condition | Cannot serve by other methods after diligent inquiry |
| Court order | Must obtain order authorizing publication |
| Publication | Newspaper of general circulation in county |
| Duration | Once each week for 3 consecutive weeks |
| Mailing | Also mail to last known address |
| Muskegon County | Muskegon Chronicle or equivalent |

## Service of Subsequent Documents — MCR 2.107

### After Initial Service — All Subsequent Filings

| Method | MCR Authority | Days Added | Effective Date |
|--------|---------------|------------|----------------|
| Personal delivery | 2.107(C)(1) | 0 | Date of delivery |
| Mail (first class) | 2.107(C)(2) | +5 days to deadline | Date of mailing |
| Email (if consented) | 2.107(C)(4) | +1 day to deadline | Date of sending |
| E-filing service | 2.107(C)(4) | +1 day to deadline | Date of transmission |
| Fax (if consented) | 2.107(C)(3) | +1 day to deadline | Date of fax |

### Who Must Be Served
- All parties who have appeared in the case
- Friend of the Court (Lane A — mandatory for all custody/support filings)
- Guardian ad litem (if appointed)
- Any intervenor

## Service Requirements by Lane

### Lane A — Custody / PPO (Watson)

| Filing Type | Must Serve | Method | Timeline |
|-------------|-----------|--------|----------|
| Custody motion | Watson + FOC | Personal or mail | ≥ 9 days (personal) or ≥ 14 days (mail) before hearing |
| PPO modification | Respondent | Per MCR 3.706 | Before or at hearing |
| Discovery requests | Watson | Any MCR 2.107 method | 28 days to respond |
| Subpoena | Witness | Personal | Reasonable time before |
| Emergency motion | Watson + FOC | Best available | As soon as practicable |

### Lane B — Housing / Civil (Shady Oaks)

| Filing Type | Must Serve | Method | Timeline |
|-------------|-----------|--------|----------|
| Complaint + Summons | Shady Oaks LLC + Alden Global LLC | MCR 2.105 (initial) | Within 91 days of filing (MCR 2.102(D)) |
| Corporate service | Registered agent (MCR 2.105(D)) | Personal on agent | Within 91 days |
| Discovery requests | Corporate counsel | MCR 2.107 | 28 days to respond |
| Motion | Corporate counsel | MCR 2.107 | ≥ 9/14 days before hearing |

**Corporate Service Note — MCR 2.105(D)**:
Service on an LLC requires delivery to an officer, director, resident agent,
or person in charge of the office. For Shady Oaks and Alden Global, serve
the registered agent listed with Michigan LARA (Department of Licensing and
Regulatory Affairs).

### Lane C — Convergence / County

| Filing Type | Must Serve | Method | Timeline |
|-------------|-----------|--------|----------|
| 42 USC § 1983 (federal) | Government defendants | FRCP 4 (federal rules) | 90 days (FRCP 4(m)) |
| State claims | County entities | MCR 2.105 | 91 days (MCR 2.102(D)) |
| JTC complaint | Judicial Tenure Commission | Mail to JTC | Per JTC rules |
| Mandamus | Government officer | Personal | Per MCR 3.305 |

## Proof of Service Requirements

### Required Elements (MCR 2.104(A))
1. Name of person served
2. Description of documents served
3. Date of service
4. Method of service
5. Address where served (if mail) or location (if personal)
6. Name and signature of person who served
7. Statement that server is ≥ 18 and not a party

### Common Proof of Service Errors
| Error | Consequence | Fix |
|-------|-------------|-----|
| Server is a party to the case | Service invalid | Use non-party server |
| Wrong address | Service may be invalid | Verify current address |
| Date omitted | Cannot calculate deadlines | Always include date |
| Method not specified | Ambiguous deadline calculation | Specify exact method |
| Documents not listed | Unclear what was served | List every document |

## Service Deadline Calculator — MCR 2.119(C)

### Motion Hearing Service Deadlines

```
Hearing Date: [H]

Personal Service:
  Motion + Brief due: H - 9 days
  Response Brief due: H - 7 days (from opposing party)
  Reply Brief due: H - 3 days (if permitted)

Mail Service:
  Motion + Brief due: H - 14 days (9 + 5 mail days)
  Response Brief due: H - 7 days
  Reply Brief due: H - 3 days

E-Filing / Email Service:
  Motion + Brief due: H - 10 days (9 + 1 e-service day)
  Response Brief due: H - 7 days
  Reply Brief due: H - 3 days
```

### Emergency / Ex Parte (MCR 3.207)
- Service: "As soon as practicable" after filing
- Method: Best available (phone + email + mail)
- Hearing: Within 14 days of ex parte order
- Document actual service attempts for the record
