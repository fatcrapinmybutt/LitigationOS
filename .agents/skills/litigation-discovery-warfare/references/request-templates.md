## I. Interrogatory Template (MCR 2.309)

### Caption Block

```
STATE OF MICHIGAN
IN THE 14TH JUDICIAL CIRCUIT COURT FOR MUSKEGON COUNTY
[FAMILY/CIVIL DIVISION]

[PLAINTIFF NAME],
        Plaintiff,                    Case No. [CASE NUMBER]
v.                                    Hon. [JUDGE NAME]
[DEFENDANT NAME],
        Defendant.
________________________________________/

PLAINTIFF'S FIRST SET OF INTERROGATORIES TO DEFENDANT [NAME]
```

### Definitions Section (Include in EVERY Set)

```
DEFINITIONS

As used in these Interrogatories:

1. "COMMUNICATION" means any oral, written, or electronic exchange of
   information, including but not limited to letters, emails, text messages,
   voicemails, social media messages, in-person conversations, and telephone calls.

2. "DOCUMENT" has the meaning set forth in MCR 2.310(A)(1) and includes
   all writings, drawings, graphs, charts, photographs, electronically stored
   information, and other data compilations from which information can be obtained.

3. "IDENTIFY" when referring to a person means state the person's full name,
   current address, telephone number, employer, and relationship to any party.
   When referring to a document, state the document's title, date, author,
   recipient(s), and custodian.

4. "RELATING TO" or "CONCERNING" means referring to, describing, evidencing,
   constituting, mentioning, or in any way pertaining to the subject referenced.

5. "YOU" and "YOUR" refer to the answering party, including agents, employees,
   representatives, and attorneys.
```

### Instructions Section

```
INSTRUCTIONS

1. These interrogatories are continuing in nature under MCR 2.302(E). If you
   obtain additional information after serving your answers, you must supplement
   your responses promptly.

2. Where an interrogatory asks you to "identify" or "describe," provide all
   information within your knowledge, possession, custody, or control, or
   available through reasonable inquiry.

3. If you object to any interrogatory, state the specific grounds for objection
   and answer to the extent not objectionable, as required by MCR 2.309(B)(4).

4. Answers must be made under oath within 28 days of service per MCR 2.309(B).
```

### Lane A — Custody Interrogatories (Sample Set)

```
INTERROGATORIES

1. Identify all persons who have resided in your household at any time since
   January 1, 2023, including dates of residency and relationship to you.

2. Describe your current work schedule, including employer name, days/hours
   worked, and any schedule changes in the past 12 months.

3. For each occasion since [DATE] on which you denied or interfered with
   Plaintiff's parenting time, state: (a) the date; (b) the reason given;
   (c) whether you provided makeup parenting time; (d) the name of any
   witness present.

4. Identify all communications between you and Gerald Rusco (GAL) from
   [DATE] to present, including: (a) date; (b) method; (c) topic discussed;
   (d) whether any other person was present or copied.

5. Describe all incidents in which the minor child(ren) expressed a preference
   regarding custody or parenting time, including date, substance, and any
   witnesses.

6. State all facts that support your contention that the current custody
   arrangement is in the best interest of the minor child(ren) under
   MCL 722.23.

7. Identify all healthcare providers (medical, dental, mental health) who
   have treated the minor child(ren) since [DATE].

8. Identify all schools, daycare providers, and extracurricular activities
   in which the minor child(ren) have been enrolled since [DATE].
```

### Lane B — Housing Interrogatories (Sample Set)

```
INTERROGATORIES

1. Identify the complete corporate ownership chain of the property known as
   Shady Oaks, including all parent companies, subsidiaries, and holding
   entities, with the dates each entity held an ownership interest.

2. Identify all maintenance requests, complaints, or work orders submitted
   by or relating to Plaintiff's unit from [DATE] to present, including:
   (a) date submitted; (b) nature of complaint; (c) date of response;
   (d) action taken; (e) person who performed work.

3. State all facts supporting your contention that the property was maintained
   in habitable condition during Plaintiff's tenancy.

4. Identify all inspections of Plaintiff's unit by any government agency,
   including: (a) date; (b) agency; (c) inspector name; (d) findings;
   (e) any violations cited.

5. Identify all communications between Shady Oaks management and Homes of
   America or Alden Global Capital concerning Plaintiff or Plaintiff's unit.

6. State the total revenue generated by Shady Oaks for each calendar year
   from 2022 to present, and the total maintenance expenditures for the
   same periods.

7. Identify all policies or procedures governing: (a) maintenance response
   times; (b) habitability standards; (c) tenant complaint handling;
   (d) eviction procedures.
```

## III. Request for Admissions Template (MCR 2.312)

### Lane A — Custody RFAs

```
REQUESTS FOR ADMISSIONS

1. Admit that you received Plaintiff's request for parenting time on [DATE].

2. Admit that you did not provide Plaintiff with parenting time on [DATE]
   as ordered by the court.

3. Admit that you communicated with Gerald Rusco (GAL) outside the presence
   of Plaintiff on [specify dates].

4. Admit that the document attached as Exhibit A is a true and correct copy
   of [describe document].

5. Admit that the minor child(ren) have expressed a desire to spend more
   time with Plaintiff.

6. Admit that you have not provided Plaintiff with the minor child(ren)'s
   school records upon request.
```

### Lane B — Housing RFAs

```
REQUESTS FOR ADMISSIONS

1. Admit that Homes of America LLC is the management company for Shady Oaks.

2. Admit that Alden Global Capital holds a controlling ownership interest
   in the entity that owns Shady Oaks.

3. Admit that Plaintiff submitted a maintenance request regarding [specific
   condition] on [DATE].

4. Admit that no repair was made to [specific condition] within 30 days of
   Plaintiff's request.

5. Admit that [government agency] cited Shady Oaks for [specific violation]
   on [DATE].

6. Admit that the document attached as Exhibit A is a true and correct copy
   of the lease agreement between Plaintiff and Shady Oaks.
```

[Witness fee: $12.00 per day enclosed / mileage at statutory rate]

## V. Deposition Notice Template (MCR 2.306)

```
NOTICE OF TAKING DEPOSITION UPON ORAL EXAMINATION

TO: [Witness Name / Attorney for Party]

PLEASE TAKE NOTICE that on [DATE] at [TIME], at [LOCATION], Plaintiff
will take the deposition upon oral examination of [DEPONENT NAME] pursuant
to MCR 2.306.

The deposition will be recorded by [stenographic / audio / video] means.

[For corporate designee:]
Pursuant to MCR 2.306(B)(5), the deposition will cover the following topics:
1. [Topic 1]
2. [Topic 2]
3. [Topic 3]

You are directed to designate one or more officers, directors, or managing
agents, or other persons who consent to testify on behalf of the organization
regarding the identified topics.
```
