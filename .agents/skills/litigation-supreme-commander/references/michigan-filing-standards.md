# Michigan Filing Standards Reference — litigation-supreme-commander

## MCR Formatting Requirements

### Caption Format (MCR 1.109(D)(1))

Every filing must include a caption with:
```
STATE OF MICHIGAN
IN THE [COURT NAME]
[COUNTY] COUNTY

[PLAINTIFF NAME],                    Case No. [NUMBER]
        Plaintiff,
                                     Hon. [JUDGE NAME]
v.

[DEFENDANT NAME],
        Defendant.
_________________________________/
```

For Pigors v Watson (14th Circuit):
```
STATE OF MICHIGAN
IN THE CIRCUIT COURT FOR THE COUNTY OF MUSKEGON

ANDREW JAMES PIGORS,                Case No. 2024-001507-DC
        Plaintiff,
                                     Hon. Jenny L. McNeill
v.

EMILY A. WATSON,
        Defendant.
_________________________________/
```

### Document Title
- Must appear below the caption, centered or left-justified
- Must accurately describe the document (e.g., "MOTION FOR DISQUALIFICATION OF JUDGE")
- Include "AND BRIEF IN SUPPORT" if combined per MCR 2.119(A)(2)

## Page Limits

| Court | Document Type | Page Limit | Authority |
|-------|--------------|------------|-----------|
| Circuit Court | Brief in support of motion | 20 pages | MCR 2.119(A)(2) |
| Circuit Court | Response brief | 20 pages | MCR 2.119(A)(2) |
| Circuit Court | Reply brief | 10 pages | MCR 2.119(A)(2) |
| COA | Appellant's brief | 50 pages | MCR 7.212(B) |
| COA | Appellee's brief | 50 pages | MCR 7.212(B) |
| COA | Reply brief | 25 pages | MCR 7.212(B) |
| MSC | Application for leave | 50 pages | MCR 7.305(A) |
| USDC WDMI | Brief in support | Per local rule | W.D. Mich. LCivR 7.2 |

Page limits exclude caption, table of contents, table of authorities, signature block,
certificate of service, and appendix/exhibits unless otherwise specified.

### Motion to Exceed Page Limit
If more pages are needed, file a separate motion BEFORE the substantive filing.
Form available at `form_motion-to-exceed-page-limit.pdf`.

## Certificate of Service Requirements (MCR 2.107)

### Required Elements
Every certificate of service must include:
1. Date of service
2. Method of service (personal, mail, electronic)
3. Name and address of each person served
4. Signature of person who performed service

### Template
```

I, Andrew James Pigors, certify that on [DATE], I served a copy of the
foregoing [DOCUMENT TITLE] on the following by [METHOD]:
```

## E-Filing Requirements

### TrueFiling (COA/MSC)
- PDF format only
- Max file size: 25 MB per document
- OCR-searchable text required
- Bookmarks for documents over 20 pages
- Separate PDF for each document type (brief, appendix, exhibits)

### MiFile (Circuit Court)
- PDF format preferred
- Documents must be legible
- Filing fee payment at time of filing (or fee waiver order)
- Proposed orders submitted separately

### Rejection Standards
Common rejection reasons (see `rejection_stds.pdf`):
- Illegible documents
- Missing case number
- Wrong court selected
- Missing filing fee / fee waiver
- Incorrect document type selected
- Missing required signatures
