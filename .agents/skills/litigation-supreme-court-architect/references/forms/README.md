# MSC FORM CATALOG

## PURPOSE

Complete catalog of every form required for Michigan Supreme Court filings.
Each form is mapped to its use case, governing rule, and completion instructions.

---

## FORM: CC 298 — APPLICATION FOR LEAVE TO APPEAL

### Identification
- **Form Number**: CC 298
- **Title**: Application for Leave to Appeal
- **Source**: Michigan Supreme Court Clerk's Office
- **Required For**: PATH 1 (Leave to Appeal), PATH 2 (Bypass)

### Required Fields
1. **MSC Case Number** — Leave blank; assigned by clerk upon filing
2. **COA Case Number** — From COA docket (e.g., No. XXXXXX)
3. **Trial Court Case Number** — From circuit court (e.g., 2024-001507-DC)
4. **Applicant Name and Address** — Full legal name, mailing address, phone, email
5. **Respondent Name and Address** — Opposing party/parties
6. **Trial Court** — 14th Judicial Circuit, Muskegon County
7. **Trial Judge** — Name of trial judge whose order is challenged
8. **COA Judges** — Names of COA panel judges
9. **Date of COA Decision** — Exact date
10. **Questions Presented** — Brief statement (detailed in application brief)
11. **Grounds for Leave** — MCR 7.305(B) subsection(s) relied upon
12. **Signature** — Applicant or attorney signature and date

### Completion Notes
- Type or print clearly — illegible forms may be returned
- If pro se: include "In Propria Persona" after name
- Verify all case numbers are accurate — errors cause delays
- Attach to front of application brief

## FORM: DC 53 — APPEAL WORKSHEET

### Identification
- **Form Number**: DC 53
- **Title**: Appeal Record Worksheet
- **Source**: Michigan Courts
- **Required For**: Record tracking; recommended for all appeals

### Purpose
- Tracks all documents constituting the record on appeal
- Ensures completeness of record transmitted to MSC
- Identifies missing transcripts, exhibits, or orders

### Required Fields
1. **Case Caption and Numbers** — All court levels
2. **List of Trial Court Documents** — Date filed, description, page count
3. **Transcript List** — Hearing date, reporter, pages, status (ordered/received/filed)
4. **Exhibit List** — Exhibit number, description, admitted (Y/N)
5. **COA Documents** — Briefs, opinions, orders

### Completion Notes
- Complete this form FIRST — before drafting any brief
- Use to identify gaps in the record
- File with trial court clerk to settle record disputes per MCR 7.210

## FORM: MC 20 — FEE WAIVER / AFFIDAVIT OF INDIGENCY

### Identification
- **Form Number**: MC 20
- **Title**: Affidavit of Indigency / Request for Fee Waiver
- **Source**: Michigan Courts — SCAO approved form
- **Required For**: Any filing where $375 fee cannot be paid

### Required Fields
1. **Personal Information** — Name, address, date of birth
2. **Employment Status** — Employed/unemployed, employer, income
3. **Monthly Income** — All sources (wages, benefits, child support, etc.)
4. **Monthly Expenses** — Rent, utilities, food, medical, transportation, etc.
5. **Assets** — Bank accounts, vehicles, property, investments
6. **Dependents** — Number and relationship
7. **Public Assistance** — Whether receiving DHHS benefits, food assistance, Medicaid
8. **Verification** — Sworn under penalty of perjury
9. **Signature and Notarization** — Must be signed; notarization preferred

### Standard for Fee Waiver
- Unable to pay fees without depriving self or dependents of necessities of life
- Receipt of public assistance (DHHS, food stamps, Medicaid) creates presumption of eligibility
- Court has discretion — may grant full waiver, partial waiver, or installment plan

### Completion Notes for Pro Se
- Be thorough and honest — misrepresentation is contempt of court
- Attach proof of income (pay stubs, benefit letters) if available
- If receiving any form of public assistance, state this prominently
- File with the application — do not file separately

## FORM: MC 12 — PROOF OF SERVICE

### Identification
- **Form Number**: MC 12
- **Title**: Proof of Service
- **Source**: Michigan Courts — SCAO approved form
- **Required For**: EVERY filing in MSC — no exceptions

### Required Fields
1. **Case Caption and Number** — MSC number (if assigned), COA number, trial court number
2. **Document Served** — Title of document(s) served
3. **Date of Service** — Exact date
4. **Method of Service** — Check appropriate box:
   - [ ] First-class mail
   - [ ] Personal delivery
   - [ ] Electronic service (if consented per MCR 2.107(C)(4))
   - [ ] Overnight delivery
5. **Persons Served** — Name and address of each person/attorney served
6. **Signature** — Person who performed service (may be the filing party)
7. **Date Signed**

### Service Rules — MCR 2.107
- Service on EVERY party or attorney of record
- Must be simultaneous with filing (same day)
- Mail service: add 3 days to any response deadline (MCR 2.107(C)(3))
- Certificate of service may substitute for MC 12 if it contains same information

### Completion Notes
- Complete MC 12 AFTER actually performing service — not before
- Keep proof of mailing (USPS receipt, certified mail receipt)
- If serving multiple parties, list ALL on one MC 12
- Attach as last page of filed document

## COVER PAGE REQUIREMENTS

### Governing Rule
- MCR 7.212(A) — applicable to MSC briefs via MCR 7.300(B)

### Required Elements
1. **Court Identification**: "IN THE SUPREME COURT OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN"
2. **MSC Case Number**: (assigned by clerk — leave blank on initial filing)
3. **Case Caption**: Full party names in proper format
   ```
   ANDREW PIGORS,
        Plaintiff-Appellant,
   v
   [OPPOSING PARTY NAME],
        Defendant-Appellee.
   ```
4. **Lower Court Information**:
   - Court of Appeals Case No.: [COA number]
   - Trial Court: 14th Judicial Circuit Court, Muskegon County
   - Trial Court Case No.: [number]
   - Trial Judge: Hon. [Judge name]
5. **Document Title**: e.g., "APPLICATION FOR LEAVE TO APPEAL"
6. **Filing Party Information**:
   ```
   Andrew Pigors, In Propria Persona
   [Address]
   [Phone]
   [Email]
   ```

### Formatting
- All caps for court name
- Centered alignment for case caption
- 8.5 x 11 paper, 1-inch margins
- 12-point font (Times New Roman preferred)
- No color — black ink only

## TABLE OF CONTENTS / TABLE OF AUTHORITIES — MCR 7.212(C)

### Table of Contents (TOC)
Required for any brief exceeding 5 pages.

**Format:**
```
TABLE OF CONTENTS
                                                          Page
Statement of Questions Presented ......................... ii
Statement of Jurisdiction ................................ 1
Statement of Proceedings and Facts ....................... 3
Argument ................................................. 7
  I.  [First Issue] ..................................... 7
  II. [Second Issue] .................................... 14
Relief Requested ......................................... 22
Appendix ................................................. A-1
```

### Table of Authorities (TOA)
Required for any brief exceeding 5 pages.

**Format:**
```
TABLE OF AUTHORITIES
                                                          Page(s)
Cases:
  Mireles v Waco, 502 US 9 (1991) ...................... 12, 15
  Pulliam v Allen, 466 US 522 (1984) ................... 16

Constitutional Provisions:
  US Const Am XIV ....................................... 8, 9
  Const 1963 Art 6 §4 .................................. 3, 5, 7

Court Rules:
  MCR 7.302 ............................................. 1, 2
  MCR 7.305(B) .......................................... 4, 6

Statutes:
  42 USC §1983 .......................................... 15, 16
  MCL 691.1407 .......................................... 18
```

### Citation Rules (Michigan)
- Michigan cases: *Party v Party*, vol Mich page (year) — e.g., *Ross v Consumers Power*, 420 Mich 567 (1984)
- Michigan COA: *Party v Party*, vol Mich App page (year)
- US Supreme Court: *Party v Party*, vol US page (year)
- Federal: *Party v Party*, vol F3d/F4th page (Circuit year)
- MCR: MCR [number] — e.g., MCR 7.305(B)(2)
- Michigan Constitution: Const 1963 Art [#] §[#]
- US Constitution: US Const Am [#] or US Const Art [#] §[#]
- Statutes: MCL [number] or 42 USC §[number]
