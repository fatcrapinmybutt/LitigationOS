# Citation Formats — Michigan Litigation Reference

## Michigan Statute Citations (MCL)

### Standard Format
```
MCL § [chapter].[section]
MCL § [chapter].[section]([subsection])([paragraph])
```

## Michigan Court Rule Citations (MCR)

### Standard Format
```
MCR [chapter].[rule]
MCR [chapter].[rule]([subrule])
MCR [chapter].[rule]([subrule])([sub-subrule])
```

## Case Law Citation Format

### Michigan Supreme Court
```
[Party v Party], [vol] Mich [page], [pinpoint]; [vol] NW2d [page] ([year])
Example: Vodvarka v Grasmeyer, 259 Mich App 499, 509; 675 NW2d 847 (2003)
```

### Michigan Court of Appeals (Published)
```
[Party v Party], [vol] Mich App [page], [pinpoint]; [vol] NW2d [page] ([year])
Example: Brown v Loveman, 260 Mich App 576, 600; 680 NW2d 432 (2004)
```

### Michigan Court of Appeals (Unpublished)
```
[Party v Party], unpublished per curiam opinion of the Court of Appeals,
issued [date] (Docket No. [number])
NOTE: Must identify as unpublished per MCR 7.215(C)(1)
```

### Federal Courts (when applicable)
```
[Party v Party], [vol] F3d/F4th [page], [pinpoint] ([Circuit] [year])
[Party v Party], [vol] F Supp 3d [page], [pinpoint] ([District] [year])
```

## Record Citation Format (Appellate Briefs)

```
Transcript:         (Tr at [page])
                    (3/15/25 Tr at 45)     — date-specific transcript
Exhibit:            (Ex [number])
                    (Pl's Ex 12)           — plaintiff's exhibit
                    (Def's Ex 7)           — defendant's exhibit
Deposition:         ([Name] Dep at [page])
Register of Actions: (ROA Entry [date])
Court Order:         ([date] Order at [page])
```
