## Cross-Lane Opposing Counsel Coordination Risk

**Risk**: If Lane A, B, and C opposing parties discover related litigation, they
may coordinate defense strategies or share information.

**Indicators of Coordination**:
- Similar language appearing in separate defense filings
- Simultaneous motion filing across lanes
- Shared expert witnesses or consultants
- Joint discovery requests

**Mitigation**: File in sequence, not simultaneously. Monitor opposing filings
for cross-lane intelligence sharing.
