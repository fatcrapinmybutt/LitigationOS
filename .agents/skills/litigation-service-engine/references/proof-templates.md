# Proof of Service Templates and Process Server Instructions

> MC 12 form guidance, affidavit templates, and server instructions
> Context: Pigors v Watson | 14th Judicial Circuit, Muskegon County

---

## 1. MC 12 — PROOF OF SERVICE (Michigan Court Standard Form)

### Overview:
MC 12 is the standard Michigan court form for proving service of process. It must be completed by the person who served the documents — NOT the plaintiff.

### Field-by-Field Completion Guide:

```
PROOF OF SERVICE

Court:     Circuit Court for the County of Muskegon
Case No.:  [assigned case number]

PLAINTIFF: Andrew Pigors
v.
DEFENDANT: [Defendant Name]

I served a copy of the:
  [✓] Summons and Complaint
  [ ] Other: _______________

On: [Full legal name of person served]

At: [Complete address where service was made]
    [City, State, ZIP]

On: [Date of service: Month/Day/Year]
At: [Time of service: ___:___ AM/PM]

By:
  [ ] Personal service on the individual named above
  [ ] Leaving copies at the individual's usual place of abode 
      with [name], a member of the household of suitable age 
      and discretion
  [ ] Registered mail, return receipt requested 
      (attach signed return receipt)
  [ ] Certified mail, return receipt requested 
      (attach signed return receipt)
  [ ] Tacking on door and first-class mailing on [date]
  [ ] Other: _______________

I am not a party to this action. I am at least 18 years old.

Server's Name (print): _________________________
Server's Address: _____________________________
Server's Phone: _______________________________

Signature: ____________________________________
Date: _________________________________________
```

### Filing:
File the completed MC 12 with the Muskegon County Circuit Court Clerk.
Keep a copy for plaintiff's records.

## 2. AFFIDAVIT OF SERVICE (Alternative to MC 12)

When a more detailed proof is needed or the MC 12 doesn't capture the specifics:

```
STATE OF MICHIGAN
IN THE CIRCUIT COURT FOR THE COUNTY OF MUSKEGON
14TH JUDICIAL CIRCUIT

ANDREW PIGORS,
        Plaintiff,                    Case No. ____________

v.

[DEFENDANT NAME],
        Defendant.
________________________________________/

AFFIDAVIT OF SERVICE

STATE OF MICHIGAN  )
                   ) ss.
COUNTY OF [SERVER'S COUNTY] )

I, [Server's Full Name], being first duly sworn, depose and 
state as follows:

1. I am over 18 years of age and am not a party to this action.

2. On [date], at approximately [time], I served [Defendant's 
   Full Name] with copies of the Summons and Verified Complaint 
   (with attached exhibits) in the above-captioned matter.

3. Service was accomplished by [describe method]:
   [FOR PERSONAL SERVICE:]
   I personally delivered the documents to the Defendant at 
   [address]. I identified the Defendant by [describe: asked 
   name, recognized, physical description matches].
   
   [FOR SUBSTITUTE SERVICE:]
   I left copies of the documents at the Defendant's usual 
   place of abode at [address] with [Name of Person], who 
   identified [himself/herself] as [relationship to defendant] 
   and who appeared to be of suitable age and discretion.
   
   [FOR TACKING:]
   After [number] unsuccessful attempts at personal service on 
   [dates], I securely attached the documents to the front door 
   of Defendant's dwelling at [address] in a conspicuous manner. 
   I also mailed copies by first-class mail to the same address 
   on [date].

4. The documents served consisted of:
   a. Summons (issued [date])
   b. Verified Complaint ([number] pages)
   c. Exhibits A through [X]
   d. [Any additional documents]

5. [FOR CORPORATE/LLC SERVICE:]
   I served [Name of Person] in [his/her] capacity as 
   [registered agent / officer / manager / member] of 
   [Entity Name].

Further affiant sayeth not.

____________________________
[Server's Printed Name]

Subscribed and sworn to before me this ____ day of _________, 
20___, in [County], Michigan.

____________________________
Notary Public
[County], Michigan
My commission expires: ____________
```

## 3. AFFIDAVIT FOR SERVICE BY PUBLICATION

```
STATE OF MICHIGAN
IN THE CIRCUIT COURT FOR THE COUNTY OF MUSKEGON
14TH JUDICIAL CIRCUIT

ANDREW PIGORS,
        Plaintiff,                    Case No. ____________

v.

[DEFENDANT NAME],
        Defendant.
________________________________________/

AFFIDAVIT IN SUPPORT OF SERVICE BY PUBLICATION

STATE OF MICHIGAN  )
                   ) ss.
COUNTY OF MUSKEGON )

I, ANDREW PIGORS, being first duly sworn, depose and state:

1. I am the Plaintiff in the above-captioned action.

2. I have been unable to serve Defendant [Name] by any method 
   authorized under MCR 2.105, despite diligent efforts.

3. My efforts to locate and serve Defendant include:

   a. On [date], I [or process server] attempted personal 
      service at [address]. [Result: no answer / wrong address / 
      person stated defendant no longer lives there].

   b. On [date], I [or process server] attempted personal 
      service at [second address]. [Result].

   c. On [date], I [or process server] attempted personal 
      service at [third address]. [Result].

   d. On [date], I sent summons and complaint by certified mail, 
      return receipt requested, to [address]. The mail was 
      returned [unclaimed / refused / no such address].

   e. I searched the following databases/records:
      - Michigan Secretary of State records
      - LARA Corporate Entity Search
      - Muskegon County property tax records
      - White pages / public people search databases
      - Social media accounts ([list platforms searched])
      - [Any other sources]

   f. I contacted the following persons:
      - [Name, relationship, what they said about defendant's 
        whereabouts]
      - [Additional contacts]

4. Based on my diligent search, I believe Defendant [Name] 
   [cannot be found within Michigan / has an unknown current 
   address / is deliberately evading service].

5. Defendant's last known address was [address].

6. I respectfully request that this Court authorize service by 
   publication pursuant to MCR 2.106.

____________________________
ANDREW PIGORS, Pro Se

Subscribed and sworn to before me this ____ day of _________, 
20___, in Muskegon County, Michigan.

____________________________
Notary Public
Muskegon County, Michigan
My commission expires: ____________
```

## 4. PROCESS SERVER INSTRUCTIONS

When hiring a process server, provide:

### Written Instructions Packet:
```
PROCESS SERVER INSTRUCTIONS
Case: Pigors v Watson | Muskegon County Circuit Court

DEFENDANT TO BE SERVED:
  Name: [Full Legal Name]
  Known Addresses:
    - [Primary address]
    - [Secondary address]
  Known Workplaces:
    - [Employer name and address]
  Physical Description: [if known — age, height, build, hair]
  Vehicle: [if known — make, model, color, plate]
  Known Schedule: [if known — typically home evenings, works days]

DOCUMENTS TO SERVE:
  1. Summons (original — verify date is within 91 days)
  2. Verified Complaint (__ pages)
  3. Exhibits A through [X]
  Total pages: ___

SERVICE REQUIREMENTS:
  - Personal service preferred (MCR 2.105(A)(1))
  - If personal service fails after 3 attempts on different 
    days/times, contact me before attempting substitute service
  - DO NOT serve by tacking without my authorization
  - Keep detailed notes: date, time, who you spoke to, what 
    happened at each attempt

AFTER SERVICE:
  - Complete the MC 12 Proof of Service form (enclosed)
  - OR prepare an Affidavit of Service
  - Return completed proof to me within 48 hours
  - Include a log of all attempts (successful and unsuccessful)

CONTACT:
  Andrew Pigors
  [Phone]
  [Email]
```

## 5. RETURN OF SERVICE CHECKLIST

After receiving the proof of service back from the server:

- [ ] Correct defendant name (matches complaint exactly)
- [ ] Date of service is within the 91-day summons period
- [ ] Address of service is correct
- [ ] Method of service is properly described
- [ ] Server's name and signature are present
- [ ] Server confirms they are 18+ and not a party
- [ ] For substitute service: name and relationship of person served
- [ ] For certified mail: signed return receipt attached
- [ ] For tacking: date of mailing documented
- [ ] All served documents are listed (summons, complaint, exhibits)
- [ ] File with the Muskegon County Circuit Court Clerk
- [ ] Keep a copy for your records
- [ ] Note the defendant's answer deadline on your calendar
