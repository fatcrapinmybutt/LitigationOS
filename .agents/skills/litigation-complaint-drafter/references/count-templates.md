# Count Templates — Michigan Verified Complaint

> 20+ cause-of-action count templates, each with element-by-element allegations
> Jurisdiction: Michigan | Context: Pigors v Watson

---

## TEMPLATE 1: NEGLIGENCE

```
COUNT [N] — NEGLIGENCE
(Against Defendant [Name])

[¶]. Plaintiff incorporates by reference paragraphs 1 through [X] above.

[¶]. At all relevant times, Defendant [Name] owed Plaintiff a duty of care 
arising from [the landlord-tenant relationship / ownership and control of 
the premises / statutory obligation under MCL ___].

[¶]. Defendant [Name] breached that duty by [specific negligent acts or 
omissions: failing to maintain, failing to repair, failing to warn, 
creating dangerous condition].

[¶]. Defendant's breach was the direct and proximate cause of Plaintiff's 
injuries and damages.

[¶]. As a direct and proximate result, Plaintiff suffered damages including 
[medical expenses, lost wages, property damage, pain and suffering, 
emotional distress, loss of use and enjoyment], in an amount to be proven 
at trial but not less than $25,000.00.

WHEREFORE, Plaintiff requests judgment against Defendant on Count [N] for 
compensatory damages, costs, pre- and post-judgment interest, and such 
other relief as this Court deems just.
```

## TEMPLATE 2: FRAUD (Particularity Required — MCR 2.112(B)(1))

```
COUNT [N] — FRAUD
(Against Defendant [Name])

[¶]. Plaintiff incorporates by reference paragraphs 1 through [X] above.

[¶]. On or about [specific date], at [specific location], Defendant [Name] 
made the following material representation to Plaintiff: "[quote or 
describe the specific statement]."

[¶]. This representation was false. In truth, [state the actual facts 
contradicting the representation].

[¶]. At the time Defendant made this representation, Defendant knew it was 
false [OR made it recklessly without knowledge of its truth or falsity].

[¶]. Defendant made this representation with the intent that Plaintiff 
would rely upon it and [enter into the transaction / sign the lease / 
pay money / refrain from action].

[¶]. Plaintiff actually and justifiably relied upon Defendant's 
representation by [describe specific action taken in reliance].

[¶]. As a direct and proximate result of Plaintiff's reliance on 
Defendant's false representation, Plaintiff suffered damages including 
[specify: out-of-pocket losses, benefit of the bargain, consequential 
damages], in an amount to be proven at trial but not less than $25,000.00.

WHEREFORE, Plaintiff requests judgment against Defendant on Count [N] for 
compensatory damages measured by [out-of-pocket / benefit of the bargain], 
costs, pre- and post-judgment interest, and such other relief as this 
Court deems just.
```

## TEMPLATE 6: BREACH OF IMPLIED WARRANTY OF HABITABILITY (MCL 554.139)

```
COUNT [N] — BREACH OF IMPLIED WARRANTY OF HABITABILITY
(Against Defendant [Name])

[¶]. Plaintiff incorporates by reference paragraphs 1 through [X] above.

[¶]. At all relevant times, Defendant leased residential premises to 
Plaintiff at [address].

[¶]. Pursuant to MCL 554.139(1)(a), Defendant covenanted that the premises 
and all common areas were fit for the use intended by the parties.

[¶]. Pursuant to MCL 554.139(1)(b), Defendant covenanted to keep the 
premises in reasonable repair during the term of the lease.

[¶]. These covenants cannot be waived by any provision of the lease. 
MCL 554.139(2); Trentadue v Gorton, 479 Mich 378 (2007).

[¶]. Defendant breached these covenants by [failing to repair / 
maintaining the premises in a condition unfit for habitation / 
specifically: list defects].

[¶]. Defendant had [actual / constructive] notice of these conditions 
as alleged in paragraphs [X] through [Y].

[¶]. As a direct result, Plaintiff suffered damages including [rent 
overpayment, repair costs, diminished use and enjoyment, health effects, 
relocation costs], in an amount to be proven at trial.

WHEREFORE, [standard prayer including rent abatement and lease termination].
```

   a. [First specific violation with factual basis]
   b. [Second specific violation with factual basis]
   c. [Additional violations as applicable]

[¶]. Defendant's violations were knowing, as evidenced by [facts showing 
defendant knew the practice was deceptive — for treble damages].

[¶]. As a direct result of Defendant's unfair and deceptive practices,

$250.00, whichever is greater, pursuant to MCL 445.911(2)].

WHEREFORE, Plaintiff requests judgment against Defendant including:
a. Actual damages or $250.00 minimum per MCL 445.911(2);
b. Treble damages for knowing violations per MCL 445.911(2);
c. Reasonable attorney fees per MCL 445.911(2);
d. Costs; and
e. Such other relief as this Court deems just.
