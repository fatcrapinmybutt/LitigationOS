# Complaint Structure — Complete Anatomy Reference

> MCR 2.111, MCR 2.113, MCR 2.112 | Michigan Circuit Court
> Context: Pigors v Watson | 14th Judicial Circuit, Muskegon County

---

## 1. CAPTION — MCR 2.113(A)

### Required Elements:
- Court name: "STATE OF MICHIGAN / IN THE CIRCUIT COURT FOR THE COUNTY OF [COUNTY]"
- Circuit designation: "[Nth] JUDICIAL CIRCUIT"
- Plaintiff name(s) with designation "Plaintiff" or "Plaintiffs"
- "v." separator
- Defendant name(s) with designation "Defendant" or "Defendants"
- Case number line (blank for initial filing, filled after clerk assigns)
- Judge line (blank for initial filing or "Hon. [Name]" if assigned)
- Closing separator line ("________________________________________/")

### Caption Template:
```
STATE OF MICHIGAN
IN THE CIRCUIT COURT FOR THE COUNTY OF MUSKEGON
14TH JUDICIAL CIRCUIT

ANDREW PIGORS,
        Plaintiff,                    Case No. ____________

v.                                    Hon. _______________

[DEFENDANT FULL LEGAL NAME],
individually and as [capacity],
[DEFENDANT 2 FULL LEGAL NAME],
a Michigan [limited liability company/corporation],
        Defendants.
________________________________________/

VERIFIED COMPLAINT AND JURY DEMAND
```

### Document Title:
Below the caption, center the document title. Common titles:
- VERIFIED COMPLAINT
- VERIFIED COMPLAINT AND JURY DEMAND
- FIRST AMENDED VERIFIED COMPLAINT
- VERIFIED COMPLAINT FOR INJUNCTIVE AND DECLARATORY RELIEF

### Jury Demand:
MCR 2.508 — Jury demand must be filed at or before the time the case is set for trial. Best practice: include in the complaint caption and as a separate paragraph at the end.

## 2. JURISDICTION BASIS

### Subject Matter Jurisdiction:
Michigan circuit courts have original jurisdiction over all civil claims where the amount in controversy exceeds $25,000 (MCL 600.605).

**Template paragraph:**
```
1. This Court has subject matter jurisdiction over this action 
   pursuant to MCL 600.605, as the amount in controversy exceeds 
   $25,000.00, exclusive of interest and costs.
```

### Personal Jurisdiction — MCR 2.105:
Established through proper service under MCR 2.105. For Michigan residents, personal jurisdiction exists based on domicile. For out-of-state defendants, long-arm jurisdiction under MCL 600.701-745 requires minimum contacts.

**Template paragraph:**
```
2. This Court has personal jurisdiction over Defendant [Name] 
   because Defendant resides in [and/or conducts business in] 
   the State of Michigan. [OR: pursuant to Michigan's long-arm 
   statute, MCL 600.715, because Defendant transacted business 
   within this state giving rise to the claims alleged herein.]
```

### Venue — MCR 2.221-2.226:
Circuit court venue rules:
- **MCR 2.221(1)**: County where cause of action arose
- **MCR 2.221(2)**: County where defendant resides or has principal place of business
- **MCR 2.221(3)**: County where defendant conducts business (if cause of action arose there)

**Template paragraph:**
```
3. Venue is proper in Muskegon County pursuant to MCR 2.221 
   because the events giving rise to this action occurred in 
   Muskegon County, Michigan, and Defendant [resides/conducts 
   business] in Muskegon County.
```

## 3. PARTY IDENTIFICATION AND CAPACITY

### Individual Defendants:
```
4. Defendant [FULL NAME] is an adult individual who, at all 
   times relevant herein, resided at [address], Muskegon County, 
   Michigan. Defendant [NAME] is sued in [his/her] individual 
   capacity.
```

### Corporate Defendants:
```
5. Defendant [CORPORATION NAME] is a [state] corporation 
   authorized to do business in the State of Michigan, with its 
   principal place of business at [address]. Defendant may be 
   served through its registered agent, [Agent Name], at 
   [agent address].
```

### LLC Defendants:
```
6. Defendant [LLC NAME] is a [state] limited liability company 
   with its principal place of business at [address], Muskegon 
   County, Michigan. Defendant may be served through its 
   registered agent, [Agent Name], at [agent address].
```

### Government Defendants — MCR 2.105(G):
```
7. Defendant [GOVERNMENT ENTITY] is a [municipal corporation / 
   county / state agency] organized under the laws of the State 
   of Michigan. Service is made pursuant to MCR 2.105(G).
```

### Pro Se Plaintiff:
```
8. Plaintiff ANDREW PIGORS is an adult individual residing at 
   [address], Muskegon County, Michigan. Plaintiff brings this 
   action pro se.
```

## 4. GENERAL ALLEGATIONS / FACTUAL BACKGROUND

### Drafting Rules:
1. **Chronological order** — start with the earliest relevant event
2. **One fact per paragraph** — each paragraph alleges one discrete fact
3. **Date every event** — "On or about [date]..."
4. **Name every actor** — "Defendant Watson" not "they" or "the landlord"
5. **Reference evidence** — "(See Exhibit A, attached hereto)"
6. **No legal conclusions** — facts only; save characterization for the counts
7. **No argument** — do not explain why facts matter; that comes in the counts

### Template paragraphs:
```
9.  On or about [date], Plaintiff and Defendant [Name] entered 
    into a [lease agreement / contract / transaction] for [describe 
    subject matter]. (See Exhibit A.)

10. At all times relevant herein, Defendant [Name] was responsible 
    for [describe role/duty].

11. On or about [date], Defendant [Name] [specific action taken]. 
    (See Exhibit B.)

12. As a result of Defendant's [action], Plaintiff suffered 
    [specific harm — lost money, physical damage, emotional 
    distress, displacement].
```

## 5. INDIVIDUAL COUNTS — STRUCTURE

### Count Header:
```
COUNT [Roman Numeral] — [CAUSE OF ACTION IN CAPS]
(Against Defendant [Name])
```

### Incorporation Paragraph:
```
[Next ¶]. Plaintiff incorporates by reference paragraphs 1 
through [last general allegation paragraph] as though fully 
restated herein.
```

### Element Paragraphs:
Each element of the cause of action gets its own paragraph, connecting back to specific fact paragraphs:

```
[Next ¶]. [Element 1 stated]: Defendant [Name] owed Plaintiff a 
duty of care arising from [the landlord-tenant relationship / 
specific statutory duty / etc.], as alleged in paragraphs 
[X] through [Y] above.

[Next ¶]. [Element 2 stated]: Defendant [Name] breached that 
duty by [specific conduct], as alleged in paragraphs [X] 
through [Y] above.
```

### Damage Paragraph (per count):
```
[Next ¶]. As a direct and proximate result of Defendant's 
[conduct alleged in this count], Plaintiff has suffered damages 
including but not limited to [list specific damages for this 
count], in an amount to be proven at trial but not less than 
$[amount].
```

### Per-Count Prayer:
```
WHEREFORE, Plaintiff respectfully requests that this Court 
enter judgment against Defendant [Name] on Count [N] and award:
a. Compensatory damages in excess of $25,000.00;
b. [Statutory damages / treble damages / etc.];
c. [Attorney fees pursuant to MCL ___];
d. Pre-judgment and post-judgment interest;
e. Costs of this action; and
f. Such other relief as this Court deems just and equitable.
```

## 6. GENERAL PRAYER FOR RELIEF

After all counts, include a general prayer aggregating all requested relief:

```
GENERAL PRAYER FOR RELIEF

WHEREFORE, Plaintiff ANDREW PIGORS respectfully requests that 
this Court:

A. Enter judgment in Plaintiff's favor on all Counts;
B. Award compensatory damages in an amount to be determined at 
   trial, but not less than $[total];
C. Award treble damages on Count [N] pursuant to MCL 445.911(2);
D. Award double damages on Count [N] pursuant to MCL 554.613;
E. Award attorney fees on Counts [N, N] pursuant to [statutes];
F. Award pre-judgment interest pursuant to MCL 600.6013;
G. Award post-judgment interest pursuant to MCL 600.6013;
H. Award costs and disbursements of this action;
I. [Enter injunctive relief as described in Count N];
J. [Enter declaratory relief as described in Count N];
K. Award such other and further relief as this Court deems 
   just and equitable.
```

```
EXHIBIT LIST

Exhibit B — [Correspondence dated ___]
Exhibit C — [Photographs taken ___]
Exhibit D — [Building inspection report dated ___]
Exhibit E — [Receipts for ___]
```

### Exhibit Attachment Rules:
- Only attach documents specifically referenced in the body
- Tab each exhibit clearly
- Include cover sheet for each exhibit identifying it by letter
- If exhibit is voluminous, attach relevant excerpts with notation that full document is available
