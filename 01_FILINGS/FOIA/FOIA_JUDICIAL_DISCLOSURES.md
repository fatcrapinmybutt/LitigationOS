**RECORDS REQUESTED:**

### 1. Judicial Disclosure Forms

All financial disclosure forms, conflict-of-interest disclosures, and any other mandatory disclosure documents filed by **Hon. Jenny L. McNeill** during her tenure at the 14th Circuit Court, including but not limited to:
   - Annual financial disclosure statements;
   - Prior employment disclosures;
   - Any disclosures of former law partnerships or business associations (specifically including any disclosure of the former **Ladas, Hoopes & McNeill** law firm);
   - Disclosures of familial or personal relationships with other judges, magistrates, or court employees.

### 2. Recusal / Disqualification History

All records reflecting instances where **Judge McNeill** has:
   - Recused herself from any case (voluntarily or by motion);
   - Been the subject of a motion for disqualification under MCR 2.003;
   - Been disqualified from a case;
   - Disclosed a potential conflict of interest on the record.

**Date Range:** From the date of Judge McNeill's appointment/election to the present.

### 3. Case Assignment Records

All records related to the assignment of **Case No. 2024-001507-DC (Pigors v. Watson)** to Judge McNeill, including:
   - The case assignment order or notation;
   - Any random assignment records or blind draw documentation;
   - Any reassignment records;
   - Any communications between chambers regarding case assignment;
   - Chief Judge Kenneth Hoopes' involvement (if any) in the assignment process for this case.

### 4. Former Law Firm Records

Any records in the court's possession reflecting:
   - Judge McNeill's prior legal practice, including employment at or partnership in the firm of **Ladas, Hoopes & McNeill**;
   - Any bar application materials or judicial appointment materials that reference the Ladas, Hoopes & McNeill firm;
   - Any screening protocols implemented when Judge McNeill joined the bench to address conflicts arising from her former partnership with Chief Judge Kenneth Hoopes.

### 5. Personnel Records (Non-Exempt Portions)

The non-exempt portions of Judge McNeill's employment/judicial personnel records, including:
   - Dates of service;
   - Current and prior positions held;
   - Any disciplinary actions (non-exempt portions only);
   - Any formal complaints filed against Judge McNeill (to the extent they are public records).
