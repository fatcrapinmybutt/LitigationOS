## ORDER ON MOTION REGARDING PARENTAL ALIENATION AND CUSTODIAL INTERFERENCE

THIS MATTER having come before the Court on Plaintiff's Motion Regarding Parental Alienation and Custodial Interference, and the Court having reviewed the motion, supporting brief, and attachments, and being otherwise fully advised in the premises;

IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that:

1. Defendant is found to have engaged in parental alienation and custodial interference against Plaintiff with respect to the minor child, L.D.W.

2. Plaintiff's parenting time with L.D.W. is immediately restored in accordance with the parenting time schedule established by this Court.

3. Defendant shall not interfere with Plaintiff's parenting time with L.D.W., directly or indirectly, including but not limited to failing to produce L.D.W. at the designated exchange times and locations.

4. Defendant shall participate in co-parenting counseling with a qualified professional within 30 days of the date of this Order, and shall continue participation until discharged by the counselor.

5. Violations of this Order shall result in contempt proceedings, which may include fines, modification of custody, and/or incarceration.

IT IS SO ORDERED.
