Dear Executive Director:

I respectfully submit the enclosed Request for Investigation — Judicial Tenure Commission under Article 6, §30 of the Michigan Constitution of 1963.

## Summary

The enclosed Complaint documents **1,127 discrete judicial violations** committed by Judge McNeill in the referenced domestic relations and personal protection order cases. These violations include:

- **150 ex parte violations**, including an order rate of 44% entered without hearing or notice;
- **167 violations warranting disqualification** under MCR 2.003 for bias and prejudice;
- **161 instances of procedural misconduct**, including failure to comply with mandatory court rules;
- **126 violations of MCL 600.2950/2950a**, the personal protection order statutes;
- **Complete failure to apply the 12 mandatory best-interest factors** of MCL 722.23 in proceedings that deprived me of custody of my child;
- **Use of derogatory language** ("crazy," "liar") in official court orders;
- **Retaliatory sanctions of $250** imposed for the exercise of the constitutional right to file motions; and
- **Disregard of exculpatory evidence**, including a negative drug screen.

These violations are supported by an impeachment database of **15,171 items**, including 545 documented deviations from the Michigan Judicial Institute Benchbook and 381 instances of PPO weaponization.

## Urgency

I bring to the Commission's attention the urgent nature of this matter. **The misconduct documented herein is ongoing, and a minor child continues to suffer harm.** My child has been deprived of a relationship with a fit parent through proceedings that did not comply with the minimum requirements of Michigan law. Every day that passes without intervention compounds the harm to my child.

The pattern of escalating retaliation documented in the enclosed Statistical Analysis (Attachment C) demonstrates that the misconduct is not self-correcting. Judge McNeill's response to legal challenges has been to escalate adverse action, not to reconsider or correct course. Appellate remedies, while being pursued, cannot address the immediacy of the ongoing harm.

## Request

I respectfully request that the Commission:

1. **Open an investigation** into the conduct of Judge McNeill as documented in the enclosed Complaint and attachments;
2. **Assign an investigator** to review the court record in Cases 2024-001507-DC and 2023-5907-PP;
3. **Consider interim measures** to protect my child and my parental rights during the pendency of any investigation; and
4. **Proceed to Request for Investigation** under MCR 9.220 if the investigation substantiates the documented pattern of misconduct.
