### ☐ STEP 7: Notarization (30 minutes)

**Before Going to Notary:**
- ☐ Locate notary public (bank, UPS store, law office, mobile notary)
- ☐ Call to confirm hours and cost (usually $10-15)
- ☐ Bring valid government-issued photo ID (driver's license or state ID)
- ☐ Bring Verification page **UNSIGNED**
- ☐ Bring pen

**At Notary:**
1. ☐ Show ID to notary
2. ☐ Tell notary: "I need to verify this document under oath"
3. ☐ Notary will ask: "Do you swear/affirm that the statements in this document are true?"
4. ☐ Answer: "Yes" or "I do"
5. ☐ Sign Verification page in notary's presence
6. ☐ Notary completes acknowledgment section:
   - Fills in date
   - Signs name
   - Prints name and title
   - Fills in commission expiration
   - Affixes official notary seal (required in Michigan)
7. ☐ Review completed notarization for accuracy
8. ☐ Pay notary fee

**After Notarization:**
- ☐ Make copy of notarized Verification for your records
- ☐ Keep notarized original for filing

### ☐ STEP 15: Prepare for Investigation

**Anticipated JTC Actions:**
1. **Preliminary Review (30-60 days):**
   - JTC staff reviews complaint
   - Determines if allegations, if true, would constitute misconduct
   - May request additional information

2. **Investigation (60-168 days):**
   - JTC may contact you for interview
   - JTC will notify Judge McNeill and request her response
   - JTC may review court files
   - JTC may interview court personnel

3. **Resolution (by day 168):**
   - Dismissal (most common outcome)
   - Private admonishment
   - Formal charges filed with Michigan Supreme Court (rare, for serious cases)

**How to Prepare:**

**Be Ready for Interview:**
- ☐ Review complaint and exhibits to refresh memory
- ☐ Prepare to answer questions about allegations
- ☐ Be truthful and direct
- ☐ Don't exaggerate or embellish
- ☐ Admit if you don't know or don't remember
- ☐ Stay calm and professional
- ☐ Consider bringing attorney to interview if possible

**Maintain Records:**
- ☐ Keep complete copy of filing package
- ☐ Keep all JTC correspondence
- ☐ Keep proof of service
- ☐ Don't discard any relevant documents

**Keep Contact Info Current:**
- ☐ If you move, notify JTC immediately
- ☐ If phone/email changes, notify JTC
- ☐ Check mail and email daily
