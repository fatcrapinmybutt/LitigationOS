## 1. GOVERNING AUTHORITY

### Constitutional Foundation
**Michigan Constitution 1963, Article 6, § 30** — Establishes the Judicial Tenure Commission with authority to investigate and recommend discipline for judicial misconduct.

### MCR 9.200-9.252 — Full JTC Procedure

| Rule | Title | Key Provisions |
|------|-------|----------------|
| **MCR 9.200** | Construction | Preserve integrity of judicial system; enhance public confidence |
| **MCR 9.201** | Definitions | "Judge" includes any person serving by election, appointment, or assignment |
| **MCR 9.202** | Standards of Judicial Conduct | **(A)** Judge personally responsible for own behavior. **(B)** Subject to censure, suspension, retirement, or removal for: **misconduct in office, persistent failure to perform judicial duties, conduct clearly prejudicial to administration of justice** |
| **MCR 9.210** | Commission Organization | 9-member commission; appointment procedures |
| **MCR 9.211** | Powers & Review | Commission has all powers under Const art 6 § 30; may NOT function as appellate court |
| **MCR 9.220** | Preliminary Investigation | Request must be in writing, verified on oath; commission investigates unless "clearly unfounded or frivolous" |
| **MCR 9.221** | Evidence | Commission may take evidence; respondent has 21 days to comment |
| **MCR 9.222** | 28-Day Letter | Written notice to respondent before complaint; 28-day response period |
| **MCR 9.223** | Conclusion of Investigation | Options: dismiss, dismiss with caution, admonish, recommend private censure |
| **MCR 9.224** | Complaint | Issued upon sufficient evidence of misconduct; disciplinary counsel appointed |
| **MCR 9.225** | Interim Suspension | Commission may petition MSC for suspension pending adjudication |
| **MCR 9.230** | Pleadings | Complaint and answer only; service requirements |
| **MCR 9.231** | Appointment of Master | MSC appoints hearing master |
