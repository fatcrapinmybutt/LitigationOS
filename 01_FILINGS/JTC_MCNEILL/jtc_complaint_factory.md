# JTC Complaint Factory
## Components
- Allegation table (required): date, conduct, canon/rule category, evidence pins, pattern notes
- Chronology narrative from ChronoDB (pinned)
- Exhibit list with relevance and authentication notes
- Requested investigation scope

## Constraints
- Allegations are factual and pinned.
- If judicial conduct authority texts are missing, produce PINPOINT_MISSING in output.
