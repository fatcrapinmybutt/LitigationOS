# JUDICIAL TENURE COMMISSION — COMPLAINT STRUCTURE (DRAFT)

## Purpose
Build a **fact-and-record anchored** complaint that tracks each allegation to:
- a canon/rule,
- a record item (order, transcript, ROA, email),
- and a precise harm.

## Core Allegation Buckets (examples; keep only those supported by pinned record)
1) **Ex parte evidence exposure** (staff listened to recording before service; unequal access)
2) **Due process suppression** (indefinite parenting-time suspension without timely evidentiary hearing; barriers to presenting evidence)
3) **Bias / appearance of impropriety** (pattern + statements + disparate treatment)
4) **Procedural irregularities** (communications channel restrictions; clerk handling; off-record fact reliance)

## Evidence Spine (must attach)
- Signed/file-stamped orders
- ROA excerpts
- Transcripts
- Emails/letters from court staff (authenticated)

## Canon/Rule Mapping (to be filled with pinpoints)
- Michigan Code of Judicial Conduct (cite specific canons)
- Applicable court rules (MCR 2.003, MCR 3.207, evidence rules where applicable)
