## ORDER ON MOTION REGARDING CHILD WELFARE AND MEDICAL CARE

THIS MATTER having come before the Court on Plaintiff's Motion Regarding Child Welfare and Medical Care, and the Court having reviewed the motion, supporting brief, and attachments, and being otherwise fully advised in the premises;

IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that:

1. The Court orders that L.D.W. shall receive all necessary medical care, including but not limited to routine medical, dental, and mental health treatment.

2. Both parties shall cooperate in ensuring that L.D.W.'s medical needs are met promptly and completely, and shall share all medical information regarding L.D.W. with the other party.

3. Neither party shall make unilateral medical decisions regarding L.D.W. without providing reasonable advance notice to the other party, except in cases of genuine medical emergency.

4. Failure to comply with this Order shall constitute contempt of Court and may result in sanctions, modification of custody, or other relief as the Court deems appropriate.

IT IS SO ORDERED.
