## ORDER GRANTING MOTION TO DISQUALIFY JUDGE

THIS MATTER having come before the Court on Plaintiff's Motion to Disqualify Judge pursuant to MCR 2.003, and the Court having reviewed the motion, supporting brief, and attachments, and being otherwise fully advised in the premises;

IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that:

1. Hon. Jenny L. McNeill is hereby disqualified from presiding over this matter pursuant to MCR 2.003.

2. This case is reassigned to a new judge in accordance with the procedures of the 14th Judicial Circuit Court.

3. All orders entered during the period of disqualifying conflict are subject to review by the successor judge upon motion of either party.

4. The Clerk shall effectuate the reassignment and notify all parties of the successor judge.

IT IS SO ORDERED.
