## ORDER GRANTING MOTION TO TERMINATE PERSONAL PROTECTION ORDER

THIS MATTER having come before the Court on Respondent's Motion to Terminate Personal Protection Order, and the Court having reviewed the motion, supporting brief, and attachments, and being otherwise fully advised in the premises;

IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that:

1. The Personal Protection Order issued against Andrew James Pigors in Case No. 2023-5907-PP is hereby terminated.

2. The PPO is vacated in its entirety.

3. The Clerk shall update all records to reflect the termination of the PPO and shall notify the Michigan State Police and all relevant law enforcement agencies.

IT IS SO ORDERED.
