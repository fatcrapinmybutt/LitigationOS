## ORDER GRANTING EMERGENCY MOTION FOR TEMPORARY CUSTODY

THIS MATTER having come before the Court on Plaintiff's Emergency Motion for Temporary Custody, and the Court having reviewed the motion, supporting brief, and attachments, and being otherwise fully advised in the premises;

IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that:

1. Temporary emergency custody of the minor child L.D.W. is hereby granted to Plaintiff Andrew James Pigors pending further hearing.

2. Defendant Emily A. Watson shall immediately deliver L.D.W. to Plaintiff upon service of this Order.

3. A full evidentiary hearing on custody shall be scheduled within fourteen (14) days of the date of this Order.

4. This Order is effective immediately upon entry and shall remain in effect until further order of the Court.

IT IS SO ORDERED.
