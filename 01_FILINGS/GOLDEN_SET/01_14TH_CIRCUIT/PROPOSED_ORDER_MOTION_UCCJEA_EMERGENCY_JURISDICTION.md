## ORDER GRANTING MOTION FOR EMERGENCY JURISDICTION UNDER UCCJEA

THIS MATTER having come before the Court on Plaintiff's Motion for Emergency Jurisdiction Under the Uniform Child Custody Jurisdiction and Enforcement Act (UCCJEA), and the Court having reviewed the motion, supporting brief, and attachments, and being otherwise fully advised in the premises;

IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that:

1. This Court exercises emergency jurisdiction pursuant to MCL 722.1204 (UCCJEA) based upon the finding that the minor child L.D.W. is present in this State and has been subjected to or threatened with mistreatment or abuse, or that it is necessary in an emergency to protect L.D.W.

2. Temporary emergency custody of L.D.W. is hereby granted to Plaintiff Andrew James Pigors pending further proceedings.

3. This Order is effective immediately upon entry.

4. This temporary emergency order shall remain in effect until a court with jurisdiction under MCL 722.1201 or MCL 722.1203 enters an order, or until further order of this Court.

IT IS SO ORDERED.
