## ORDER GRANTING MOTION TO APPOINT GUARDIAN AD LITEM

THIS MATTER having come before the Court on Plaintiff's Motion to Appoint Guardian Ad Litem, and the Court having reviewed the motion, supporting brief, and attachments, and being otherwise fully advised in the premises;

IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that:

1. A Guardian Ad Litem is hereby appointed to represent the best interests of the minor child L.D.W. in this proceeding.

2. The Guardian Ad Litem shall have full access to all records, reports, and documents relevant to this case, including but not limited to school records, medical records, counseling records, and Friend of the Court files.

3. The Guardian Ad Litem shall conduct an independent investigation and file a written report with the Court.

4. The costs of the Guardian Ad Litem shall be shared equally between the parties, or as the Court may otherwise direct.

IT IS SO ORDERED.
