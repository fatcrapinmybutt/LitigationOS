## ORDER GRANTING EMERGENCY MOTION TO RESTORE PARENTING TIME

THIS MATTER having come before the Court on Plaintiff's Emergency Motion to Restore Parenting Time, and the Court having reviewed the motion, supporting brief, and attachments, and being otherwise fully advised in the premises;

IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that:

1. Plaintiff's parenting time with the minor child L.D.W. is immediately restored.

2. Defendant shall make L.D.W. available to Plaintiff per the existing parenting time schedule currently in effect in this case.

3. Any and all orders suspending Plaintiff's parenting time with L.D.W. are hereby vacated.

4. This Order is effective immediately upon entry.

IT IS SO ORDERED.
