## STRATEGIC OVERVIEW

The filing sequence is designed to:
1. **Protect the children first** — emergency custody/parenting time restoration
2. **Signal urgency to every level of the judiciary** — simultaneous multi-court pressure
3. **Preserve all rights and deadlines** — nothing waived, nothing missed
4. **Build momentum** — each filing reinforces the next
5. **Create a paper trail of systemic failure** — if lower courts don't act, upper courts see the pattern
