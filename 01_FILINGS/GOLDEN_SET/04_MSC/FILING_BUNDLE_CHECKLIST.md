## FILING INSTRUCTIONS

### Petition for Superintending Control — MCR 7.304 / 7.316:
1. **Number of copies:** Original + **8 copies** (9 total) if filing by mail/in person
2. If e-filing via MiFILE, only 1 electronic copy required
3. **Page limit:** No specific page limit, but conciseness is expected
4. **Paper size:** 8.5" × 11", printed on one side only
5. **Margins:** At least 1" on all sides
6. **Font:** 12-point or larger proportional font (e.g., Times New Roman)
7. **Binding:** Bound or stapled on left side
8. **Cover:** RED cover for Petitioner — MCR 7.312(A)
9. Filing fee: $375 or fee waiver (MC 20)
10. Certificate of service required for all parties

### When to File — MCR 7.305:
- Application for leave to appeal: may be filed at any time during pendency of the COA case or within 56 days of COA decision
- Superintending control (MCR 7.304/7.316): when lower court has failed to perform a duty or has exceeded jurisdiction, and no adequate remedy at law exists
- No strict deadline for superintending control petitions, but timeliness strengthens the petition

## POST-FILING NOTES

- MSC may request a response from opposing party (typically 28 days)
- MSC may request oral argument (rare for superintending control)
- MSC may act without oral argument
- Possible outcomes:
  - Grant petition (issue order of superintending control)
  - Deny petition
  - Hold in abeyance pending COA proceedings
  - Remand to lower court with instructions
- If denied, consider alternative relief (legislative, federal court)
