## IV. PRELIMINARY STATEMENT

This Formal Complaint is filed pursuant to Michigan Constitution of 1963, Article 6, § 30; MCL 600.1401 *et seq.*; and MCR 9.200–9.220, requesting that the Michigan Judicial Tenure Commission investigate, and upon finding cause, discipline the Honorable Jenny L. McNeill for a sustained pattern of judicial misconduct in the above-captioned cases.

The misconduct documented herein is not the product of isolated judicial error or the exercise of reasonable discretion. Rather, it reflects a **systematic pattern** of due process violations, ex parte communications, denial of fundamental parental rights, hostile treatment of a pro se litigant, abuse of contempt powers, and disregard of mandatory court rules — all of which have caused, and continue to cause, irreparable harm to a minor child (L.D.W., age 3) and his father.

This Complaint is supported by analysis of **457+ evidence files**, yielding **1,157 documented misconduct findings** through comprehensive review of the court record. The evidence establishes clear and convincing proof of violations of the Michigan Code of Judicial Conduct, the Michigan Court Rules, and binding constitutional protections.

Parenting time may be restricted only upon specific findings that it would endanger the child's health or safety. No such findings have been made. The blanket suspension of all contact since August 8, 2025, violates MCL 722.27a. (Allegation 2.)

## XIII-A. PREEMPTIVE RESPONSE TO ANTICIPATED DEFENSES
