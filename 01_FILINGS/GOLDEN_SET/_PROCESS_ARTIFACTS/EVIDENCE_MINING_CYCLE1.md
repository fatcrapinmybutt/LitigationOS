3. **Fourth Instance of Inadequate Service**: August 2025 was the FOURTH documented instance of Emily Watson providing inadequate service. Father objected on record each time. Court ignored pattern.

4. **52 Ex Parte Orders Documented**: Analysis of 1,435 PDFs revealed 52 separate ex parte orders — suggesting systematic pattern, not isolated incident.
