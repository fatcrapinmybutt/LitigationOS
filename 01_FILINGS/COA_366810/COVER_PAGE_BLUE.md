## ⮞ ORAL ARGUMENT IS REQUESTED ⮜

### (MCR 7.212(C)(1) — Oral Argument Designation)

---

This appeal presents questions concerning (1) whether ex parte orders suspending all parenting time may be entered without verified pleadings or specific findings required by MCR 3.207(B)(1), (2) whether a parent may be deprived of all contact with his child for 571+ days without due process, and (3) whether the complete absence of MCL 722.23 best interest findings before eliminating parenting time constitutes reversible error. Oral argument will materially assist this Court in resolving these issues.
