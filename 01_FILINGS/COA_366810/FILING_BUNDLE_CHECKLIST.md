## FILING INSTRUCTIONS — MCR 7.205(A) REQUIREMENTS

### Application for Leave to Appeal:
1. Must be filed within **21 days** of the order being appealed (or within 21 days of denial of a timely motion for rehearing/reconsideration) — MCR 7.205(A)
2. **Number of copies:** Original + 4 copies (5 total) if filing by mail/in person — MCR 7.205(A)
3. If e-filing via MiFILE, only 1 electronic copy required
4. **Page limits:** Application body shall not exceed **50 pages** (double-spaced) — MCR 7.212(B)
5. **Paper size:** 8.5" × 11", printed on one side only
6. **Margins:** At least 1" on all sides — MCR 7.212(A)
7. **Font:** 12-point or larger proportional font (e.g., Times New Roman) — MCR 7.212(A)
8. **Binding:** Bound on left side; no spiral or ring binding
9. **Color of cover:** Appellant/Applicant = **BLUE** cover — MCR 7.212(A)(1)

### Cover Page Format — MCR 7.212(A):
- Court of Appeals case number (or "Case No. ___" if not yet assigned)
- Lower court case number
- Title of document
- Name of lower court and judge
- Names of all parties and their designations (Plaintiff-Appellant, Defendant-Appellee, etc.)
- Name, address, bar number, phone, and email of filing party (or "In Propria Persona")

- [ ] **Cover Page** (BLUE) — per MCR 7.212(A) format
