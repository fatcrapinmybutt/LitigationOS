## MCR 7.212 Compliance Checklist:

### Brief Requirements (MCR 7.212(B)):
- [ ] Brief does not exceed 16,000 words (current: ~14,070)
- [ ] 12-point proportionally spaced serif font (Times New Roman)
- [ ] Double-spaced text, 1-inch margins
- [ ] Pages numbered consecutively at bottom center
- [ ] Table of contents included
- [ ] Index of authorities included
- [ ] Statement of jurisdiction included
- [ ] Statement of questions presented
- [ ] Statement of facts with record citations
- [ ] Argument section with standard of review for each issue
- [ ] Relief requested
- [ ] Signature block with address, phone, email

### Appendix Requirements (MCR 7.212(H)):
- [ ] Appendix bound separately from brief
- [ ] Appendix index as first page
- [ ] Orders appealed from included
- [ ] Register of actions included
- [ ] Transcript excerpts cited in brief included
- [ ] All documents cited in brief or reasonably necessary included
- [ ] Pages numbered consecutively

### Service Requirements (MCR 7.211(B)):
- [ ] Proof of service on all parties
- [ ] Service within 7 days of filing
- [ ] Service on opposing counsel AND/OR party
- [ ] Service on trial court clerk (via MiFILE or mail)
- [ ] Service on Friend of the Court

## Emergency Contingencies:

| Scenario | Action |
|---|---|
| **TrueFiling/MiFILE is down** | File by mail (postmark date counts). Call COA Clerk: (517) 373-0786 |
| **Need extension** | File Motion for Extension BEFORE deadline per MCR 7.212(A)(4). Must show good cause. |
| **Over word limit** | File Motion for Leave to File Oversized Brief per MCR 7.212(B). Show good cause. |
| **Transcripts unavailable** | File Statement in Lieu of Transcript per MCR 7.210(B)(3)(b) |
| **Cannot locate opposing counsel** | Serve Appellee directly by mail. Note diligent efforts in Proof of Service. |
