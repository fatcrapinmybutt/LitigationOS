##   EX PARTE ORDER SUSPENDING ALL PARENTING TIME (AUGUST 8, 2025)
## ======================================================================

**Date:** August 8, 2025
**Title:** Ex Parte Order — Suspension of All Parenting Time

On August 8, 2025, Judge McNeill entered FIVE (5) separate ex parte orders, suspending ALL of Father's parenting time. Father received no notice of the ex parte motion and no opportunity to be heard before the suspension took effect.

**Key facts:**
- Filed by Emily Watson on behalf of Mother
- No certification of efforts to give notice (MCR 3.207(B))
- No finding of "immediate and irreparable injury"
- No hearing within 14 days as required by MCR 3.207(B)(3)
- Belated evidentiary hearing held October 29, 2025 — 82 days later

**This order is the primary subject of the appeal.**

##   ORDER IMPOSING $250 FILING BOND REQUIREMENT (MAY 16, 2025)
## ======================================================================

**Date:** May 16, 2025
**Title:** Order Imposing Filing Bond Requirement

The Court imposed a $250 bond requirement on all future filings by Plaintiff. This order effectively restricts Plaintiff's access to the court in violation of *Boddie v Connecticut*, 401 US 371 (1971) (prohibiting financial barriers to court access in domestic relations proceedings).

**This order is challenged on appeal as an unconstitutional access-to-courts barrier.**
