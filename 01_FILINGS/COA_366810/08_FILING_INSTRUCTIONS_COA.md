### B. Format the Brief

MCR 7.212(B) format requirements:

- [ ] **Font**: 12-point proportionally spaced serif font (Times New Roman recommended)
- [ ] **Spacing**: Double-spaced (footnotes may be single-spaced)
- [ ] **Margins**: 1 inch on all sides
- [ ] **Page numbers**: Consecutively numbered at bottom center
- [ ] **Word limit**: 16,000 words maximum (or 50 pages if not counting words)
- [ ] **Paper size**: 8.5" x 11"

### C. Convert to PDF

- [ ] Export each document to PDF format
- [ ] Ensure PDFs are text-searchable (not scanned images)
- [ ] Combine brief + TOC + TOA into a single PDF for the brief
- [ ] Keep appendix tabs as separate PDFs or one combined PDF with bookmarks
