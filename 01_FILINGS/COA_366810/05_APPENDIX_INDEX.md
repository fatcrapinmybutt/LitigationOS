## REQUIRED APPENDIX MATERIALS — MCR 7.212(H)

MCR 7.212(H) requires the appendix to contain:

> (1) The decision, judgment, or order appealed from, including any written opinion or findings of fact;
> (2) Any other relevant order or ruling;
> (3) Any portion of the transcript specifically cited in the brief;
> (4) Any other document or part of the record cited in the brief or reasonably necessary for consideration of the issues raised.
