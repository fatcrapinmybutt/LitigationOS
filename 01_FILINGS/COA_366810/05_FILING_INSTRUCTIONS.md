## IV. FEE WAIVER APPLICATION (MC 20)

### A. Filing Fees

| Filing | Fee |
|--------|-----|
| Application for Leave to Appeal | $375.00 |
| Complaint for Superintending Control | $375.00 |

### B. Fee Waiver — Form MC 20

If unable to pay the filing fees, file **SCAO Form MC 20** ("Fee Waiver Request") with each filing.

**Form Location**: https://www.courts.michigan.gov/siteassets/forms/scao-approved/mc20.pdf

### C. MC 20 Requirements

The fee waiver application requires:

1. **Personal Information**: Name, address, phone number.
2. **Financial Information**:
   - Monthly income (all sources).
   - Monthly expenses.
   - Assets (bank accounts, property, vehicles).
   - Liabilities (debts, obligations).
3. **Public Assistance**: Check box if receiving any of:
   - [ ] Supplemental Security Income (SSI)
   - [ ] Temporary Assistance for Needy Families (TANF)
   - [ ] Food Assistance Program
   - [ ] Medicaid
   - [ ] Other (specify)
4. **Statement of Inability to Pay**: Explain why filing fees create undue hardship.
5. **Signature and Date**: Under penalty of perjury.

### D. Filing the Fee Waiver

- Submit MC 20 simultaneously with each filing.
- In TrueFiling, select "Fee Waiver" as the payment method.
- Upload the completed MC 20 as a separate PDF.
- The court will rule on the fee waiver; if denied, you will receive notice to pay within a specified period.
