## ORDER ON MOTION FOR DISCLOSURE OF EXCULPATORY EVIDENCE (BRADY)

THIS MATTER having come before the Court on Defendant's Motion for Disclosure of Exculpatory Evidence Pursuant to Brady v. Maryland, 373 U.S. 83 (1963), and the Court having reviewed the motion, supporting brief, and attachments, and being otherwise fully advised in the premises;

IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that:

1. The prosecution shall disclose all exculpatory and impeachment evidence in its possession, custody, or control, as required by Brady v. Maryland, 373 U.S. 83 (1963), and its progeny, including but not limited to Giglio v. United States, 405 U.S. 150 (1972), and Kyles v. Whitley, 514 U.S. 419 (1995).

2. Such disclosure shall be made to Defendant within fourteen (14) days of the date of this Order.

3. Failure to comply with this Order shall result in sanctions, up to and including dismissal of the charges with prejudice.

IT IS SO ORDERED.
