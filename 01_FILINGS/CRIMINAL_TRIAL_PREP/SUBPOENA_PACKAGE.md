**Documents / Items Requested:**
1. All body-worn camera footage from every officer present at the scene of the incident underlying the charges in Case No. 2025-25245676-SM;
2. All body-worn camera footage from any officer who interacted with the Defendant, Andrew James Pigors, on the date of the incident;

## SERVICE INSTRUCTIONS

1. **Request issuance from Court Clerk.** Present each subpoena to the 60th District Court Clerk for issuance. Pro se defendants have the right to request subpoenas from the court clerk. MCR 2.506(A).

2. **Service method.** Subpoenas must be served by:
   - Personal service (hand delivery to the custodian or an authorized agent); or
   - Registered or certified mail, return receipt requested, to the custodian's business address.
   See MCR 2.506(G)(1).

3. **Witness/mileage fees.** If subpoenaing a witness to testify (not just records), tender the statutory witness fee and mileage. MCL 600.2552.

4. **Proof of service.** After service, complete a proof of service / affidavit of service and file it with the court. Retain copies for trial.

5. **Follow up.** Contact each custodian 3-5 business days after service to confirm receipt and anticipated compliance timeline.
