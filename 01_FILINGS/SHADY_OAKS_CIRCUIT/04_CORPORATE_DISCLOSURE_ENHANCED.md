| **(2) Fraud or Wrong** | Dissolved entity used in court; CT Corp service failure; ledger fabrication; $6,085.99 in converted payments; EGLE violations ignored; asset stripping via parallel suit | **YES** |
| **(3) Unjust Loss/Injury** | Plaintiff lost home, all personal property, custody of child for 300+ total days, employment, stable housing. Rendered homeless sleeping in car | **YES** |

**Conclusion:** All three elements of veil piercing are established. The corporate form should be disregarded, and Alden Global Capital LLC should be held jointly and severally liable for all damages. *Foodland Distributors v. Al-Naimi*, 220 Mich App 453 (1996); *Green v. Ziegelman*, 282 Mich App 292 (2009).
