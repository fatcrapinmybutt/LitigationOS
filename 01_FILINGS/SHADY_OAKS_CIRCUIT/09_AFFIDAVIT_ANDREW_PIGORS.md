### Water/Sewer Meter Fraud — Physical Impossibility

19. I reviewed my Zego billing statements for water and sewer charges. I observed that the water gallons and sewer gallons were reported as identical every single month: April 2025 (4,245 gallons water = 4,245 gallons sewer), May 2025 (3,233 = 3,233), June 2025 (3,100 = 3,100). This pattern was consistent across every billing period I examined.

20. This is a physical impossibility. During the relevant billing period, I had installed a spigot connected to the main water line via PEX tubing. This spigot supplied a sprinkler that my son L.D.W. played with daily during my parenting time. The water from the sprinkler and outdoor hose use drained into the ground — it did not enter the sewer system. Therefore, my water consumption must always have exceeded my sewer discharge. Identical readings prove the metering was fabricated or fraudulently calculated.

21. I have photographs documenting the spigot, PEX tubing, and sprinkler setup. I also have photographs of water meter packages being delivered to the Shady Oaks office — evidence that park management self-installed these meters without any municipal oversight, calibration, or third-party verification.
