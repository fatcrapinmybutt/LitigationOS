### Service Requirements — Important Notes

> **MCR 2.105 — Process; Who May Serve:**
> - Initial service of a summons and complaint must comply with MCR 2.105.
> - Personal service is the preferred method.
> - Certified mail with return receipt is an authorized alternative.
> - A copy of the summons must be obtained from the Clerk of Court before service.
>
> **MCR 2.104 — Summons:**
> - The summons must be issued by the Clerk of Court.
> - It must contain the court name, case number, names of parties, and Defendant's response deadline (typically 21 days for personal service, 28 days for mail service).
>
> [ANDREW_REQUIRED: Before serving, obtain the summons from the Muskegon County Circuit Court Clerk's office. The Clerk will issue the summons after the complaint is filed. Filing fee: approximately $175 for circuit court civil actions — verify current fee schedule with the Clerk.]
