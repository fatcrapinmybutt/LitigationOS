## METHODOLOGY NOTE

This chronology was compiled from verified database records. Where dates could be confirmed from court docket entries (`docket_events` table), they are stated precisely. Where violations are documented in the `judicial_violations` table but lack specific dates (due to NULL `date_occurred` fields), they are listed in the "Ongoing / Undated" section. All violation counts are derived from SQL queries against the litigation database and are reproducible.

The complete database containing all 5,063 violations, 1,940 bias chronology entries, and supporting evidence is available for Commission review upon request.
