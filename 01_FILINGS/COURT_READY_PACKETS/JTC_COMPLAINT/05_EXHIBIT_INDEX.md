## NOTES ON EXHIBIT PREPARATION

1. **Bates Numbering.** Exhibits are Bates-stamped with the prefix PIGORS-JTC- followed by a four-digit sequential number starting at 0001. Where an exhibit spans multiple pages, the Bates range is noted (e.g., PIGORS-JTC-0020 through PIGORS-JTC-0024).

2. **Copies Only.** Per JTC submission requirements, all exhibits submitted are copies. Originals are retained by Complainant and available upon Commission request.

3. **Compiled Exhibits.** Exhibits marked "Compiled" represent analytical work product derived from the court record and maintained in a comprehensive litigation database. The underlying data is verifiable from the 14th Circuit Court record. All database-derived statistics are traceable to specific SQL queries.

4. **Transcript Availability.** All transcripts are available through the 14th Circuit Court Reporter's Office. If transcripts have not yet been ordered for specific hearings, Complainant will order them upon Commission request.

5. **[ANDREW_REQUIRED] Items.** Exhibits marked with [ANDREW_REQUIRED] need specific dates, tracking numbers, or other information confirmed from personal records or the court file before filing.

6. **Volume.** The full evidentiary record is extensive (5,063 documented violations, 92,246 indexed evidence items, 1,436 impeachment entries). The exhibits listed represent the most probative evidence for each category. Additional supporting documentation is available upon request.

7. **Source File Locations.** Exhibits referenced are maintained in:
   - `01_FILINGS\JTC_MCNEILL\` — Previously filed JTC materials and scanned documents
   - `01_FILINGS\BERRY_DISQUALIFICATION\` — Berry-McNeill conflict evidence
   - `02_TRIAL_14TH\PRODUCTION_OUTPUT\PDF\` — Production output including ex parte order scans
   - Court file — Available from 14th Circuit Court Clerk's Office, 990 Terrace St, Muskegon, MI 49442
