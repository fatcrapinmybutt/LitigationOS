## III. STATUTORY BASIS FOR SERVICE

**Michigan-registered LLCs (Homes of America):**
- MCR 2.105(H): Service on a corporation or LLC by serving its registered agent.
- MCR 2.105(H)(1): If registered agent cannot be found, serve any officer, director, or agent.

**Foreign LLCs doing business in Michigan (Shady Oaks Park, Cricklewood, Alden):**
- MCL 600.715: Personal jurisdiction over foreign corporations/LLCs transacting business in Michigan.
- MCL 600.721: Service on the Michigan Secretary of State as agent for foreign entities.
- MCR 2.105(I): Alternative service by court order if other methods fail.

**Dissolved entities:**
- MCL 450.4802(1): A dissolved LLC may be served as though it had not been dissolved.
