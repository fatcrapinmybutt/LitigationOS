## The 50/50 Shared Custody Period (438 Days)

5. On or about May 5, 2024, this Court entered a temporary order establishing a 50/50 shared custody arrangement for L.D.W.

6. That arrangement remained in effect for approximately 438 days, through July 17, 2025.

7. During those 438 days, I provided a stable, loving home for L.D.W. during my parenting time. I cared for L.D.W.'s daily needs, including feeding, bathing, clothing, and providing age-appropriate activities and developmental stimulation. I was actively and consistently involved in my son's life.

8. To my knowledge, no adjudicated finding of abuse, neglect, or harm to L.D.W. was entered against me during this period. The arrangement functioned without incident.

---

## The Ex Parte Orders

9. On July 17, 2025, the Court entered an ex parte order that overturned the existing 50/50 arrangement and awarded Defendant sole physical custody. I was not provided notice or an opportunity to be heard before this order was entered.

10. On August 8, 2025, the Court entered a second ex parte order that same day, suspending all of my parenting time with L.D.W. Again, I was not provided notice or an opportunity to be heard.

11. Since August 8, 2025, I have had zero contact with my son — no visits, no telephone calls, no video calls, no communication of any kind. This is now the longest period L.D.W. has been completely separated from me since his birth.

---

## Compliance with Court-Ordered Conditions

12. Following the suspension of my parenting time, I complied with all conditions required by the Court, including undergoing a mental health evaluation at HealthWest.

13. On September 5, 2025, I received the results of my HealthWest evaluation (Exhibit H0002). The evaluation was favorable and did not identify any contraindication to my parenting time with L.D.W. The evaluator found no basis for concern regarding my parenting capacity, mental health, or fitness.

14. I also voluntarily submitted to drug screening. The results were **NEGATIVE** for all controlled substances.

15. Despite having completed all court-ordered requirements and having obtained a favorable evaluation and clean drug screen, no hearing has been convened to consider these results. My parenting time has not been restored.

---

## The Separation and Its Impact

16. As of the date of this Affidavit, I have been completely denied all contact with L.D.W. for more than 230 consecutive days and more than 270 total days of separation.

17. L.D.W. is now approximately 3 years old. I was an active, daily presence in his life for 438 days. The abrupt termination of all contact has, on information and belief, caused confusion, distress, and harm to L.D.W.'s emotional well-being and attachment to me as his father.

18. I have continued to financially support L.D.W. throughout the separation, including making nine (9) insurance payments totaling approximately $855 to maintain his health insurance and child support payments totaling approximately $792.

19. I have attempted to maintain my connection with L.D.W. by sending birthday messages and other communications. All attempts at direct contact have been blocked by Defendant.

20. I have documented more than 80 specific days of lost parenting time since the suspension.
