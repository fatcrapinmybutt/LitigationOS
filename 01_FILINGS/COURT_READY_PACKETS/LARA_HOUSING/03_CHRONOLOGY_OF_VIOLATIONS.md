## RETALIATION PATTERN ANALYSIS

The following chart maps Complainant's protected activities against the Park's retaliatory responses, demonstrating the temporal pattern that gives rise to the presumption of retaliation under MCL 600.5720:

```
COMPLAINANT'S ACTIONS                    PARK'S RETALIATORY RESPONSES
═══════════════════                      ═══════════════════════════

Late 2023: Reports sewage ─────────►    Mar 2024: Coerced lease, 114% rent hike
                                                   (within ~3 months)

Aug 2024: Ledger shows credit ─────►    Jan 2025: Fabricated ledger, $2,241.82 swing
                                                   (within ~5 months)

Jan 2025: Challenges billing ──────►    Jan 2025: 2nd eviction filed (3 days later)

Mar 2025: Formal sewage complaint ─►    Mar-Jul 2025: Accelerated eviction → lockout
           + EGLE involvement                      (within ~4 months)

Mar 2025: EGLE inspects, confirms ─►    Jul 2025: Forced removal + property destruction
           violations                              (within ~4 months)
```

**Every significant protected activity was met with an escalating retaliatory response, typically within days to months.**
