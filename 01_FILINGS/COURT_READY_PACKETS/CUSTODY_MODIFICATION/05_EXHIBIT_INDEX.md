## EXHIBIT AUTHENTICATION STATEMENT

Plaintiff states the following regarding exhibit authentication:

1. **Self-authenticating exhibits** under MRE 902(1) (domestic public documents) include all court orders, docket sheets, and official court records (Exhibits D-1 through D-7). Plaintiff will seek certified copies bearing the court seal.

2. **Business records** under MRE 803(6) include HealthWest evaluations (A-1, A-2), AppClose logs (E-2), FOC records (F-1, F-4), insurance records (F-2), and employment verification (F-3). Foundation will be established through records custodian affidavit or testimony.

3. **Public records** under MRE 803(8) include all police reports (B-1 through B-8), CPS records (B-3), and jail records (E-4).

4. **Recordings** (C-1, C-2) are admissible under Michigan's one-party consent doctrine, MCL 750.539c, and *Sullivan v Gray*, 117 Mich App 476 (1982). Authentication will be established through Plaintiff's testimony as a participant in the recorded conversation pursuant to MRE 901(b)(1).

5. **Summaries** (E-1, E-5) are admissible under MRE 1006 as summaries of voluminous records, with underlying records available for inspection.
