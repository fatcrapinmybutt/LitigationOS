    At a session of said Court, held in the County of Muskegon,
State of Michigan, on _________________, 20___.

    PRESENT: Honorable ________________________________


    THE COURT, having considered Plaintiff's Motion to Compel
Discovery filed pursuant to MCR 2.313(A), the Affidavit in Support,
the Exhibit Index, and all accompanying filings, and having heard
argument of the parties (or Defendant having failed to appear despite
proper notice), and being otherwise fully advised in the premises;

    THE COURT FINDS:

    1. Plaintiff served Discovery Requests upon Defendant on or about
[DATE], including Interrogatories (MCR 2.309), Requests for
Production of Documents (MCR 2.310), and Requests for Admissions
(MCR 2.312).

    2. Defendant failed to serve any response to the Discovery
Requests within the twenty-eight (28) day period required by the
Michigan Court Rules.

    3. Plaintiff made good faith efforts to obtain the requested
discovery without court intervention, as required by MCR 2.313(B)(1),
including written communications to Defendant requesting voluntary
compliance.

    4. Defendant's failure to respond to the Discovery Requests is not
substantially justified.

    5. The discovery sought is relevant to the pending custody,
parenting time, and child support matters and is within the scope
permitted by MCR 2.302(B) and MCR 2.314.


    IT IS HEREBY ORDERED:


    1. DISCOVERY COMPELLED. Defendant, Emily A. Watson, shall provide
complete, verified responses to Plaintiff's Discovery Requests in
all ten (10) categories identified in Plaintiff's Motion, as follows:

        Category 1:  Communications regarding L.D.W., custody, and
                     parenting time (Watson family communications)

        Category 2:  CPS/DHHS records (all reports, investigations,
                     and determinations)

        Category 3:  Police reports (all reports filed by Defendant
                     or Watson family members regarding Plaintiff)

        Category 4:  Financial records (income, tax returns, bank
                     statements, employment records)

        Category 5:  Medical, dental, and educational records of
                     L.D.W.

        Category 6:  PPO records and evidence (all evidence,
                     drafts, and communications related to PPO
                     petition in Case No. 2023-5907-PP)

        Category 7:  Berry-McNeill relationship documentation
                     (all records showing relationship between
                     Ronald Berry and Cavan Berry)

        Category 8:  Phone records and call logs (communications
                     with court personnel, FOC, Berry family, and
                     law enforcement)

        Category 9:  Residence documentation and household members
                     (all persons residing at 2160 Garland Drive)

        Category 10: Prior counsel communications (documents
                     exchanged with Jennifer Barnes (P55406) to the
                     extent privilege has been waived)


    2. RESPONSE DEADLINE. Defendant shall serve complete written
responses to all Interrogatories and Requests for Admissions within
fourteen (14) days of the date of this Order.

    3. DOCUMENT PRODUCTION DEADLINE. Defendant shall produce all
responsive documents, in their original format or as true and correct
copies, within twenty-one (21) days of the date of this Order.

    4. PRIVILEGE LOG. To the extent Defendant asserts any privilege,
Defendant shall provide a privilege log compliant with MCR 2.302(B)(5)
identifying each withheld document with sufficient detail to permit
evaluation of the privilege claim, within twenty-one (21) days of the
date of this Order.

    5. REQUESTS FOR ADMISSIONS. Pursuant to MCR 2.312(B)(1),
Defendant's failure to timely respond to Plaintiff's Requests for
Admissions has resulted in those matters being deemed admitted.
Defendant may move to withdraw or amend admissions under
MCR 2.312(D), subject to the Court's discretion.

    6. SANCTIONS FOR NON-COMPLIANCE. Failure to comply with this
Order within the deadlines set forth herein may result in sanctions
under MCR 2.313(B)(2), including but not limited to:

        a. An order that designated facts be taken as established in
accordance with Plaintiff's claims (MCR 2.313(B)(2)(a));

        b. An order refusing to allow Defendant to support or oppose
designated claims or defenses (MCR 2.313(B)(2)(b));

        c. An order striking Defendant's pleadings or parts thereof
(MCR 2.313(B)(2)(c));

        d. An order rendering a default judgment against Defendant
(MCR 2.313(B)(2)(d));

        e. An order treating the failure as contempt of court
(MCR 2.313(B)(2)(e)).

    7. COSTS AND EXPENSES. Pursuant to MCR 2.313(A)(5)(a), Defendant
shall pay Plaintiff's reasonable costs and expenses incurred in
filing the Motion to Compel in the amount of $_________ / as
determined by the Court upon submission of an itemization by
Plaintiff.

    8. ONGOING OBLIGATION. Defendant's discovery obligations are
continuing. If Defendant obtains or becomes aware of additional
responsive information after her initial response, she shall
supplement her responses within a reasonable time as required by
MCR 2.302(E).

    9. NO STAY. This Order shall not be stayed pending appeal.
