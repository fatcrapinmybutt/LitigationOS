    18. Defendant's failure to respond to discovery has caused and

    19. As of the date of this Affidavit, I have been completely

    20. During this entire period, I have received no information
whatsoever about L.D.W.'s health, education, medical care, welfare,
or daily life. I do not know:

        a. Whether L.D.W. is healthy;
        b. Whether L.D.W. is attending school or daycare;
        c. Whether L.D.W. has received medical or dental care;
        d. Whether L.D.W. has been referred for therapy or counseling;
        e. Who is living in the household where L.D.W. resides;
        f. Whether L.D.W. is safe.

    21. MCL 722.27a(7) entitles me, as L.D.W.'s father, to access
information regarding my child's health, education, and welfare.
Defendant's refusal to provide this information — either voluntarily

parental rights and of L.D.W.'s right to a relationship with both
parents.
