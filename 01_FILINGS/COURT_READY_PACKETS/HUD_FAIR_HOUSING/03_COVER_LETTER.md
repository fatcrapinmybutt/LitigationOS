## Summary of Complaint

I am a single father who resided at Lot 17, Shady Oaks Mobile Home Park, with my minor child, L.D.W. (age three at the time of the primary events). I am the titled owner of the manufactured home located on Lot 17. Beginning in March 2024, the corporate operators of Shady Oaks Mobile Home Park subjected me to a systematic pattern of discrimination and retaliation that included:

1. **Coerced 114% rent increase** — from $325/month to $695/month (eventually $720/month) under threat of eviction, imposed after I reported habitability violations;

2. **Retaliatory eviction campaign** — three separate eviction actions filed by dissolved entities lacking legal capacity, based on a fabricated ledger with a $2,241.82 discrepancy and $6,085.99 in omitted payments;

3. **Refusal of government housing assistance** — Respondents admitted a policy of refusing government housing assistance payments, which disproportionately impacts families with children;

4. **Failure to address sewage crisis** — EGLE confirmed raw sewage discharge near our family's home (Violation Notice VN-017235, April 15, 2025) while Respondents accelerated eviction proceedings rather than remediating hazardous conditions;

5. **Forced removal with child present** — On July 17, 2025, Respondents' agents drilled locks, destroyed personal property, and posted a "FREE" sign on my titled manufactured home, all while my three-year-old child was present;

6. **Continuing interference** — Respondents have blocked three documented willing buyers from purchasing my home by making false statements, with the most recent occurrence on February 18, 2026.
