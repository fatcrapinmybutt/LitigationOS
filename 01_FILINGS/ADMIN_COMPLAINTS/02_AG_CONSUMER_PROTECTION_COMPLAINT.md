## III. STATUTORY BASIS

### Michigan Consumer Protection Act (MCL 445.903)

The following unfair, unconscionable, and deceptive practices are specifically prohibited:

| Section | Violation | Description |
|---------|-----------|-------------|
| **§ 903(1)(c)** | Misrepresentation of affiliation | Using dissolved entity name; switching entity names on leases |
| **§ 903(1)(e)** | Misrepresentation of characteristics | 3 irreconcilable ledger versions — false balance representations |
| **§ 903(1)(n)** | Causing confusion about source of service | Multiple entity names (Shady Oaks, Cricklewood, Partridge) for same operation |
| **§ 903(1)(s)** | Failure to provide agreed-upon services | Habitability failures — sewage, maintenance neglect |
| **§ 903(1)(bb)** | Unfair, unconscionable method | Coerced lease at 113% rent increase under duress |
| **§ 903(1)(cc)** | Failing to disclose material information | Omitting $6,085.99 in payments from ledger; hiding corporate dissolution |
| **§ 903(1)(y)** | Gross discrepancy | $6,085.99 actual balance vs. claimed delinquency |

- **Actual damages** or **$250 minimum** per violation (whichever is greater)
- **Attorney fees** and costs
- **Injunctive relief** — prohibit continued deceptive practices
- **Civil penalties** — AG enforcement: up to $25,000 per violation

## IV. FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS

### A. Billing Fraud Scheme
