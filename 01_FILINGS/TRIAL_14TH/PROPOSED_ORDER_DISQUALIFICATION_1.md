THE COURT, having reviewed Plaintiff's Motion for Disqualification of Judge McNeill pursuant to MCR 2.003(D), the supporting brief, affidavit(s), and exhibits, and the file and proceedings herein, and being otherwise fully advised in the premises;

THE COURT FINDS that Plaintiff has demonstrated that disqualification is warranted under MCR 2.003(C)(1), in that the judge's conduct and rulings demonstrate actual bias or prejudice against a party, or the judge's impartiality might reasonably be questioned;

**IT IS HEREBY ORDERED:**

1. Plaintiff's Motion for Disqualification is **GRANTED**.

2. Hon. Jenny L. McNeill is hereby **DISQUALIFIED** from further proceedings in this matter, Case No. 2024-001507-DC, and all consolidated or related matters.

3. This case shall be **REASSIGNED** to another judge of the 14th Circuit Court by blind draw, pursuant to MCR 2.003(D)(4) and local administrative order.

4. All orders entered by Judge McNeill after the date on which the disqualifying bias or prejudice became manifest shall be **REVIEWED** by the successor judge for compliance with law and the best interests of the minor child under MCL 722.23.

5. The Chief Judge of the 14th Circuit Court shall effectuate the reassignment within fourteen (14) days of the entry of this Order.

6. This Order is effective immediately upon entry.

**IT IS SO ORDERED.**
