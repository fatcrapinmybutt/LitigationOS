6. **For alternative service:** Attach the Court's order authorizing alternative service

## DEFAULT JUDGMENT TIMELINE

After valid service, if a defendant fails to answer within **28 days** (MCR 2.108):

1. Wait the full 28 days from date of service
2. Verify no answer or appearance has been filed (check with Clerk)
3. File **Request and Entry of Default** (SCAO form MC 13)
4. Attach the filed Proof of Service
5. After default is entered, file **Motion for Default Judgment** with supporting affidavit
   of damages (MCR 2.603)
