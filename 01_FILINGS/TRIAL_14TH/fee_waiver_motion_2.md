### I. LEGAL STANDARD

1. MCR 2.002 provides that the court shall grant leave to proceed without payment of fees and costs if the applicant is unable to pay those costs and the applicant's claims are not frivolous.

2. Under MCR 2.002(B), a person is considered unable to pay if receiving public assistance, or if the person's income and assets are insufficient to pay fees and costs without depriving the person or his dependents of food, shelter, or other necessities of life.
