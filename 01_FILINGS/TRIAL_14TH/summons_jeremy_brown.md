**1. Service of Process:** This Summons is issued pursuant to MCR 2.102 and MCR 2.105.

**2. Individual Defendant:** Service upon an individual shall be made by delivering a summons and a copy of the complaint to the defendant personally, or by leaving them at the defendant's usual place of abode with a person of suitable age and discretion then residing therein. MCR 2.105(A).
