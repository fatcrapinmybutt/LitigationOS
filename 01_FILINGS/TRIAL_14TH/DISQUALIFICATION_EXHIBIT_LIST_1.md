## OBJECTIONS TO EXHIBITS

Plaintiff anticipates possible objections and provides the following responses:

**Objection: "Statistics are irrelevant or unreliable"**  
**Response:** Statistical evidence is admissible to show patterns. *People v Hubbard*, 217 Mich App 459, 464 (1996). Methodology is sound and based on complete case review.

**Objection: "Comparative analysis inappropriate because situations not identical"**  
**Response:** MCR 2.003 does not require identical situations, only demonstration of bias through pattern and disparate treatment. *Fletcher*, 447 Mich at 876-877.

**Objection: "Verbatim order adoption is efficient, not biased"**  
**Response:** Occasional adoption of proposed orders is proper; systematic adoption (75%) without independent analysis demonstrates predetermined outcomes. *Wells*, 102 Mich App at 127.

**Objection: "Motion seeks to overturn prior rulings"**  
**Response:** Motion seeks only disqualification and reassignment under MCR 2.003(C)(1). Rights to challenge prior orders are preserved but not asserted in this motion.
