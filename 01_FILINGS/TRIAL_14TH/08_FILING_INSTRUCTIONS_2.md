## STEP 3: SERVICE OF PROCESS (MCR 2.105)
- Personal service on each defendant OR registered mail, return receipt
- Must serve within 91 days of filing (MCR 2.102)
- Proof of service (SCAO form MC 04) for each defendant

## STEP 4: POST-FILING
- [ ] File proof of service for each defendant (within 7 days)
- [ ] Defendants have 21 days to respond (MCR 2.108)
- [ ] If no response: Motion for Default (MCR 2.603)
- [ ] Scheduling conference: typically 91 days after filing
- [ ] Serve discovery with complaint or shortly after
