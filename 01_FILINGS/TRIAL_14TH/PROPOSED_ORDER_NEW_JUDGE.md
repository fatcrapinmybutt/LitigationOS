The Court having considered Plaintiff-Father's Motion for Disqualification of Judge filed pursuant to MCR 2.003(C)(1), the Brief in Support, the Affidavit of Andrew J. Pigors, and the exhibits filed therewith, and the Court being otherwise fully advised in the premises;

**IT IS HEREBY ORDERED:**

### 1. DISQUALIFICATION GRANTED

The Honorable Jenny L. McNeill is hereby **DISQUALIFIED** from presiding over any further proceedings in the above-captioned matter, Case No. 2024-001507-DC, pursuant to MCR 2.003(C)(1)(a) and (C)(1)(b).

### 2. REASSIGNMENT OF CASE

This matter is hereby **REASSIGNED** to:

☐ Another judge of the 14th Judicial Circuit Court, to be designated by the Chief Judge; or

☐ A visiting judge, to be assigned by the State Court Administrator pursuant to MCR 8.111.

The newly assigned judge shall be: Honorable __________________________________

### 3. TRANSFER OF PENDING MATTERS

All pending motions, petitions, and matters in this case, including but not limited to:

(a) All pending custody and parenting-time motions;
(b) All pending motions for contempt;
(c) All pending personal protection order matters related to this domestic relations case;
(d) All pending discovery and evidentiary matters;
(e) All scheduling matters;

are hereby **TRANSFERRED** to the newly assigned judge for adjudication.

### 4. PRESERVATION OF RECORD

The Clerk of the Court shall ensure that the complete case file, including all pleadings, motions, orders, transcripts, and exhibits, is transferred to the newly assigned judge.

### 5. STATUS CONFERENCE

A status conference shall be scheduled before the newly assigned judge within **21 days** of the date of this Order, at which the parties shall appear to address the current posture of the case and establish a schedule for resolution of all pending matters.

### 6. PRIOR ORDERS

All prior orders entered in this case shall remain in full force and effect unless and until modified by the newly assigned judge upon proper motion.

### 7. EFFECTIVE DATE

This Order is effective immediately upon entry.
