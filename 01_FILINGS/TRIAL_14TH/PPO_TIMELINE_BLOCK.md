### 5. "Good Cause" Requirement Analysis

| Factor | Analysis |
|--------|----------|
| **Elapsed Time Since PPO:** | ~2–3 years |
| **MCR 3.707(B)(1):** | Respondent may file a motion to modify or rescind a PPO at any time. |
| **Good Cause Required?** | **No.** Under MCL 600.2950(12), the standard is whether the PPO is "no longer needed" based on changed circumstances. There is no separate "good cause" threshold for filing a motion to modify/rescind. The movant must demonstrate changed circumstances (*Hayford v. Hayford*, 279 Mich App 324 (2008)). |
| **Time Factor:** | The substantial passage of time (~2–3 years) without incident actually *strengthens* the motion — it is evidence of changed circumstances, not a barrier. |
| **If PPO Was Previously Challenged:** | If a prior motion to modify/rescind was denied, the movant must show *new* changed circumstances since the prior denial. Confirm whether any prior motion was filed. |

### 6. Respondent's 14-Day Window Analysis

| Factor | Analysis |
|--------|----------|
| **MCR 3.707(A)(2):** | When a PPO is initially entered *ex parte*, the respondent has **14 days** from service of the PPO to file a motion to modify or rescind. |
| **14-Day Window Status:** | **EXPIRED.** The PPO was entered in 2023. The 14-day objection window under MCR 3.707(A)(2) has long passed. |
| **Effect of Expiration:** | The expiration of the 14-day window does NOT bar this motion. MCR 3.707(B)(1) separately provides that a respondent may file a motion to modify or rescind "at any time." The 14-day window is the initial expedited review; the motion under MCR 3.707(B) is the ongoing remedy. |
| **Procedural Path:** | This motion proceeds under **MCR 3.707(B)** (motion to modify/rescind at any time) and **MCL 600.2950(12)** (court shall modify/rescind upon finding PPO no longer needed). |
| **Burden of Proof:** | Movant bears the initial burden of showing changed circumstances. If met, Petitioner bears the burden of establishing (by preponderance) that the PPO remains necessary. *Hayford*, 279 Mich App at 326. |
