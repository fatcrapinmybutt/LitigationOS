## SUBPOENA PROCEDURES AND REQUIREMENTS

### MCR 2.305 Requirements (Non-Party Document Production)
1. Subpoena must be served at least 14 days before documents are due
2. Must include $25.00 witness fee and mileage if attendance required
3. Non-party may object in writing within 14 days of service
4. Court may modify or quash subpoena for good cause shown

### MCR 2.506 Requirements (General Subpoena)
1. Subpoena must be issued by the clerk of the court or an attorney of record
2. Pro se litigant may request issuance from the clerk
3. Service must be made personally (not by mail) per MCR 2.506(G)
4. Witness fees: $12.00/day plus $0.10/mile round trip (MCL 600.2552)

### HIPAA Compliance (Medical Records)
1. File Motion for Qualified Protective Order per 45 CFR 164.512(e)(1)(vi)
2. Include proposed protective order limiting use to litigation
3. Serve notice on patient/parent if seeking child's records
4. Alternative: Obtain written authorization from party/parent

### FERPA Compliance (Educational Records)
1. File Motion for Court Order per 20 USC 1232g(b)(2)(B)
2. Show legitimate educational interest or specific need in litigation
3. As parent, Plaintiff has independent right of access per 20 USC 1232g(a)(1)(A)
