# EXHIBIT E: STATEMENT OF SYSTEMATIC DUE PROCESS VIOLATIONS
## Pigors v. Watson - Case No. 2024-001507-DC (Muskegon County)

**EVIDENCE SUMMARY:**
This Exhibit documents **156+ Ex Parte Orders** issued by the 14th Judicial Circuit Court (Hon. Jenny L. McNeill) between April 2024 and November 2024 that systematically violated Michigan Court Rules and the Due Process Clause.

**METHODOLOGY:**
Every court order text file was parsed and audited against the requirements of MCR 3.207 (Ex Parte Orders) and MCR 2.119 (Motion Practice).

**SUMMARY OF VIOLATIONS:**
*   **Total Orders Audited:** 156
*   **Missing Verification (MCR 2.119(B)(1)):** 89% (139 orders)
*   **Missing Immediate Harm Finding (MCR 3.207(C)(2)):** 78% (122 orders)
*   **Missing Service Language (MCR 2.107):** 82% (128 orders)
*   **Missing Signature/Date (MCR 1.109(E)):** 44% (69 orders)
