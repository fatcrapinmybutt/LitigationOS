### IMPORTANT NOTICE

**1. Service of Process:** This Summons is issued pursuant to MCR 2.102 and MCR 2.105.

**2. Out-of-State Corporate Defendant:** Service upon a foreign limited liability company may be made by:
   - (a) Serving the registered agent designated in Michigan, if any; or
   - (b) Serving an officer, director, or managing agent of the company; or
   - (c) If no registered agent or officer can be found, by serving the Michigan Department of Licensing and Regulatory Affairs (LARA) pursuant to MCL 600.715 (Michigan long-arm jurisdiction). MCR 2.105(D), (I).

**3. Long-Arm Jurisdiction:** Personal jurisdiction over Alden Global Capital, LLC is asserted under MCL 600.715 based on: (a) transaction of business within Michigan; (b) ownership and control of entities doing business in Michigan; (c) commission of tortious acts having effect in Michigan.

**4. Time to Answer:** You have 21 days after service of this summons (28 days if served outside Michigan) to file a written answer with the court and serve a copy on the Plaintiff. MCR 2.108(A)(1)-(2).

**5. Default:** If you do not file an answer within the time stated, Plaintiff may ask the court to enter a default and default judgment against you. MCR 2.603.
