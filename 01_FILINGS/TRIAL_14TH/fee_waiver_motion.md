## 3. BASIS FOR REQUEST — FINANCIAL HARDSHIP

### A. Inability to Pay

I am unable to pay the filing fees associated with this complaint due to financial hardship. Specifically:

a. I am self-represented (*pro se*) because I am unable to afford legal counsel.

b. Payment of the filing fee would deprive me or my dependents of the basic necessities of life, including food, shelter, or other essential needs.

c. I am currently **homeless** as a direct result of the Defendants' actions alleged in the Complaint. The eviction from my manufactured home at Shady Oaks Mobile Home Park rendered me without stable housing, and I have been forced to sleep in my vehicle.

d. My homelessness has directly caused the suspension of my parenting time with my minor son Lincoln, now exceeding 571 days. The filing fees for this action represent an additional barrier to my ability to seek justice and potentially restore my housing stability and parental rights.
