## LEGAL SIGNIFICANCE

This escalation pattern is textbook **fabricated allegations for custody advantage**:

1. **Escalating severity** — each new allegation raises the stakes when the prior one fails to produce results.

2. **No independent corroboration** — every allegation rests solely on Emily's word, with no witnesses, no physical evidence, and no medical confirmation.

3. **Serial investigation + serial clearance** — law enforcement investigated repeatedly and cleared Father every time. This is not a case where allegations went uninvestigated.

4. **Timing correlates with custody milestones** — allegations cluster around custody filing dates and hearing schedules.

5. **Source credibility destroyed** — Under *Vodvarka v Grasmeyer*, 259 Mich App 499 (2003), where the sole evidentiary basis for a PPO is the petitioner's uncorroborated statements, and those statements have been repeatedly investigated and found unsupported, reasonable cause does not exist. A petitioner who files seven false allegations has zero credibility to support a PPO.
