## SERVICE METHOD NOTES

**Michigan Court Rules governing service:**
- **MCR 2.105(A)(1):** Personal service — delivering summons and complaint to defendant personally.
- **MCR 2.105(A)(2):** Substituted service — leaving at usual place of abode with person of suitable age and discretion.
- **MCR 2.105(D):** Corporate service — serving officer, resident agent, or authorized agent of LLC/corporation.
- **MCR 2.105(I):** Alternative service — if personal or registered service cannot be made, Plaintiff may move for alternative service (posting, publication, etc.).
- **MCR 2.108(A)(2):** Out-of-state defendants have 28 days (not 21) to answer after service.

**Recommended service methods:**
- **Corporate defendants (Shady Oaks Park, Shady Oaks MHP, HOA, Alden, Partridge):** Certified mail, return receipt requested, to registered agent. Keep green card as proof.
- **Attorney defendants (Brown, Cox):** Personal service at their law offices, or certified mail with return receipt.
- **Individual defendants (Brandel, Doe Manager):** Personal service by process server or sheriff.
