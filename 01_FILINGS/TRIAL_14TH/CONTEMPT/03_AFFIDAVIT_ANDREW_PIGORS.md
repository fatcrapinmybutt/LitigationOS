## III. SPECIFIC INSTANCES OF DENIED PARENTING TIME

### A. Extended Withholding — March 26 to May 2, 2024

7. On or about March 26, 2024, Defendant unilaterally removed L.D.W. from my care and refused to return the child for my scheduled parenting time.

8. This unilateral withholding continued for **37 consecutive days**, from March 26, 2024 through May 2, 2024.

9. During this period, no court order authorized the withholding of L.D.W. from me. Defendant acted on her own initiative in complete disregard of the existing parenting time order.

10. I made multiple attempts to contact Defendant to arrange parenting time during this period. Defendant refused to respond or cooperate.

### B. Easter 2025 Denial — April 13–15, 2025

11. On April 13, 2025, I sent a message to Defendant through the AppClose co-parenting communication platform asking whether she would allow me an extra day during the Easter holiday, which fell during my scheduled parenting weekend.

12. On April 14, 2025, at 10:13 AM, Defendant responded: **"No. I won't be giving you an extra day this week."**

13. On April 14, 2025, at 10:58 AM, I replied: **"I'll be there on Friday."**

14. On April 15, 2025, at 10:38 AM, Defendant responded: **"I will not be there on Friday per our custody order regarding Easter holiday."**

15. On April 15, 2025, at 11:12 AM, I asked: **"So you're okay with me not seeing my son for a month?"** Defendant did not respond.

16. These messages are documented through the AppClose platform, which records message timestamps and read receipts. A true copy of the AppClose communication record for April 10–30, 2025 is attached as **Exhibit A**.

### C. Total Contact Cutoff — August 8, 2025 to Present

17. Since on or about August 8, 2025, Defendant has imposed a **complete prohibition** on all contact between me and my son L.D.W.

18. This prohibition includes:
    - All in-person parenting time
    - All telephone calls
    - All video calls (FaceTime, Zoom, etc.)
    - All text-based communication with L.D.W.
    - All communication through third parties
    - Attendance at school events, medical appointments, or extracurricular activities

19. As of the date of this Affidavit, the total period of denied or severely restricted parenting time exceeds **571 days**.

20. During this period, I have had **zero** meaningful contact with my child.

### D. Withholding of Medical Information

21. On or about November 19, 2024, Defendant withheld information regarding L.D.W.'s medical appointments and vaccinations.

22. Defendant has refused to provide me with medical records, health status updates, or information about L.D.W.'s medical providers, in violation of my rights as a parent with joint legal custody.

23. Defendant has excluded me from all medical decision-making for L.D.W.
