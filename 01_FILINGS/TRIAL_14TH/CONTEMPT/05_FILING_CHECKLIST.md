## SERVICE ON DEFENDANT — MCR 2.107

### Required Service
After filing, you MUST serve Defendant with copies of all filed documents.

### Service Methods (MCR 3.208(B)(2)):
- [ ] **Personal service** (preferred for contempt) — by process server or any adult non-party
- [ ] **Ordinary mail** — first-class to Defendant's last known address
- [ ] **MiFILE electronic service** — if Defendant has a MiFILE account

### Service Timing (MCR 3.208(B)(4)):
- Personal service: Hearing must be at least **7 days** after service
- Mail service: Hearing must be at least **9 days** after mailing

## POST-FILING STEPS

### After Filing:
- [ ] Confirm the Court has received the filing (check MiFILE or call clerk)
- [ ] Obtain hearing date from the Court (request within 21 days)
- [ ] Complete service on Defendant
- [ ] Complete service on FOC
- [ ] File proof of service with the Court
- [ ] Calendar the hearing date
- [ ] Begin preparing for hearing (organize exhibits, outline testimony)

### Hearing Preparation:
- [ ] Prepare testimony outline covering all violation dates
- [ ] Organize exhibits in order (Exhibits A through H)
- [ ] Bring extra copies of all documents to hearing
- [ ] Prepare questions for cross-examination of Defendant
- [ ] Review MCL 552.644 burden-shifting framework:
  - **Your burden:** Show prima facie case of parenting time denial
  - **Defendant's burden:** Prove compliance or good cause by preponderance
  - **If she fails:** Court SHALL find violation — mandatory

### Key Legal Points for Hearing:
1. MCL 552.644(3) — **"SHALL"** find violation if Defendant fails burden
2. MCL 552.644(6) — **"MUST"** impose sanctions for bad faith
3. MCL 552.644(8) — **"MUST"** order costs for bad faith
4. MCL 552.644(2)(c) — Makeup parenting time authorized
5. MCL 552.645 — Compensatory parenting time framework
6. *Brausch v. Brausch*, 283 Mich App 339 (2009) — "SHALL" is mandatory
7. *Harvey v. Harvey*, 470 Mich 186 (2004) — Parenting time enforcement
8. *In re Contempt of Dougherty*, 429 Mich 81 (1987) — Elements of contempt
