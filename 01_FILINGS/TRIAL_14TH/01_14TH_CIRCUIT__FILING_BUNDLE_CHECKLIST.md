## FILING INSTRUCTIONS

1. All documents must comply with MCR 2.113 (form of papers)
2. Caption must include: case number, judge name, parties, attorney/pro se designation
3. All pages must be numbered; documents must be on 8.5" × 11" paper
4. Original + 2 copies for court + 1 copy per opposing party
5. Certificate of Service required on ALL filings (MCR 2.107)
6. Filing fees payable by cash, check, or money order to "Muskegon County Clerk"
7. If filing fee waiver needed: attach MC 20 (Fee Waiver Request) with each motion
8. Retain file-stamped copy for your records

## ⚠️ MANDATORY SCAO FORMS (Required with custody-related filings)

**The following forms are REQUIRED by the Friend of the Court and Muskegon County Clerk. Copies are located in `05_ANALYSIS/legal_output/04_FORMS/`.**

| Form | Name | Required For | Location |
|------|------|-------------|----------|
| **FOC 30** | Affidavit re: Residency (UCCJEA) | **EVERY custody filing** — establishes jurisdiction | `EMERGENCY_CUSTODY/foc30.pdf` |
| **FOC 30A** | UCCJEA Supplement | Complex multi-state/multi-party cases | `EMERGENCY_CUSTODY/foc30a.pdf` |
| **FOC 2** | Motion Cover Page | All motions filed in Family Division | `EMERGENCY_CUSTODY/foc2.pdf` |
| **FOC 39** | Case Information Sheet | Initial filings | `SUPPORTING_FORMS/foc39.pdf` |
| **FOC 88** | Affidavit/Declaration | Sworn statements supporting motions | `EMERGENCY_CUSTODY/foc88.pdf` |
| **FOC 89** | Proposed Parenting Time Schedule | Any parenting time modification | `EMERGENCY_CUSTODY/foc89.pdf` |
| **MC 229** | Motion/Affidavit for Discovery | Discovery enforcement | `DISCOVERY_MOTIONS/mc229.pdf` |
| **MC 20** | Fee Waiver Request | If requesting fee waiver | Obtain from Clerk |
| **PCM 201** | Protected Personal Identifying Info | All filings with PII | `SUPPORTING_FORMS/pcm201.pdf` |

**CRITICAL:** FOC 30 (UCCJEA Affidavit of Residency) must be filed with EVERY custody-related motion. This is a jurisdictional requirement — filing without it may result in rejection.
