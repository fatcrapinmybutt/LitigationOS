EMILY A. WATSON, Petitioner, Case No. 2023-5907-PP
v. Hon. ___________________
ANDREW J. PIGORS, Respondent.

**ORDER TERMINATING PERSONAL PROTECTION ORDER**

This matter having come before the Court on Respondent's Motion to Terminate Personal Protection Order, the Court having reviewed the motion, exhibits, and the record, and good cause appearing:

**THE COURT FINDS:**

1. The factual basis supporting the original PPO has been affirmatively refuted by multiple independent law enforcement investigations, each of which cleared Respondent.

2. Respondent poses no reasonable apprehension of violence to Petitioner under MCL 600.2950(1).

3. Circumstances no longer exist that require continuation of the PPO.

**IT IS HEREBY ORDERED:**

1. The Personal Protection Order in Case No. 2023-5907-PP is **TERMINATED**, effective immediately.

2. The Court Clerk shall file a Removal of Personal Protection Order from LEIN with the Norton Shores Police Department.

3. All pending Show Cause motions are **DISMISSED WITH PREJUDICE**.
