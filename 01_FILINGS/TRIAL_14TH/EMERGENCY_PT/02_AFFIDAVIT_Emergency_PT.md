    I, Andrew J. Pigors, being first duly sworn, depose and state
as follows:

    1. I am the Plaintiff/Father in the above-captioned matter.
I am over the age of eighteen (18) years and competent to testify
to the matters stated herein. I have personal knowledge of the
facts stated in this Affidavit.

    2. I am the biological father of the minor child, L.D.W.,
who is the subject of this action.

    3. I reside at 1423 W. Norton Ave., Norton Shores, Michigan
49441. I maintain stable housing suitable for the care of L.D.W.

                  PARENTING TIME DEPRIVATION

    4. On or about August 8, 2025, this Court entered an ex parte
order suspending my parenting time with L.D.W. I received no
advance notice of the motion and was given no opportunity to be
heard before the order was entered.

    5. Since August 8, 2025, I have been denied all meaningful
parenting time with my child — a period of approximately 571+ days
and continuing.

    6. The ex parte order was based in substantial part on a
recording made by Lori Watson (Defendant's mother). I believe this
recording was obtained in violation of MCL 750.539c, Michigan's
felony eavesdropping statute.

                    EXCULPATORY EVIDENCE

    7. I voluntarily submitted to drug screening. The results
were NEGATIVE for all controlled substances.

    8. I was evaluated by HealthWest. The initial evaluation
returned "ALL ZEROS" — finding no basis for concern regarding my
parenting capacity or mental health. After Judge McNeill sent a
communication to the evaluator, the finding was changed to a
"Rule Out" designation, which is not a finding of impairment or
danger.

    9. Law enforcement has conducted nine (9) separate
investigations based on allegations made by Defendant and/or her
family members. Every single investigation resulted in ZERO
findings against me.

    10. No criminal charges have been filed against me based on
any of the allegations in this case.

    11. No child welfare findings have been
substantiated against me.

              PATTERN OF INTERFERENCE AND ALIENATION

    12. Defendant has engaged in a systematic pattern of parenting
time interference and parental alienation, including but not
limited to:

        a. Refusing to make L.D.W. available for court-ordered
           parenting time exchanges on multiple occasions;

        b. Refusing to allow telephone and video contact between
           me and L.D.W.;

        c. On or about Easter weekend 2025, Defendant refused the
           scheduled parenting time exchange despite a court order
           requiring same. I documented this through the AppClose
           application and contacted police;

        d. Watson family members — specifically Tony Watson Sr.,
           Diana Watson, and Tony Watson Jr. — have repeatedly
           interfered with parenting time exchanges, intimidated
           me at exchange locations, and contributed to the
           alienation of L.D.W. from me. I have documented
           approximately 305 separate intimidation incidents;

        e. Defendant and her family have filed serial allegations
           against me — none of which have been substantiated —
           as a strategy to justify the ongoing denial of my
           parenting time;

        f. Defendant has made statements and taken actions designed
           to alienate L.D.W. from me and to undermine our
           parent-child relationship.

                      HARM TO THE CHILD

    13. I believe L.D.W. is suffering emotional harm from the
prolonged separation. Prior to the ex parte order, L.D.W. and I
had a close, loving, bonded relationship.

    14. L.D.W. has expressed a desire to spend time with me.

    15. Each day that passes without contact causes further,
potentially irreversible harm to my relationship with my child.
Research consistently demonstrates that children benefit from
meaningful relationships with both parents, and prolonged separation
causes measurable psychological harm.

                  INCARCERATION AND IMPACT

    16. I was incarcerated for 59+ days on civil contempt, which
further disrupted my employment, my stability, and my ability to
maintain contact with L.D.W.

                     COMPLIANCE AND EFFORTS

    17. I have made every effort to comply with court orders and
to demonstrate my fitness as a parent. I have cooperated with
evaluations, submitted to drug screening, and made myself available
for all proceedings.

    18. I have made good-faith efforts to resolve parenting time
disputes without court intervention, including communicating with
Defendant and her counsel, and utilizing the Friend of the Court.
These efforts have been unsuccessful.

                     EMERGENCY NECESSITY

    19. I believe emergency relief is necessary because: (a) the
harm is ongoing, (b) the harm compounds daily, (c) the harm will
become irreversible if not addressed immediately, and (d) all
objective evidence supports my fitness as a parent and the safety
of parenting time with me.

    20. I have made reasonable efforts to notify Defendant of this
emergency filing through [MiFILE / first-class mail / email].
