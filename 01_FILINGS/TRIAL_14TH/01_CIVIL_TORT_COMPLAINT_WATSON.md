## I. INTRODUCTION

1. This civil tort action arises from a coordinated, systematic campaign by the Defendants — members of the Watson family — to destroy Plaintiff Andrew J. Pigors's relationship with his minor child, to deprive him of his constitutionally protected parental rights, and to inflict severe emotional, psychological, financial, and reputational harm upon him through fraud, perjury, intimidation, abuse of legal process, defamation, and civil conspiracy.

2. The Watson family's campaign has resulted in over 571 days of enforced separation between Plaintiff and his child, achieved through weaponized Personal Protection Orders ("PPOs") obtained through fraud, perjured testimony in court proceedings, coordinated false reports to Child Protective Services ("CPS"), systematic intimidation, and abuse of the judicial process.

The evidence supporting this Complaint is derived from 5407 documented harmful acts committed by Watson family members, including 3,949 harms attributed to Emily Watson alone, 305 acts of intimidation by Albert Watson, 280 acts by Lori Watson, and 65 acts by Cody Watson, as documented through court records, transcripts, text messages, police reports, CPS records, and other authenticated evidence.
