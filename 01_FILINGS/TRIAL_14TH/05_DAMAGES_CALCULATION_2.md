## III. PUNITIVE DAMAGES

**Michigan Standard:** Punitive damages available where conduct is willful, wanton, malicious, or shows reckless disregard.

- Civil conspiracy: 2-3x compensatory
- IIED: Where conduct is particularly egregious
- Coordinated family scheme: Aggravating factor
