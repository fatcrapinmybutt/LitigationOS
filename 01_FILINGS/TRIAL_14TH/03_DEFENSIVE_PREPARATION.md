# PROBATE DEFENSIVE PREPARATION
## Emergency Response Framework -- Guardianship/Conservatorship Defense
## Pigors v. Shady Oaks MHC et al.

---

## PURPOSE

This document prepares defensive responses in the event any party attempts to invoke
probate court jurisdiction or file guardianship/conservatorship petitions against
Andrew J. Pigors or his minor child.

---

## I. IMMEDIATE RESPONSE -- IF GUARDIANSHIP PETITION FILED

### Step 1: Object Within 14 Days (MCL 700.5207)
- File written objection with Muskegon County Probate Court
- Cite MCL 700.5204 -- fit parent available
- Cite *In re Hatcher*, 443 Mich 426 (1993) -- parental rights presumption

### Step 2: Demonstrate Parental Fitness
- Present evidence of ongoing parental involvement
- Show housing instability was caused by Defendants' illegal conduct
- Cross-reference Fair Housing Act claims

### Step 3: Motion to Dismiss
- File motion to dismiss guardianship for lack of basis
- Argue: MCL 700.5204 requires showing BOTH parents unable/unfit
- Watson as custodial parent has no basis for third-party guardianship
