## REQUEST FOR EXPEDITED CONSIDERATION

Petitioner respectfully requests that the Court give this Petition **expedited consideration** due to the ongoing and irreparable nature of the constitutional injury. Specifically:

- **229 consecutive days** of complete parent-child separation since July 29, 2025, following the trial court's revocation of a 50/50 custody arrangement that had operated successfully for over fourteen months (438 days);
- During the current withholding period, Watson removed L.D.W. from the country entirely from October 20 through November 15, 2025 — with the trial court's permission — while simultaneously filing a show cause motion against Petitioner for an alleged verbal exchange, for which no evidence was presented;
- L.D.W. is a male child, born November 9, 2022, currently age 3 — these are critical developmental years;
- No court has found Petitioner to be an unfit parent;
- The separation is the result of the lower court's failure to enforce mandatory parenting time statutes (MCL 552.644) and the entry of ex parte orders without notice;
- Every additional day of delay causes irreparable developmental harm to L.D.W. that cannot be undone.
