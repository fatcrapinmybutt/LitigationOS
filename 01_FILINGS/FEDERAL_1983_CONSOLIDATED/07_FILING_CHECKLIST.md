## VIII. FILING PROCEDURE

### Option A: Paper Filing (recommended for IFP)

1. Print Complaint, Brief, Declaration, IFP Application, Civil Cover Sheet
2. Sign all documents by hand
3. Attach IFP supporting financial documents
4. Mail to WDMI Clerk via **certified mail, return receipt requested**:
   ```
   Clerk of Court
   United States District Court
   Western District of Michigan
   One Monroe Center
   110 Michigan Street NW
   Grand Rapids, MI 49503
   ```
5. Include self-addressed stamped envelope for file-stamped copies
6. Keep certified mail receipt as proof of filing

### Option B: Electronic Filing (if approved for pro se)

1. Convert all documents to PDF
2. Log into CM/ECF
3. File Complaint as new case
4. Attach Brief, Declaration, Exhibits as supporting documents
5. File IFP Application and Civil Cover Sheet
6. System generates summonses

### After Filing:

1. Calendar the 90-day service deadline (FRCP 4(m))
2. Wait for IFP screening decision
3. Upon IFP approval, deliver completed summonses and USM-285 forms to U.S. Marshal
4. Monitor docket for service returns
5. Calendar defendants' answer deadlines (21 days after service)
6. Prepare for possible Motion to Dismiss — have opposition brief framework ready
