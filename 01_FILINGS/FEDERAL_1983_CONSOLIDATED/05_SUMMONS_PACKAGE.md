# SUMMONS PACKAGE
# Pigors v. McNeill et al.
# United States District Court, Western District of Michigan

---

## INSTRUCTIONS

Each defendant requires a separate **AO 440 Summons** form and a separate **USM-285 Process Receipt and Return** form. For IFP litigants, service is performed by the United States Marshal pursuant to 28 U.S.C. § 1915(d) and Fed. R. Civ. P. 4(c)(3). The Clerk of Court issues the summonses upon filing of the Complaint.

**Steps:**
1. Submit this Complaint with the IFP Application
2. Upon IFP approval, the Clerk issues summonses
3. Complete USM-285 forms for each defendant
4. Deliver summonses + USM-285 forms + copies of Complaint to the U.S. Marshal
5. The U.S. Marshal serves each defendant and files proof of service
