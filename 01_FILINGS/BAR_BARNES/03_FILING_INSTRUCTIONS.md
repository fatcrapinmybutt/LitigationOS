## FILING METHOD
- Online: https://www.agcmi.com/filing-a-request-for-investigation/
- Mail: Submit Request for Investigation form
- No filing fee

## REQUIRED MATERIALS
- [ ] AGC Request for Investigation form (download from website)
- [ ] Narrative of attorney misconduct
- [ ] Supporting documentation (court orders, filings, correspondence)
- [ ] Copies of relevant court filings

## PROCESS
1. File Request for Investigation with AGC
2. AGC reviews and investigates
3. If probable cause found: Formal Complaint filed with Attorney Discipline Board
4. Hearing before Hearing Panel
5. Panel recommendation to full Board
6. Board decision (reprimand, suspension, disbarment)

## IMPORTANT NOTES
- Investigations are confidential until formal charges
- No fee required
- Complainant will receive status updates
- Average investigation: 6-12 months
- Consider filing after or simultaneously with appellate proceedings
