## ORDER ON MOTION REGARDING THREATS AND INTIMIDATION

THIS MATTER having come before the Court on Plaintiff's Motion Regarding Threats and Intimidation, and the Court having reviewed the motion, supporting brief, and attachments, and being otherwise fully advised in the premises;

IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that:

1. Defendant is ordered to immediately cease all threats and intimidation directed at Plaintiff, whether directly or through third parties.

2. Defendant shall have no contact with Plaintiff except through written communication (text message, email, or co-parenting application) solely regarding the care and welfare of L.D.W.

3. Violations of this Order shall result in contempt proceedings and potential referral to the Muskegon County Prosecuting Attorney for criminal investigation.

IT IS SO ORDERED.
