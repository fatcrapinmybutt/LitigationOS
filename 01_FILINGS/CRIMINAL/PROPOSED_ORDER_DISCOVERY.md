                ORDER GRANTING DEFENDANT'S MOTION
              FOR DISCOVERY AND INSPECTION (MCR 6.201)


    The Court, having reviewed Defendant's Motion for Discovery and

Inspection Under MCR 6.201, and being otherwise fully advised in the

premises;

    IT IS HEREBY ORDERED:

    1.  The People shall produce all discovery identified in Section

IV of Defendant's Motion within seven (7) days of the date of this

Order, including but not limited to:

        (a)  All body camera footage from the Muskegon County Jail
             on December 7, 2025, relating to the Defendant or the
             charged incident;

        (b)  All jail surveillance camera footage covering the area
             where the charged incident occurred;

        (c)  All incident reports, use-of-force reports, shift
             reports, and internal affairs reports related to the
             incident;

        (d)  All medical records for the Defendant generated on or
             after December 7, 2025;

        (e)  The names, badge numbers, and assignments of all
             corrections officers assigned to the Defendant's
             housing area on December 7, 2025;

        (f)  The names and booking numbers of the five (5) inmates
             identified as threatening the Defendant;

        (g)  All written or recorded witness statements;

        (h)  All exculpatory evidence as required by Brady v.
             Maryland, 373 U.S. 83 (1963).

    2.  The People shall file a certification with the Court within

seven (7) days confirming that a litigation hold has been issued for

all electronically stored information related to the December 7,

2025 incident.

    3.  The People shall identify all Brady material known to the

prosecution and any investigating agency.

    4.  Failure to comply with this Order may result in sanctions

under MCR 6.201(J), including preclusion of evidence, continuance,

or dismissal of the charges.


IT IS SO ORDERED.
