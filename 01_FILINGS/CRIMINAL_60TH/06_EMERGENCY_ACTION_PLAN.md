## THREE STRATEGIC OPTIONS

### Option 1: Force Campanelli to Act (Preferred)
- Send demand letter → file pro se motions as supplement → FOIA footage yourself
- **Pro:** Keeps appointed counsel; dual-track approach covers all bases
- **Con:** Relies on Campanelli eventually cooperating

### Option 2: Get New Attorney
- File motion to substitute → request continuance → get new public defender or private attorney
- **Pro:** Fresh representation; reset the relationship
- **Con:** Delay (continuance needed); new attorney needs time to prepare

### Option 3: Go Fully Pro Se
- Fire Campanelli → represent yourself → use all strategy documents prepared
- **Pro:** Full control; no reliance on unresponsive counsel
- **Con:** Higher risk; self-representation in criminal case is challenging

### **Recommendation: Execute Options 1 AND 2 Simultaneously**
Do everything yourself (FOIA, motions, subpoenas) while also demanding Campanelli act AND preparing the substitute counsel motion as backup. This covers every scenario.
