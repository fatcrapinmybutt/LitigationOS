## IMPORTANT NOTES

1. **MCR 2.003(D)(1)**: The motion must be filed "at the earliest practicable opportunity after the party discovers the grounds for disqualification."

2. **MCR 2.003(D)(2)**: After filing, Judge McNeill must either (a) grant the motion and recuse, or (b) refer the motion to the chief judge for determination.

3. **If denied**: Under MCR 2.003(D)(3), refer to the chief judge, who must decide within 14 days. If the chief judge is unavailable or is the subject judge, the SCAO assigns a judge.

4. **Appellate preservation**: If denied at all levels, the issue is preserved for appeal. Maintain a complete record.

5. **Stay of proceedings**: Consider filing a companion motion to stay all proceedings pending resolution of the disqualification motion.
