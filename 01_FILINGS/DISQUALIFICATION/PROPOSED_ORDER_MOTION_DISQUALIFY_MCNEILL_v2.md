## ORDER ON MOTION TO DISQUALIFY JUDGE

THIS MATTER having come before the Court on Plaintiff's Motion to Disqualify the Honorable Jenny L. McNeill Pursuant to MCR 2.003, and the Court having reviewed the motion, supporting brief, and attachments, and being otherwise fully advised in the premises;

IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that:

1. The Honorable Jenny L. McNeill is disqualified from presiding over Case No. 2024-001507-DC pursuant to MCR 2.003.

2. This case is reassigned to a new judge by the Chief Judge of the 14th Circuit Court in accordance with MCR 2.003(D).

3. All orders entered during the period of disqualifying conflict are subject to review by the successor judge upon motion of either party.

IT IS SO ORDERED.
