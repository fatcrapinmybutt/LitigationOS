### Leave to Amend (MCR 2.118)
- [ ] Check if leave of court is required to file amended complaint
- MCR 2.118(A)(1): A party may amend a pleading **once as a matter of course** before a responsive pleading is served
- MCR 2.118(A)(2): Otherwise, amendment requires leave of court or written consent of adverse party
- If Defendants have already answered the original complaint, **file a Motion for Leave to Amend** with the amended complaint attached as an exhibit
- Leave shall be freely given when justice requires (MCR 2.118(A)(2))

### Motion for Leave to Amend (if needed)
If leave is required, file:
1. **Motion for Leave to File Amended Complaint** — brief motion citing MCR 2.118(A)(2)
