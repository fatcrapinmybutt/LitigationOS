## ORDER ON EMERGENCY MOTION TO PROTECT PROPERTY

THIS MATTER having come before the Court on Plaintiff's Emergency Motion to Protect Property, and the Court having reviewed the motion, supporting brief, and attachments, and the Court finding that immediate and irreparable harm will result absent emergency relief, and being otherwise fully advised in the premises;

IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that:

1. Defendants, their agents, employees, and all persons acting in concert with them, are immediately restrained from entering, damaging, removing property from, or disposing of Plaintiff's mobile home located at Shady Oaks Mobile Home Community.

2. Defendants shall immediately restore all utilities to Plaintiff's mobile home, including but not limited to water, electricity, and sewage service.

3. Defendants shall not interfere with Plaintiff's quiet enjoyment of the premises, and shall take no action to evict, lock out, or constructively evict Plaintiff without lawful process.

4. A hearing on Plaintiff's request for a preliminary injunction is set for __________________ at __________ a.m./p.m. in this Court, at which time Defendants may appear and show cause why this Order should not be continued.

IT IS SO ORDERED.
