## IMPORTANT REMINDERS

1. **No filing fee.** The AGC does not charge a fee to file a grievance complaint.

2. **Be factual, not emotional.** The complaint should read like a report, not a letter of frustration. The AGC investigates facts, not feelings.

3. **The AGC is confidential.** AGC investigations are confidential unless formal charges are filed with the Attorney Discipline Board. Berry will eventually be notified of the complaint and given a chance to respond, but the investigation itself is not public.

4. **This does not replace your court case.** Filing an AGC complaint does not affect the pending custody or PPO cases. Continue pursuing your remedies in court independently.

5. **Retaliation is prohibited.** An attorney may not retaliate against a person for filing an AGC complaint. If Berry takes any retaliatory action, report it to the AGC immediately.

6. **Keep copies of everything.** Digital and paper. Store in LitigationOS under `01_FILINGS/AGC/` and keep a physical binder.
