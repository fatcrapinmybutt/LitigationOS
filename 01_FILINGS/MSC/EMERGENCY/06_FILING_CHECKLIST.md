- **CONVERT TO PDF:** These markdown files must be converted to properly formatted PDFs before filing. Use 12pt Times New Roman, double-spaced, 1" margins throughout.

- **NOTARIZE THE AFFIDAVIT:** The Affidavit (Document 03) must be signed in the presence of a notary public. Many banks, UPS stores, and FedEx offices offer notary services. [ANDREW_REQUIRED: Locate a convenient notary before filing day.]

- **SERVICE FIRST, THEN FILE:** Complete service on all parties BEFORE filing with the Court. The Proof of Service must reflect completed service, not anticipated service.

- **KEEP COPIES OF EVERYTHING:** Maintain a complete copy of all documents as filed, including any exhibits, for your records and for potential future proceedings.

- **MONITOR FOR RESPONSE:** After filing, monitor TrueFiling and your mail for any response from Watson or any orders from the Court. The proposed order gives Watson 14 days to respond.

- **DO NOT CONTACT THE COURT EX PARTE:** Once filed, do not contact the Court about the substance of the case outside of formal filings. You may contact the Clerk's office for procedural questions only.
