## ORDER GRANTING APPLICATION TO PROCEED IN FORMA PAUPERIS
### MCR 7.302(D)(2); MCR 2.002

---

At a session of the Michigan Supreme Court, held at Lansing, Michigan, on __________________, 2026.

**PRESENT:** [All Justices]

---

The Court having received and reviewed the Application of Petitioner Andrew J. Pigors to proceed *in forma pauperis* pursuant to MCR 7.302(D)(2) and MCR 2.002, together with the supporting Affidavit of Inability to Pay Costs and Brief in Support, and being otherwise fully advised in the premises;

The Court having determined that:

1. Petitioner has demonstrated, by sworn affidavit, that he is unable to pay the filing fees and costs associated with proceedings in this Court without being deprived of the necessities of life;

2. Petitioner's financial hardship is caused in substantial part by the proceedings in the lower court that are the subject of the Application for Leave to Appeal;

3. The Application for Leave to Appeal and/or Petition for Extraordinary Writs raises claims that are not frivolous and present issues of sufficient merit to warrant review;

4. The requirements of MCR 2.002 and MCR 7.302(D)(2) are satisfied;

NOW, THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED:

**1. GRANTED.** Petitioner Andrew J. Pigors' Application to proceed *in forma pauperis* is **GRANTED**.

**2. FEE WAIVER.** The filing fee of **$375.00** and all other fees and costs associated with Petitioner's Application for Leave to Appeal and/or Petition for Extraordinary Writs in this Court are hereby **WAIVED**.

**3. FILING ACCEPTED.** The Clerk of this Court is directed to **ACCEPT** Petitioner's Application for Leave to Appeal and/or Petition for Extraordinary Writs for filing without prepayment of fees or costs.

**4. FUTURE COSTS.** Petitioner is exempt from payment of fees and costs associated with this matter unless and until the Court determines, upon motion or its own initiative, that Petitioner's financial circumstances have materially improved such that he is able to pay some or all fees and costs.

**5. NOTIFICATION.** Petitioner shall promptly notify this Court in writing if his financial circumstances materially improve during the pendency of this matter.

**6. COPIES.** Petitioner is entitled to receive copies of court documents in this matter without charge.

---

IT IS SO ORDERED.
