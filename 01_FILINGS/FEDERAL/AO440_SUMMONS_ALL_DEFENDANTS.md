### SERVICE NOTE — JUDGE McNEILL

Per **FRCP 4(e)**, service on a state judicial officer sued in individual capacity may be accomplished by:

1. Personal delivery to the individual (FRCP 4(e)(2)(A));
2. Leaving copies at the individual's dwelling or usual place of abode with a person of suitable age and discretion (FRCP 4(e)(2)(B)); or
3. Delivering copies to an agent authorized by appointment or by law to receive service (FRCP 4(e)(2)(C)).

**Caution:** Service at the courthouse in a judicial officer's official capacity may not satisfy the requirements for individual-capacity service. Plaintiff should confirm the method of service with the Clerk.

### SERVICE NOTE — COUNTY OF MUSKEGON (MUNICIPAL ENTITY)

Per **FRCP 4(j)(2)**, service on a state, municipal, or other governmental organization subject to suit must be accomplished by:

> (A) delivering a copy of the summons and complaint to its chief executive officer; **or**
>
> (B) serving a copy of each in the manner prescribed by that state's law for serving a summons or like process on such a defendant.

Under **MCL 600.1925** and Michigan practice, service on a county is properly made upon the **County Clerk**. Accordingly, the summons is directed to the County Clerk's office at 990 Terrace Street, Muskegon, MI 49442.
