### 9. ADDITIONAL STATEMENT

I state further that because of my financial condition, I am unable to pay the costs of these proceedings and am unable to give security therefor. The nature of this action — seeking vindication of fundamental constitutional rights including the right to parent my child — requires access to the federal courts regardless of my financial circumstances. See *Troxel v Granville*, 530 US 57; 120 S Ct 2054 (2000); *Santosky v Kramer*, 455 US 745; 102 S Ct 1388 (1982). The claims asserted herein are non-frivolous and meritorious, as documented by 1,127 instances of constitutional violations resulting in 571+ days of forced parent-child separation.

Requiring prepayment of fees would effectively deny me access to the only forum capable of providing relief for federal constitutional violations — a result inconsistent with the purposes of 28 U.S.C. § 1915 and the Due Process Clause itself.
