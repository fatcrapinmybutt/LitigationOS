**Cite the U.S. Civil Statute under which you are filing:**

> **42 U.S.C. § 1983** — Deprivation of civil rights under color of state law
> **42 U.S.C. § 1985** — Conspiracy to deprive civil rights

**Brief description of cause:**

> State court judge and officials, acting under color of state law, violated Plaintiff's fundamental constitutional rights to substantive and procedural due process, equal protection, and freedom from First Amendment retaliation in custody proceedings — through a systematic pattern of 24 ex parte orders (44% of all orders), 571+ days of forced parent-child separation without evidentiary hearing, and coordinated conspiracy to deprive Plaintiff of the parent-child relationship.
