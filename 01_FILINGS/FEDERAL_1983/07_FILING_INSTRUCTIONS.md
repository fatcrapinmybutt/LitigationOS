# FILING INSTRUCTIONS
## Federal Complaint -- WDMI
## Pigors v. Shady Oaks MHC, LLC et al.

---

## Step-by-Step Filing Guide

### 1. Prepare Documents
- [X] Civil Complaint (signed)
- [X] Civil Cover Sheet (JS 44)
- [X] Verification under 28 USC 1746
- [X] Certificate of Service (FRCP 5)
- [X] Exhibit List
- [ ] Application to Proceed IFP (28 USC 1915) -- if applicable
- [ ] Summons for each defendant (Clerk will issue)

### 2. File with Court
- **Court:** U.S. District Court, Western District of Michigan
- **Address:** 110 Michigan Street NW, Suite 399, Grand Rapids, MI 49503
- **Filing Fee:** $405.00 (or submit IFP application)
- **CM/ECF:** https://ecf.miwd.uscourts.gov
- **Pro Se Filing:** May file in person at Clerk's office or by mail

### 3. Service of Process (FRCP 4)
- **Deadline:** 90 days from filing (FRCP 4(m))
- **Method:** Personal service or waiver of service (FRCP 4(d))
- **Corporate Defendants:** Serve registered agent per FRCP 4(h)
- **File proof of service** with Court after service is complete

### 4. After Filing
- Monitor docket for scheduling order
- Respond to any motions within FRCP deadlines
- Prepare for Rule 16 scheduling conference
- Complete initial disclosures per FRCP 26(a)(1) within 14 days of Rule 26(f) conference

| Action | Deadline | Rule |
