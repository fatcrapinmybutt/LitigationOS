# IX. CRITICAL REMINDERS

### ⚠️ Before Filing
- ☐ **Timing Decision**: Confirm whether state proceedings have concluded (Younger abstention risk if pending)
- ☐ **Complete ALL blanks** in Complaint and IFP Application — no placeholders
- ☐ **Verify all dates** against official court records
- ☐ **Verify all defendant names** are spelled correctly
- ☐ **Verify defendant addresses** are current
- ☐ **Attach all IFP supporting documents** (tax returns, bank statements, etc.)
- ☐ **Consider attorney consultation** — even limited-scope review recommended

### ⚠️ After Filing
- ☐ **Retain file-stamped copies** of everything
- ☐ **Note case number and judge** immediately
- ☐ **Calendar ALL deadlines** — missing a deadline can be fatal
- ☐ **Monitor IFP ruling** — respond promptly if additional information requested
- ☐ **Follow up with Marshals** within 30 days to verify service progress
- ☐ **Prepare for § 1915(e)(2) screening** — complaint designed to survive
- ☐ **Prepare for Motion to Dismiss** — review immunity matrix (02_IMMUNITY_DEFENSE_MATRIX.md)
