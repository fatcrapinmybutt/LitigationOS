**IMPORTANT SERVICE NOTES:**

1. **90-Day Deadline:** Pursuant to **Fed. R. Civ. P. 4(m)**, service must be completed within **90 days** after the complaint is filed. Failure to serve within 90 days may result in dismissal without prejudice.

2. **Waiver of Service:** Plaintiff may request waiver of formal service under **Fed. R. Civ. P. 4(d)**. If a defendant fails to waive service without good cause, the court must impose costs of service on that defendant.

3. **Filing Proof of Service:** Unless service is waived, proof of service must be filed with the court per **Fed. R. Civ. P. 4(l)**.
