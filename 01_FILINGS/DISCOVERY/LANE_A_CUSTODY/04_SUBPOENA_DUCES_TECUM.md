## LEGAL BASIS

These subpoenas are issued pursuant to MCR 2.305 (Subpoena for Production of Documents and Tangible Things from Non-Parties) and MCR 2.506 (Subpoenas). Each subpoena directs the custodian of records to produce the specified documents within fourteen (14) days of service or at a time otherwise agreed upon.

**Service Requirements (MCR 2.506(G)):** Each subpoena must be served personally (not by mail) and accompanied by the statutory witness fee of $12.00 per day plus $0.10 per mile round-trip (MCL 600.2552).

**HIPAA Compliance:** Medical and mental health records require a valid authorization or qualified protective order per 45 CFR 164.512(e). Plaintiff will file a Motion for Qualified Protective Order where necessary.

**FERPA Compliance:** Educational records require parental consent or a court order per 20 USC 1232g. As a parent, Plaintiff has independent right of access per 20 USC 1232g(a)(1)(A) and will seek a court order where necessary.
