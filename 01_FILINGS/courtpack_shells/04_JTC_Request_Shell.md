# JTC REQUEST FOR INVESTIGATION — Shell (Verified on Oath)
NOTE: Request must be in writing and verified on oath of the grievant (see MCR 9.220(A)).

## What to attach (record-first)
- The order(s) with quote-locked adverse statements
- Proof of the record-integrity defect (e.g., unfiled/unsigned/unsworn reliance; service gaps)
- Transcript excerpts where possible (or recorder certification / ROA minute entries)

## What NOT to do
- Do not argue merits like an appeal; present record facts + dates + documents + specific conduct.
