# SERVICE COMPUTATION RULES

## Deadline Calculation Reference

| Method of Service | MCR Authority | Additional Days | Effective Deadline Formula |
|-------------------|---------------|-----------------|---------------------------|
| **Personal Service** | MCR 2.105 | 0 days added | Response period = stated days |
| **Service by Mail** | MCR 2.107(C)(3) | **+3 days** per MCR 2.108(1) | Response period = stated days + 3 |
| **Service by Email** | MCR 2.107(C)(4) | 0 days added | Response period = stated days |
| **Service by Fax** | MCR 2.107(C)(4) | 0 days added | Response period = stated days |

## Common Response Periods

| Filing | Response Period | By Mail (add 3) | Authority |
|--------|----------------|------------------|-----------|
| Motion (general) | 14 days before hearing | 17 days before hearing | MCR 2.119(C)(1) |
| Response to motion | 7 days before hearing | 10 days before hearing | MCR 2.119(C)(2) |
| Reply brief | 4 days before hearing | 7 days before hearing | MCR 2.119(C)(2) |
| Answer to complaint | 21 days after service | 24 days after service | MCR 2.108(1) |
| Objection to ex parte order | 14 days after service | 17 days after service | MCR 3.207(C) |
| Discovery responses | 28 days after service | 31 days after service | MCR 2.309(B)(2) |

## Day-Count Rules (MCR 1.108)

- **Exclude** the day of the act or event from which the period runs
- **Include** the last day of the period
- If the last day falls on a **Saturday, Sunday, or legal holiday**, the period runs until the end of the next business day
- When the period is **less than 7 days**, exclude intermediate Saturdays, Sundays, and legal holidays
