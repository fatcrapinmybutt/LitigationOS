## CORE MICHIGAN STATUTES

### MCL 554.139 — Implied Warranty of Habitability
In every residential lease, the landlord covenants:
(a) Premises and common areas are **fit for use intended**
(b) Keep premises in **reasonable repair** during term
(c) Comply with applicable **health and safety laws**

Exception: Disrepair caused by tenant's willful/irresponsible conduct.

### MCL 600.5720 — Tenant Remedies
When landlord breaches habitability:
- Recover **damages**
- **Rent abatement** for uninhabitable period
- **Equitable relief** (injunction ordering repairs)
- **Rent withholding** after proper notice

### MCL 554.601a–.641 — Truth in Renting Act
- MCL 554.633: Lease provisions violating tenant rights are **void and unenforceable**
- MCL 554.636: Tenant may recover **actual damages or $250** (whichever greater)
  plus **reasonable attorney's fees**

### 42 USC § 3604 — Fair Housing Act (Federal)
Prohibits discrimination in housing based on race, color, religion, sex,
familial status, national origin, disability. Available if discrimination angle exists.
