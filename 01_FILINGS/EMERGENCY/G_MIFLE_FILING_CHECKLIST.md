## STEP-BY-STEP MiFILE SUBMISSION GUIDE

### Step 1: Access MiFILE

1. Open web browser
2. Navigate to: **https://mifile.courts.michigan.gov**
3. Log in with your MiFILE account credentials
   - If you do not have an account, click "Register" and create one
   - You will need a valid email address for account creation

### Step 2: Select Court and Case

1. From the dashboard, click **"File Into Existing Case"**
2. Select Court: **14th Circuit Court**
3. Select County: **Muskegon County**
4. Enter Case Number: **2024-001507-DC**
5. Click **"Search"** to locate the case
6. Verify the case caption: PIGORS v. WATSON
7. Click **"File Into This Case"**

### Step 3: Select Filing Type

1. Filing Category: **Motion**
2. Filing Type: **Emergency Motion** (or "Motion - Emergency" depending on menu)
3. If "Emergency Motion" is not available as a specific type, select:
   - Filing Type: **Motion**
   - In the description/comments field, clearly state: **"EMERGENCY MOTION -- IMMEDIATE RELIEF REQUESTED PURSUANT TO MCR 2.119(B)(7)"**

### Step 4: Upload Documents (In This Order)

Upload each document as a separate filing or as a combined PDF, depending on court preference. Recommended order:

| Order | Document | Filename |
|-------|----------|----------|
| 1 | Emergency Motion to Restore Parenting Time | A_EMERGENCY_MOTION_RESTORE_PARENTING_TIME.pdf |
| 2 | Verified Affidavit of Andrew J. Pigors | B_VERIFIED_AFFIDAVIT.pdf |
| 3 | Brief in Support of Emergency Motion | C_BRIEF_IN_SUPPORT.pdf |
| 4 | Proposed Order | D_PROPOSED_ORDER.pdf |
| 5 | Certificate of Service | E_CERTIFICATE_OF_SERVICE.pdf |

**IMPORTANT:** If filing as a single combined PDF, ensure documents are in the order listed above with clear page breaks between each document.

1. Indicate that service has been or will be completed
2. Method of service on Defendant:
   - [ ] Electronic service via MiFILE (if Defendant has a MiFILE account)

### Step 7: Payment / Fee Waiver

1. If the court requires a filing fee for motions:
   - Select **"Request Fee Waiver"** or **"Indigency Affidavit"**
   - Upload the MC 20 Fee Waiver Application as a supporting document
   - Note in the comments: "Fee Waiver (MC 20) filed contemporaneously"
2. If no fee is required for motions, proceed to submission
3. If the fee waiver option is not available in MiFILE:
   - File the MC 20 as a separate filing before or simultaneously with the motion
   - Contact the clerk's office at (231) 724-6241 to confirm fee waiver procedures

### Step 8: Review and Submit

1. Review all uploaded documents for correctness
2. Verify case number: **2024-001507-DC**
3. Verify court: **14th Circuit Court, Muskegon County**
4. Verify all documents are attached and in correct order
5. Click **"Submit"** or **"File"**
6. **SAVE** the confirmation number and filing receipt
7. Print or save a copy of the confirmation page for your records

### Step 9: Post-Filing Follow-Up

1. **Same day:** Confirm filing acceptance by checking MiFILE status
2. **Same day:** Complete service on Defendant (if not done via MiFILE)
3. **Same day:** Call the court clerk's office to confirm the filing was received and to request that the judge be notified of the emergency nature of the filing
   - **14th Circuit Court Clerk:** (231) 724-6241
   - State: "I have filed an emergency motion pursuant to MCR 2.119(B)(7) requesting immediate relief. The case number is 2024-001507-DC. I am requesting that this be brought to the judge's attention on an expedited basis."
4. **Next business day:** Check MiFILE for any court response or scheduling order
5. **Within 7 days:** If no response received, file a follow-up status inquiry with the clerk

## IMPORTANT REMINDERS

- **EMERGENCY FILING**: After submitting through MiFILE, CALL the clerk's office to alert them to the emergency nature of the filing. MiFILE does not automatically flag emergency filings for expedited judicial review.

- **SERVICE**: You MUST serve the Defendant independently -- MiFILE filing alone does not constitute service unless the Defendant has a MiFILE account and is registered as a party on the case.

- **FEE WAIVER**: If your fee waiver is denied, you will need to pay the filing fee to proceed. Keep funds available as a contingency.

- **PROPOSED ORDER**: The proposed order must be submitted as a separate Word document (.doc or .docx) in addition to the PDF version, so the judge can edit and sign it electronically. Check with the clerk for the preferred format.

- **NOTARIZATION**: The Verified Affidavit requires notarization. File it after obtaining the notary signature and seal. If unable to obtain notarization immediately, file the motion with a verification under penalty of perjury and follow up with the notarized affidavit as soon as possible.

- **KEEP COPIES**: Save copies of everything you file, including the MiFILE confirmation receipts, for your records.
