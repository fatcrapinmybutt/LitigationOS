## ORDER GRANTING DISQUALIFICATION OF JUDGE

THIS MATTER having come before the Court on Plaintiff's Motion for Disqualification of Judge pursuant to MCR 2.003, and the Court having reviewed the motion, supporting brief, and attachments, and being otherwise fully advised in the premises;

IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that:

1. The Honorable Jenny L. McNeill is hereby **disqualified** from presiding over this matter pursuant to MCR 2.003.

2. This case is hereby **reassigned** to a new judge by the Chief Judge of the 14th Circuit Court in accordance with MCR 2.003(D).

3. All prior orders entered in this matter shall remain in effect pending review by the successor judge, who shall have full authority to review, modify, or vacate any such orders as justice requires.

IT IS SO ORDERED.
