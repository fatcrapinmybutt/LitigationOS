## I. AUTHORITY

1. **MCL 600.1701(a)** — Courts have power to punish contempt for willful disobedience of court orders.

2. **MCL 722.27a(6)-(7)** — Provides specific remedies for parenting time violations including:
   - Compensatory parenting time (makeup time)
   - Modification of the parenting time order
   - Civil fine up to $100 per violation
   - Bond or security for future compliance
   - Attorney fees and costs
   - Finding of contempt

3. **MCR 3.208** — Enforcement of parenting time orders.

4. **MCR 3.606** — Contempt proceedings in domestic relations cases.
