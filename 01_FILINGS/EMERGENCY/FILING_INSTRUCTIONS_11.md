## WHAT TO INCLUDE IN YOUR FILING

### Required Information:
- [ ] Your full name, address, and contact information
- [ ] Name and address of the mobile home community
- [ ] Name of the community owner/licensee (if known)
- [ ] Name of the management company (if different from owner)
- [ ] Detailed description of the violation(s)
- [ ] Dates of the violation(s)
- [ ] Specific Michigan statute(s) violated
- [ ] What resolution you are seeking

### Recommended Attachments:
- [ ] **LARA_COMPLAINT.md** (or PDF version) — The formal complaint
- [ ] **VIOLATION_SCHEDULE.md** (or PDF version) — Detailed violation list
- [ ] **EVIDENCE_INDEX.md** (or PDF version) — Evidence inventory
- [ ] Copies of key exhibits (Exhibits A through J as available)
- [ ] Photographs of conditions (sewage, property damage)
- [ ] Copies of correspondence with management
- [ ] Copies of ledger records and payment receipts
- [ ] Copies of lease agreements
- [ ] Any court filings related to eviction proceedings

## TIMELINE AND FOLLOW-UP

1. **File the complaint immediately** — Do not delay
2. **Request written confirmation** of receipt from LARA
3. **Follow up in 30 days** if no response received
4. **Keep copies** of everything submitted
5. **Document any further violations** that occur after filing
6. **Note:** Filing this complaint does NOT replace or affect any pending court proceedings
7. **Retaliation protection:** MCL 125.2337 prohibits the community from retaliating against you for filing this complaint
