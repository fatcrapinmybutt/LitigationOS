## ORDER TO PRESERVE EVIDENCE

THIS MATTER having come before the Court on Plaintiff's Motion to Preserve Evidence, and the Court having reviewed the motion, supporting brief, and attachments, and being otherwise fully advised in the premises;

IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that:

1. All parties, their agents, and any persons acting in concert with them are hereby **ordered to preserve** all evidence relevant to the claims and defenses in this matter, including but not limited to:

   a. All electronic communications, including text messages, emails, social media messages, voicemails, and recordings;

   b. All documents, whether in paper or electronic form, relating to the minor child L.D.W., parenting time, custody, financial matters, and communications between the parties;

   c. All photographs, videos, audio recordings, and screenshots;

   d. All records from third parties, including school records, medical records, counseling records, and law enforcement records, currently in the possession of any party;

   e. All metadata associated with the foregoing documents and communications.

2. Any **spoliation of evidence** in violation of this Order shall result in an adverse inference instruction to the trier of fact, and may further result in sanctions, including but not limited to dismissal of claims, striking of defenses, default judgment, contempt of court, and/or monetary penalties.

IT IS SO ORDERED.
