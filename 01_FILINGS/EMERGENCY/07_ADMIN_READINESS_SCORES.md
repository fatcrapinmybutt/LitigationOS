### ⚠️ CRITICAL NOTE ON MDCR CLAIM

**Elliott-Larsen requires discrimination based on a protected class** (race, color, religion, national origin, sex, age, marital status, familial status, disability). The Fair Housing Act has broader protections, but MDCR requires identifying which protected characteristic was the basis for discrimination.

**Potential protected class arguments:**
- **Familial status**: Andrew is a parent with minor child — different treatment of families with children
- **Sex/gender**: If evidence shows male tenants treated differently
- **Other**: If evidence connects to any protected class

**If no protected class nexus can be established**, MDCR claim may not be viable. In that case, prioritize HUD and federal court filings.
