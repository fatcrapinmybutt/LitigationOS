## CASE SUMMARY

This action arises from persistent and severe habitability violations at Shady Oaks Mobile Home Park, located in Laketon Township, Muskegon County, Michigan. Plaintiff Andrew J. Pigors, a tenant residing at Lot 17, brings claims under Michigan's implied warranty of habitability (MCL 554.139), landlord-tenant remedies statute (MCL 600.5720), and related housing laws.

The litigation database documents **3,397 HOUSING_HARM entries** and **15 direct housing violations** attributable to Defendant's failure to maintain the premises in a condition fit for habitation. Housing instability resulting from these violations was subsequently weaponized against Plaintiff in related custody proceedings (Lane C convergence — cross-referenced in PKG-10/PKG-11).

**Case Strength Assessment:** Lane B = C grade (40/100). This package is designed to establish the factual and statutory record for escalation and consolidation with related claims.
