## Detailed Violation Narratives

### V-1: Fraudulent Ledger — Security Deposit Listed as Charge
**Date:** Approximately May 2024
**Statute:** MCL 125.2328, MCL 125.2328a
**Severity:** CRITICAL

Complainant's security deposit was converted from a credit to a charge on the account ledger, immediately creating a false appearance of arrears. This manipulated ledger was then used as the basis for the first eviction action. The community used the complainant's own deposit against him to initiate removal proceedings.

**Supporting Evidence:**
- Exhibit C: Full unredacted ledger showing security deposit as charge
- Exhibit C-1: Ledger/balance screenshots
- Email: "Gmail — SHADY OAKS CASSANDRA UNPROFESSIONAL FALSE LEDGER Andrew Pigors Current Ledger"
- Cashed check records showing payment of $1,300.26

---

### V-2: Failure to Update Ledger After $1,300.26 Payment
**Date:** May–July 2024
**Statute:** MCL 125.2328, MCL 125.2328a
**Severity:** CRITICAL

After being ordered to pay and making a payment of approximately $1,300.26 by check (which was cashed by Shady Oaks and/or an affiliated LLC), the ledger was never updated to reflect this payment. The uncorrected ledger showing false arrears was used during Complainant's custody proceedings on July 17, 2024, contributing to an adverse finding regarding financial stability.

**Collateral Harm:** The false ledger directly contributed to negative findings in Complainant's custody case.

---

### V-3 through V-5: Systematic Fee Stacking and Payment Manipulation
**Date:** Ongoing 2024–2025
**Statutes:** MCL 125.2328, MCL 125.2328a, MCL 125.2322

The community has systematically stacked unauthorized fees, refused to accept partial payments, refused MDHHS and TrueNorth housing assistance payments, and maintained a payment portal that prevents Complainant from making payments. The cumulative effect is a perpetually inflated balance designed to support continued eviction pressure.

---

### V-6 & V-7: Illegal Rent Increases
**Date:** March 2024; 2024–2025
**Statutes:** MCL 125.2328c, R 125.1921
**Severity:** CRITICAL

Rent has been increased from $395 to $695 to $720 — an 82% total increase — in rapid succession, coinciding with a change in management. The increases were imposed without proper written notice as required by the MHCA.

---

### V-9: Coerced Lease Execution
**Date:** March 26, 2024
**Statutes:** MCL 125.2328(2), MCL 125.2328(3)
**Severity:** CRITICAL

Complainant was forced under threat of eviction to sign a new lease at an off-site location with materially different terms and significantly higher rent. This lease was obtained through duress and is void under the MHCA.

---

### V-11: Active Sewage Leak / EGLE Violations
**Date:** Late 2024 through Mid-2025
**Statutes:** MCL 125.2333, MCL 324.3101 et seq.
**Severity:** CRITICAL

Raw sewage has been present near Complainant's lot for an extended period. EGLE issued violation notices. The community took approximately 31–34 days to respond to EGLE. Despite the health hazard, the community continued billing full rent and utility charges without any remediation credit.

---

### V-16 & V-17: Unlawful Set-Out and Property Destruction
**Date:** July 17, 2025
**Statutes:** MCL 125.2316(1), MCL 600.2918
**Severity:** CRITICAL

Locks were drilled, Complainant's home was cleared, personal property and appliances were removed or destroyed. Within 24 hours a "FREE" sign was placed and neighbors took items. Henry Brandel removed appliances and supplied the drill. No proper inventory or receipts were provided.

**Collateral Harm:** Complainant became homeless, slept in his car, and "unstable housing" was used in custody proceedings to suspend parenting time with his son for approximately two months.

---

### V-20 & V-21: Retaliatory Actions
**Date:** 2024–2025
**Statute:** MCL 125.2337
**Severity:** CRITICAL

The timeline of eviction filings, rent increases, and fee stacking is directly correlated with Complainant's exercise of his legal rights, including challenging ledger inaccuracies and filing complaints. This constitutes unlawful retaliation prohibited by MCL 125.2337.
