## IV. SPECIFIC VIOLATIONS

### A. Illegal Fees and Ledger Manipulation

**Violation: MCL 125.2312a, MCL 125.2321**

Respondents imposed unauthorized fees and manipulated Complainant's lot rent ledger:

1. Fabricated arrears entered on ledger with no corresponding services or charges authorized by lease
2. Unauthorized "administrative fees," "legal fees," and other charges not permitted by lease or statute
3. Misapplication of payments — valid payments credited to wrong periods or not credited at all
4. Refusal to accept government housing assistance (MDHHS, TrueNorth) while accepting identical payments from other tenants

**DB Evidence:** 549 FINANCIAL_HARM entries; Claims CL-0009, CL-0020, CL-0021

### B. Blocked Sales (Transfer Interference)

**Violation: MCL 125.2319**

MCL 125.2319 provides that a manufactured housing community shall not unreasonably withhold approval of a purchaser. Respondents blocked Complainant's sale on three (3) occasions:

1. **Sale Attempt #1**: Community manager told prospective buyer that Complainant "no longer owns his home"
2. **Sale Attempt #2**: Community manager told prospective buyer that Complainant had "abandoned" the home
3. **Sale Attempt #3**: Community manager represented that a "lawyer is working on taking/selling" the home; refused buyer application

Each constitutes unreasonable withholding of transfer approval under MCL 125.2319.

### C. Coerced Lease Modification

**Violation: MCL 125.2312a, MCL 125.2321, MCL 554.633**

On or about March 26, 2024, Respondents coerced Complainant into signing an off-site lease at different terms and higher rent:

- Lease signed under threat of eviction
- Signing occurred off-site (not at community office as customary)
- Terms materially different from prior lease
- Rent increased without proper notice per MCL 125.2321
- Coerced lease provisions are void under MCL 554.633

### D. Utility Billing Without Remediation

**Violation: MCL 125.2324, MCL 125.2325**

From late 2024 through mid-2025:
- Raw sewage present near Complainant's lot
- EGLE issued violations to community
- Respondents continued billing utilities without remediation credits
- Failed to remediate within reasonable time (≈31–34 day delays) [CL-0008]
- Failed to maintain common areas and utility systems as required

### E. Retaliatory Eviction

**Violation: MCL 125.2331, MCL 600.5720**

Respondents evicted Complainant in retaliation for:
- Reporting sewage violations to EGLE
- Challenging fabricated ledger charges
- Asserting rights under the Mobile Home Commission Act
- Attempting to sell his home

MCL 125.2331 prohibits eviction in retaliation for exercising rights under the Act.

### F. Property Destruction During Set-Out

**Violation: MCL 600.5744 (set-out procedures)**

During the July 17, 2025 eviction execution:
- Locks drilled without proper authority for non-officers to participate
- Personal property removed and destroyed rather than stored
- "FREE" sign placed on belongings within 24 hours
- Neighbor Brandel facilitated property removal/destruction
- No proper inventory or receipts provided [CL-0013]
- Writ execution exceeded scope — did not authorize distribution/destruction [CL-0013]

## VI. LARA JURISDICTION

LARA exercises jurisdiction through the **Bureau of Construction Codes — Manufactured Housing Division**:

- Licenses and regulates manufactured housing communities
- Investigates complaints from homeowners and tenants
- Enforces Mobile Home Commission Act
- Can revoke/suspend community licenses
- Can refer matters to Attorney General for enforcement
