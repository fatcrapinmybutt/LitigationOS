## Step-by-Step Filing Guide

### 1. Administrative Complaint to HealthWest ORR
- **File with:** HealthWest Office of Recipient Rights, 376 E. Apple Ave, Muskegon, MI 49442
- **Method:** Written complaint per MCL 330.1748
- **Timeline:** HealthWest ORR must investigate within 90 days (MCL 330.1748(3))
- **Documents:** Investigation Request + Verification + Exhibit List

### 2. MDHHS Complaint (if ORR fails to act)
- **File with:** MDHHS Recipient Rights Division, 333 S. Grand Avenue, Lansing, MI 48909
- **Authority:** MCL 330.1748a (appeal from ORR determination)
- **Method:** Written appeal with copy of original complaint and ORR response (or non-response)

### 3. HHS OCR Complaint (HIPAA violations)
- **File with:** HHS Office for Civil Rights -- Region V, 233 N. Michigan Ave, Suite 240, Chicago, IL 60601
- **Online:** https://ocrportal.hhs.gov/ocr/smartscreen/main.jsf
- **Deadline:** 180 days from the violation (45 CFR 160.306(b))
- **Documents:** HIPAA complaint form + supporting evidence

### 4. Service Requirements
- Serve all parties listed in Proof of Service
- Retain certified mail receipts
- Keep copies of all filed documents
