## NOTES FOR FILING

1. **Michigan Court Rule MCR 2.104(A)(1):** Service must be made by a person who is at least 18 years old and not a party to the action, unless the court permits otherwise or service is made by mail.

2. **Self-service by mail:** A party may serve documents (other than the initial Summons and Complaint) by mail under MCR 2.107. For initial service of the Summons and Complaint, it is recommended to use a professional process server or a qualified third party.

3. **Filing deadline:** This Proof of Service must be filed with the Court promptly after service is completed. Failure to file proof of service may delay the proceedings.

4. **Identifying the proper party for service:** Before service, confirm the identity and address of the owner/operator/registered agent of Shady Oaks Mobile Home Park through:
   - Michigan LARA Corporate Entity Search (https://cofs.lara.state.mi.us/SearchApi/Search/Search)
   - Muskegon County Register of Deeds
   - Property tax records
